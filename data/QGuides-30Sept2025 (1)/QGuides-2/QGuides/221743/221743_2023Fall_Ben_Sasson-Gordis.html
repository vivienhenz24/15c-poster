Comments
I HIGHLY recommend this course. Avishay is a fantastic professor, discussion leader, and has infinite knowledge on this topic. You should definitely take this class if you are already interested in how the civilian and military components of the government interact, but I would still recommend it to those who don't fall into this category. A great way to get your theory requirement for Gov out of the way without having to do intensive philosophical work. I learned a lot about the field of civil–military relations and our weekly seminar discussions were very rewarding. You must do the readings to get something out of the class.
Take the class and prepare to be challenged on a topic that is not often talked about. Doesn't matter if you're ROTC or from a military family or not––it's a really cool and important topic that Prof. does a great job of unpacking.
I can't recommend this class enough. As someone who hadn't heard of civil–military relations or taken a political theory class, I was a bit nervous going into this course, but it ended up being one of my favorite courses I have taken at Harvard. Professor Sasson–Gordis facilitated discussions that were engaging, inclusive, and no–stress. He was very kind, understanding, and accessible for help outside of class. The workload was manageable (the debates were not stressful!), and I enjoyed the readings. Definitely take this course, especially if you're looking for an awesome political theory option!
This class holds immense value not only for individuals with a military or defense–related professional background but also for citizens in democratic societies and students of political science. The professor's teaching style not only fosters dynamic discussions but also ensures a focused exploration of key concepts. I wholeheartedly recommend enrolling in this course, as it offers a rich and relevant experience that transcends specific professional backgrounds, making it beneficial for a broad range of learners.
Take it
Excellent course, I truly recommend it! The readings are engaging and Avishay is great at moderating debates. The assignment are thought–intensive and not menial work, which is lovely. And Avishay could teach anyone to write an amazing paper, if you just ask. Possibly one of the best government classes for people interested in security and the military.
