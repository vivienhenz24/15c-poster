Julie is an awesome professor who really cares about the subject she is teaching. She is flexible and gives great feedback. The course readings are interesting and you will learn a lot about the Global South. Additionally, you will gain a lot of valuable information about research design and effective reading. I genuinely learned a lot from and enjoyed this course.
Take this class! Dont worry if you don't know too much about the Global South, this class is a great introduction. It's also a very well organized gov 94, where each assignment builds into the next. A lot of gov 94s are a bit messier, so it's a rare gem in that regard. Julie is great at managing discussion and discourse.
Take! This! Class! I'm almost done with my gov requirements and this was the best gov class I've taken so far! Julie is so nice and cares about student growth, and the discussion was super active and open, you learn so much although there wasn't much lecturing. You learn so much material about governance around the world and about research design. The final paper length may seem a bit long but its not a hard limit, and with how interesting the material is and how much you learn about research design, it really isn't bad. If you are interested in global democracy, you'll be wishing you had more time to spend on the readings, they really are that interesting. Whether gov or not, take this class, you won't regret it at all.
Last assignment is heavy ! 20 pages is a lot, so don't leave till last minute. Utilize Julie as a resource – chatting about your assignment over coffee is always a great option.
This class is AMAZING!!! Professor Weaver is such an amazing professor, really one of the best I've ever had. She's very helpful in guiding us through both new content and new skills, and she's very understanding of all levels of experience with the kind of content we engage with. The class is also divided really neatly into both interpreting research and understanding how to go about creating our own research designs. The topics are really relevant and important, and you really feel like you're taking a lot away from the things you're reading. I love this class and would happily recommend it to those interested!
