This course is an amazing intro to the literature on democracy in the global south, but beyond that, Dr. Weaver provides a really great overview of academic reading and writing that would be extremely helpful to any student, but especially those hoping to write a thesis or pursue a career in academia.
Julie is a great professor who is very intentional about structuring the course and helping students leave with a takeaway from each reading/week. You read a good amount of original research, which is super helpful for getting a higher–level view of the state of research. Good exposure to field, great discussions, and the assignments are not super high–pressure.
Take this class! Julie is an incredible teachers who leads amazing discussions. It is a decent amount of work each week, but Julie's reading guidance is great and makes the readings super manageable. One of my favorite classes at Harvard!
Invest more time into the research paper earlier on in the course so that it's easier later on
Take this class with Prof.. Weaver! She is very nice, and the reading can be a bit tedious, but it is worth it. Please don't think it is a gem, but as a class to learn more about the global South and democratization. It is a comparative government class where the information is presented clearly in class, and then we synthesize our thoughts in class. You will be expected to do a lot of work in this role.
Julie truly cares for her students and the course material. Though, given the amount of time and effort she puts into the class and outlining guidelines for the paper, she also expects a good amount from her students.
This is a great option for a gov94! Julie Weaver is an excellent professor, and the readings and discussions in this course were particularly interesting and engaging. The weekly workload is manageable, and assignments allow for flexible exploration of research interests. The final paper may seem intimidating, but assignments in this course build upon each other and support is readily available and super helpful – I would definitely recommend taking this course!
