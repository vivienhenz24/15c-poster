Comments
This course is a fantastic overview of the German short story tradition––of the Kurzgeschichte––over two centuries. I really enjoyed taking it; there's a story for everyone in this class. Also––Anne Dymek is a fantastic lecturer, and you should take a class with her if you get the chance to.
If you are willing to engage seriously with a text and have a reasonably strong knowledge of reading German, this is an absolutely brilliant course, and I could not recommend it highly enough (especially if Dr. Dymek is teaching it).
German 119B was a fantastic course that allowed me to get a glimpse of a variety of German literature from the 18th to the 20th century.
Professor Dymek is a wonderful lecturer, with an incredible knowledge of German culture, literature and philosophy, and many other fields. She's also very kind and understanding, and her encouragement gives everyone the support and motivation they need to produce their best work. You absolutely should take a course with her if you get the chance!
TAKE THIS CLASS (especially if it is with Anne Dymek). It will teach you a lot, not only about German literature, but how to have meaningful discussions about texts and short stories.
