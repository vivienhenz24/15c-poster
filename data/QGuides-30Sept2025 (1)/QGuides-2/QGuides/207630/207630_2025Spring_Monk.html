TAKE THIS COURSE!!! Professor Monk is a true genius and I learned so much in this course. I would recommend it to everyone.
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! This is arguably the only course at Harvard where I received the academic experience I've been searching for. I enjoyed learning about race and ethnicity (my understanding of which has completely transformed) through several distinct, interesting cases across time and space. Ellis made it a point to apply what we learned to contemporary society, which I found especially valuable. Moreover, he managed to create a laidback (but still engaging) environment that always invited discussion, which I really did appreciate. I will admit that the readings could feel a bit dense at times, though I was taking this course with other courses with very dense readings. I think every student should take this course for how transformative and applicable it isif you have the space, take this course, and if you don't, make the space!
