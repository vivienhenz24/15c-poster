Easy graduate research seminar to meet advanced math requirement.
Future students should be aware that this course is extremely unstructured, with minimal guidance, few assignments, and very limited instructor involvement. There are no weekly problem sets, and lectures often end early. Grades are based largely on a final project and class participation, with very little substantive feedback provided. If you are looking for a rigorous, supportive environment to deeply learn evolutionary dynamics, you may find this course disappointing. If you are looking for a low–intensity course requiring minimal ongoing work, it may meet your expectations.
