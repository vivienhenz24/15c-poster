Awesome material. World class professor. Optional homework. Optional section. One real project that you can use. Pretty $WAG I cannot lie. Bringing me closer to that math secondary.
This course was barely any work, but I got nearly nothing out of it. The lectures were disorganized and often focused on provocative applications or philosophy more than on the actual mathematics. Since there were no required psets, I never learned how to use/apply the material. This was a fine course to get a requirement done with minimal, work, but I truly got very little out of it.

Personally, I was also frustrated by the discussions of how we could use the dynamic models discussed to describe cycles of war or other society–level phenomena. I felt these discussions to be unhelpfully provocative, ridiculously oversimplifying complex problems by declaring that these extremely simple models could relate to them.
