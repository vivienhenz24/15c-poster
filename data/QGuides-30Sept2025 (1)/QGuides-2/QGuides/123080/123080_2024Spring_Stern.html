This is a wonderful class and you can get a lot out of it if you put in the work. You don't need any formal training to be successful and create beautiful work. You will learn a lot.

It is a very small class and many people who want to be in it aren't able to take it. In light of that, please only enroll if you are going to take the class seriously. It benefits everyone to only have students who are actively engaged.
You NEED to take this class. So much fun, so much freedom to make whatever you want every week and just have fun with it, and the pace is very manageable. I would strongly recommend this class to anyone. I hope you take it.
I cannot recommend this class enough. It helped me immensely to hone my artistic skills, and my communication skills more broadly. I feel like what I learned in this class is also very transferable to other areas of my life, including when writing my thesis (which was a science thesis and not at all related to TDM).
This is an awesome class. Marcus is possibly one of the nicest and most supportive teachers ever. The pace of work is fast – a film or live scene every 2 weeks – but if you stick with it, plan ahead, and really dedicate yourself to producing interesting work, you'll get so much out of the course. It takes time but is so rewarding!
