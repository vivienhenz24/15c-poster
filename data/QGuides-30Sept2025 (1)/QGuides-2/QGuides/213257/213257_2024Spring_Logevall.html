I would tell future students that this class is what you make of it and to put the time and effort into getting the most out of the class.
Great class, interesting discussions, and amazing guest speakers (e.g. Graham Allison)
This is a fantastic history course that helps develop logical thinking skills and approaches that can be used in everyday life. Lectures are riveting although the contributions from grad students in the course during the discussion may be jarring/intimidating at first.
This is a great HKS class. Be prepared that there isn't much "lecture" so if you are looking to learn about history this is more discussion based. Professor Logevall is iconic and always has excellent perspectives whilst challenging your own. HKS students are also great to listen to and learn from.
I highly recommend this course to anyone interested in history, foreign policy and decision–making. It offers a rich grounding in Applied History, building up from basic fundamentals to the point where you can deploy your own distinct approach to policy dilemmas. Prof. Logevall is a fantastic and enthusiastic teacher, great to listen to, and cultivates a rich and respectful environment for class discussions.
This is a great course to learn about thinking. Professor Logevall is a great lecturer who is passionate and experienced in the content, and truly wants to make an impact in the outcomes of his students. If you commit to the course, it will give a lot in return!
