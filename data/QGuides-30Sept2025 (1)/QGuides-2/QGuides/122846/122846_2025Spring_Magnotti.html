Definetly take if you are thinking about writing a thesis
Take it! Research is awesome and very worthwhile as an undergraduate, and this course allows you to spend a lot of time on it!
This course has pushed me to pursue a senior thesis and become more fascinated by science. I was really scared of doing research especially as it was my first time being in lab with no prior experience or knowledge. However, I decided to begin in my journey and joined a an amazing lab that supports me through my new experiences and makes sure I feel prepared to take on my own project. I can't wait to continue my thesis with the guidance of the Murthy lab.
Do research for credit! It is a great experience!
Take it, and pick your lab carefully. That will make all the difference.
This is a great option if you want to dedicate time to research during the school year and if you are interested in writing a thesis!
Push yourself to be as active as possible in your lab environment, whether that's showing up to lab meetings in–person, scheduling meetings with your PI, or jumping in to help with tasks when needed!
I definitely recommend taking this course in both junior spring and senior fall if you know that you'll be doing a thesis because it's a great way to earn credit hours (and an easy A) for just working on your thesis. It's also a flexible but efficient way to be held accountable to make sure you're not falling behind and that your research is thoroughly planned and executed. Neuroscience advisors also give you feedback early on (during the summer before senior year), so that's really helpful.
