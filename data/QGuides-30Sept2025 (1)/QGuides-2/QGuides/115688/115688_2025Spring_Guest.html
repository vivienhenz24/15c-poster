You need to take this class, it will change movie watching for you forever!
AFVS 70 is a great course for anyone (not just AFVS students) who wants to explore film studies in a more serious way. You will learn a ton about how to watch movies, the techniques, and the production process, as well as many different kinds of directors and films. Professor Guest is an incredibly passionate lecturer who is so knowledgeable, inviting many cool directors and conversations. Be warned that screenings can be a big time commitment and given the nature of the course, there is a lot of in–person elements. However, if you have the time and buy into the experience, you'll get so much out of it!
It wasn't fully what I expected. I found myself confused about the identity of the course. The films screenings are a bigger time commitment than you might expect, but the film selections are excellent and will definitely broaden your cinematic knowledge/tastes.
