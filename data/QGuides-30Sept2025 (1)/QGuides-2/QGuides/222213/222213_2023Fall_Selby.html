It's a really, really interesting course! You will learn about Ayurveda, masculinity/femininity, aging and dying and even bodybuilding, and what these all mean within Indian society. If any of that sounds fascinating to you, definitely take this course.
I would 100% recommend anyone who is interested in medicine, south asian studies, history, or social interactions to take this course! It is SO interesting and you will truly learn so much and have such fruitful conversations.
This class was very engaging and filled with many interactive, thought–provoking discussions due to its tight–knit, seminar–styled structure. I really enjoyed the ability to prepare for and lead different week's discussions, as I think such a format helped us to dive deep into our topic of interest, while also being able to hear from our peers and see how they interpreted the readings for that week. In this way, I felt that I gained very valuable insights through our readings and discussions regarding the history and modern–day impact of Ayurveda and other forms of Indian medicine.
Great course! You learn a variety of topics about Ayurveda as well as Indian society. Amazing discussions and manageable workload.
