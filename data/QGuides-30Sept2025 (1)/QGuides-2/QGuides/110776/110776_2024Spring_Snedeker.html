I liked it
I learnt so much in this class! There is a lot of content, and you definitely need to be proactive in attending lectures and staying on top of readings, but trust me it is worth it. Professor Snedeker is incredibly knowledgeable, and she has the amazing ability to seem to never slow down or lose focus in her lectures. Her enthusiasm was borderline scary. The essays are graded pretty generously, although the exams are more difficult, partly due to the style of questions being asked leaning more towards pure memory. Figuring out the exam technique for this class made the final much easier than the midterm though. Still, I loved this class!
I would like to tell future students that no matter what you are studying, this class makes you a better person because it makes you better equipped for everything related to children. This course is the type where the vast majority of the material directly applies to your everyday life, and is taught by a passionate and dedicated teaching staff. I could not more highly recommend this course.
It is a really good class! The content is really interesting and Jesse actually knows everything about dev psych! It was really fun asking her questions because she is really enthusiastic about it and can instantly tell you a bunch of research on whatever topic you're curious about. Lecture wise it is a pretty intense course, hard to keep up in terms of taking notes because it goes really fast. But the assignments aren't that bad, the papers are very structured and the readings can be kind of a lot but they don't show up on the exams at all. Gabor ran a good section and was also very helpful for reviewing before the exams!
Its a very interred and useful class.
Only take this course if you feel you have a very strong hold on all topics from psych 1. The class is very interesting and Professor Snedeker is very engaging, but there is a ton of information that you need to be able to memorize for exams. Also, there is not a ton of structure for preparing for exams, so if that stresses you out I would not recommend this class.
