3. Statistics boring BOOOOOO: A common misconception (which Joe always addresses at the beginning of STAT110) is that this class is not technically on statistics, rather, it is an introduction to probability (as the name suggests). (6) What matters for exams is that material will show up if either (1) talked about in a HW, (2) reiterated in a section, or (3) talked about in Lecture. Use the book as a second source beyond the lecture as another way to solidify your understanding.
