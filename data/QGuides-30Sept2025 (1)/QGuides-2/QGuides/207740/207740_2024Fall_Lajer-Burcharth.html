What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I highly recommend this course to any student who appreciates drawing and would like to study it on a deeper level. The class discussions were highly engaging, and I appreciated everyone bringing their unique perspectives to the topics. I truly enjoyed the opportunity to take this class in the Harvard Art Museums special collections room, where we had the privilege of examining a variety of master drawings up close with our bare eyes!
My recommendation would be to try to participate in some way in every class. Not every reading or subject will capture your fancy, to be sure, but these are the areas most fertile for growth. Your fellow classmates will help you along the way.
A great class to take in the HAA department. Ewa is an absolute pleasure to have class with and is super knowledgable in her field. She is very encouraging towards students and wants to see them succeed... there is a never dull moment in class. It is very exploratory where for the final paper, you are able to research what YOU enjoy.
You'll get to see some really, really neat art if you take this class. It's going to be so hard to take your eyes off of it. The professor is one of the coolest and most knowledgeable people you'll ever meet. Take this class and see drawing in a totally new way!!
