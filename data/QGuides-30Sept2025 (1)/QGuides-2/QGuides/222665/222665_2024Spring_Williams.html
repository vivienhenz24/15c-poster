This might be my favorite class at Harvard. The material is absolutely beautiful and I would recommend taking this or any class with Professor Williams if you have the opportunity. Her lectures are extremely clear and she provides all of the necessary definitions and background, so you shouldn't be scared about taking this class, even without a huge amount of background.
Math 287Z was in many ways the best course I've taken this semester. Prof. Williams is a phenomenal teacher; you will feel like you are consistently getting the best presentation of the material, while gleaming some insights into the research/problem–solving that you can't easily find in texts. The material was comprehensive and compellingly interesting, spanning from the necessary basics all the way up to recent developments and relevant open problems. Take this course if you can; it really was a wonderful experience.
this class was a lot of fun and I'd really recommend taking it. the material is exciting, the assignments are short, and the lectures are great :D
