Comments
Writing a thesis was a lovely experience it was definitely a huge project, and I did get crunched at the end, but I don't have any regrets. The tutorial part of this class is not the most stringent, so make sure you're keeping up with your own work and maintaining a good relationship with your advisor. Good luck!
Thesising is soooo difficult but soooo worth it! It was so meaningful to put this much time and attention into a project and produce something that is actually sort of good after. You just get to learn so much and get really deep into a topic. That being said, it is a lot of time! Leading up to the deadline I was basically spending every spare moment on the thesis. So make sure to choose a topic you want to learn a lot about and make sure to structure your schedule in a way that will allow you to dedicate enough time to the thesis.
