This is a very intensive experience, but it also very rewarding for the information, knowledge, and wisdom about the topic and the world that you gain!!
Make sure you stay on top of your individual deadlines– the concentration does not hand hold you. Meet with your advisor weekly/bi–weekly, and read past thesis examples to have an idea of what you are striving for with your end product throughout the process.
You take this class if you're working on a thesis. Plan ahead and you'll be fine!
