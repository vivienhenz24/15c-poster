What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
ECON 2040 is a great field course that will expose you to some of the most advanced work in the field of experimental economics. Professor Enke is an effective lecturer who knows so much both through his own work and his close connections with many of the researchers in the field, and I learned a lot through class discussions of papers. The problem sets and final project are also great ways to apply learning to potential projects of our own, which is a rewarding experience. Professor Enke is genuinely invested in his students' success, and always interested in giving you constructive feedback!
I you want to be an experimental economist, you must take this course. Full stop – best phd course I've ever taken. If you want to be any kind of experimental social scientist, I kid you not – go take phd micro so that you can enroll in this course. Changed my entire perspective and prepared me to do real research in ways I never before considered. Prof Enke is superb – the problem sets are both informative and help you come up with new ideas. Every paper we read was interesting and Ben is a very good lecturer. Seriously. Take this course.
Excellent course that introduces students to currently used tools in experimental economics while covering frontier behavioral experimental economics research. It's focused less on applications and more on behavioral theory, but the assignments are creative and pushing you to think more about your own research.
If you are interested in pursuing an academic career in economics or experimental methods, this course is essential. If you are not, it may not be worth the time or commitment.
