Dr. Fairchild is such a joy to have as a teacher! I loved her lectures and going to her office hours. She is very knowledgable, approachable, and kind. I appreciated that she was able to make our large lecture feel interactive by allowing for plenty of opportunities for students to answer questions or raise comments.

I genuinely had so much fun completing assignments, especially toward the end when we got to do them in the real world (e.g., the interview). Perhaps a little more clarity on the fact that these assignments didn't need to be related to our final project would've been helpful, in addition to clarity about the fact that the final assignment isn't actually a research project you conduct in the real world (rather, a proposal). These points aside, the readings were really interesting, the midterm exam was fair (not too hard, not too easy, just right), and I feel like I learned a lot, both from Fairchild and Jen–Chen (my TF).
