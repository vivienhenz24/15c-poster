Comments
You will love this course, as it gives you an introduction to the field of screenwriting and encourages you to venture out and try new things with your writing. It also gives you a chance to develop your skills as the feedback and workshop component allows you to hear your peers' opinions, which is helpful for the process for improving writing.
This course was a fantastic introduction to screenwriting! Musa is fantastic, bringing an excellent energy and enthusiasm to each class. The course assignments are almost all screenwriting exercises or short scriptswith script feedback given to peersso the course feels very focused in its goal and topic. I really enjoyed the films sent as viewing before class and the films we watched during class; they displayed a wide variety of styles, themes, genres, and tones. If you have a script in mind for development, the workshopping in class is an invaluable way to get feedback.
This is one of the courses I will remember from my Harvard career for a long, long timepossibly forever. It was SO interesting, and I loved meeting everyone through the class. Musa does an incredible job of cultivating the community within the course, and I'm so grateful I had the opportunity to take this class! I love the balance between getting to work on your own writing and getting feedback while also learning about and analyzing shorts that have been produced. I had so much fun and learned a lot. It was truly always the highlight of my week!
Musa is a great professor who is obviously very knowledgable and passionate about the short film format. Discussions are vibrant and informative, and you are likely to get as much out of the class as you put in.
You should definitely take this class. Musa is the best ever and so nice. You will come out of this feeling comfortable writing screenplays.
