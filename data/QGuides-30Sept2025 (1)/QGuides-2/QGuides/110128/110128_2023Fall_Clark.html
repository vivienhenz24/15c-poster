If you are on the fence about joining RCS, try it out!! I met some of the most kind, thoughtful friends from here. RCS is like a family, especially with Liz, Justin, and Naomi! This course meets the needs of students who may not have as much experience singing with choirs by offering group review, office hours, practice tracks, and sight–reading practice every week, so I have met peers from all levels of experience here. However, if you have a busy course–load, make sure your time–management skills are top–notch as well, otherwise it can lead to burnout quickly. There are also extra rehearsal times during performance weeks.
RCS is one of the most welcoming and supportive communities at Harvard. We get to sing incredible music, travel together, and make lots of friends. It is a great break from the stress of other classes because it is fine to make mistakes – all you have to do is show up and put in effort.
