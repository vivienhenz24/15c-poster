This will not be an easy course. You will have a serious amount of work every day, and if you aren't responsible with your time it will seriously bite you. However, if you are willing to put the work in you will get a lot out of it. Ustadha Dana and Ustadh Kevin are extremely helpful, and they will be there to help you through. Ustadha Dana is one of the most kind and effective instructors I have had this semester. This class will admittedly require a decent amount of work, especially near the middle of the semester when it feels like you are getting 20 new vocabulary words thrown at you every day, but it is pretty worth it. This class will help you grow your Arabic language skills from near–nonexistent to more than you would believe. If you are interested in Arabic, I'd recommend giving this class a shot.
