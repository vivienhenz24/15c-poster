Please take this seminar! Giovanni is an amazing professor. He is great at facilitating discussions, and he is overall just an interesting and fun guy! This seminar is hardly a time commitment outside of class, and it is a great way to ease yourself into college while still taking a worthwhile course. Take this class! You get to know your peers and you get to discuss really interesting issues that will play a role in the future. It might be a little intimidating at first to participate in class discussions but do it! It is so enriching and I learned a lot about the world and myself.
Parmigiani is an amazing professor!! I learned so much from him and deepened my desire to be an oncologist just from one class a week! The class is structured so that no prior experience is needed; the first three weeks teach you everything you need to know for the class about cancer/genetics. The assignments are thought–provoking and include group presentations, student–led debates, and class discussions. There is minimal reading required and not much time needed outside of class. Perfect way to ease yourself into college with a thought–provoking seminar.
