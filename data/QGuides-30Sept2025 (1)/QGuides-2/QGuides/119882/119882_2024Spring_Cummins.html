What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
If one is willing to invest proper care into their studies, they will reap the rewards.
I strongly recommend you to take this course if you have any interest in South Asian studies. The richness of the language is amazing and it is worthwhile to study one of the epic language of this world.
Work hard. It is worth working hard.
I think Sanskrit can open new doors in terms of learning other languages, understanding important sacred texts, and providing insight into linguistics and language evolution. Surprisingly, it also gave me insight into english– we use our native tongue but don't always understand the nuts and bolts of language until we learn a classical language. However, Sanskrit is challenging to learn and I think you need a really good guide, something that you definitely get in this course.
You could not ask for a better introduction to one of the deepest and most profound languages.
