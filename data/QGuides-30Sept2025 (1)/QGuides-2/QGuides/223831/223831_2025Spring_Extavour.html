What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
The papers we read in the course were good for sampling a little bit of what is being done today (and has been done historically) across a wide range of areas within developmental biology.
Be prepared to spend a very long time reading the papers for this course. Additionally, your proposals will be judged very harshly despite the fact that you don't have much time to design them, so start thinking very early on about your topic and aims. Honestly, don't expect to get any praise in this class, it definitely trains you in a serious and intense way.
