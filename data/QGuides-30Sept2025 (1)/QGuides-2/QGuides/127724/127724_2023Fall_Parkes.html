Comments
If you have strong German and would like to learn even more about idiomatic expressions, wordplay, dialect, and all the other comedy–associated rhetorical devices you should definitely take this course. Lisa Parkes is an excellent professor who always keeps the course interesting, provides terrific feedback, and is incredibly knowledgeable. Take this course if you want to read texts that will make you laugh, cry, and everything in between.
Lisa Parkes is great, and I would highly recommend taking a class with her if you get the chance!
THIS CLASS WAS SO MUCH FUN!!!! The readings were hilarious but also thought–provoking, the perfect level of difficult (pushed my German skills but also enjoyable and understandable). Reading "Die Physiker" was probably my favorite text. Discussions were genuinely fun, engaging, and insightful. I enjoyed the acting portions of the class as well! Professor Parkes was very compassionate, understanding, knowledgeable, and accessible. She gave extensive feedback on written assignments (oftentimes on multiple drafts!!). And the workload was manageable. I cannot recommend this class enough––definitely my favorite course I have taken at Harvard.
Ihr werdet auf jeden Fall diesen Kurs lieben!!! The class is amazing –– Take it if you are interested in German or German comedy!!! Professor Parkes is wonderful, not to mention immensely nice and approachable, and the readings were super fun and interesting. That being said, a few were a little tricky to read so make sure you have a solid background in German; but they were explained clearly in the lecture. In addition, we had a lot of engaging discussions and fun activities throughout the semester and the group really felt like a close–knit community due to the small size. May be my favorite class I have taken on campus to date.
The course is wonderful. To understand a comedy, you will hone your understanding of German language and have a nice time engaging with the various pieces. It is also helpful in understanding German culture writ large.
