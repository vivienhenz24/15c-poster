Can't recommend the class enough, she and the teaching staff really help guide you through the research paper, which is the only intimidating part of the class, and you'll end up producing a body of literature you can be proud of. I left class everyday thinking meeting two hours, once a week wasn't close to enough, which is crazy because I've never wished a course had more class time. Amazing course with one of the best professors you can ask for

The material covered is incredibly important and interesting, and Professor Goldin is the best–positioned person to teach this content. She is sharp, brilliant, and compassionate to her students, and despite winning a Nobel Prize in the middle of the semester (!!!) she devotes so much time to working with her students on our final research papers. If you are interested in how people make some of their most important life decisions (education, family, fertility, career, etc.), this is THE class to take.

This is one of the best courses I have taken at Harvard. Professor Goldin is really incredible, and you will learn a lot in this course. On a week–to–week basis the work is not too much as it consists of reading and writing comments on papers, but in weeks where assignments for the research paper are due you will work very hard and spend a lot of time just thinking. Definitely start working on the paper sooner rather than later or you will make your life harder than it needs to be. I could not recommend the course more.
