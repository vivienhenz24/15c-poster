Comments
It'll definitely help you keep up your conversation skills but it is a ton of work. I was not stressed about the grading but definitely about getting stuff done. The conversation exchange was extremely anxiety inducing for me and my partner was not accommodating, so I definitely disliked that part of the class.
Definitely take this class! I personally took French 30 and 40 before this, and French 50 is certainly my favourite of the three and the one I’ve learnt the most from. There’s more work in this class than the others, but it really helps build confidence: in–class compositions and at–home conversations can be stressful, but the stakes are set reasonably and the resources to help you succeed are definitely there!
This is a good class! Dr. Turman is very passionate and brings her personal travels into the class. I learned a lot about how to express my views on important issues outside of just the regular Duolingo "this is an apple" lol. The exchange program is great for improving speaking skills, and even though it might feel like a lot of writing work, it really is necessary to help improve your writing.
This course will make your French improve immensely, however, it is not a walk in the park like other French courses. That means you should be prepared to spend a considerable amount of time on essays and homework assignments. However, you will get so much out of it.
Highly recommend! If you want to truly improve your French speaking and writing skills, this class is great! The more effort that you put in, the more that you will gain.
