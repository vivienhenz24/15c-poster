great class, moves significantly faster than korean bb.
This is a fun class, pacing is done very well
This class is incredible!! The Korean department cares so much about the content and making class fun but also teaching very thoroughly. That being said..... this class is a LOT. Class every day, vocab quizzes on Mondays, dictation quizzes on Fridays, and two small essays and two presentations, and homework due Tuesday and Thursday. Not even gonna lie, even though I already spoke a little Korean at home, it was so easy for me to fall behind (and I did many times lol), especially skipping Korean BA, I was not used to the structure of the class, and it was a lot for me. But if you're prepared to spend a little more time, you will LOVE this class.
This class is great. It is difficult and time consuming, but worth the effort! You learn a lot, since you are continually tested, but you will get used to it and there is a lot of support. I recommend an Arc Tutor, as that was of much support to me as well. The classes are a lot of fun and everyone in the department is so kind! Professor Kim (male) and Professor You (section) are great!! We play lots of games and activities that help you practice Korean, so the process is fun!
