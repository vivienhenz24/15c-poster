What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is an amazing class. Yes, it's a lot of work, you have to memorize kanji and vocabulary on a weekly basis, you have a lot of assignments, and you can only speak in Japanese in class, but it's amazing. Having been in the same class with the same teacher and a lot of the same classmates for two years, you're all in it together, and it's really a great time. The teacher is a great teacher and also very understanding and there are a lot of assignments to pad your grade, so as long as you show up to class, do all the assignments, and submit everything on time, you will not have to worry about your grade. Of course, if you actually want to do quite well speaking Japanese in class, you will have to put in more work memorizing and practicing grammar and vocab, but most people don't put in that work, so you all kind of suck together sometimes but it's fun and it's part of the vibes. I highly highly recommend this class – Japanese has been my favorite class at Harvard every year so far.
Please take this class. The instructors are so nice and genuinely want you to get the most out of the course. I especially recommend East Asian languages speakers to take the course because your Japanese will improve exponentially!
There is no better way to take Japanese than this course. However, you will need to put in a lot of work. Its rewarding because you actually learn the language. The grading system is fair, and the teaching staff is excellent. Just a generally very well put together course
Best language class I have ever had. Strongly recommended for anyone who is interested in learning Japanese.
TAKE THIS CLASS! I know class meetings 5 times a week seems like a lot but it is genuinely a class I look forward to every day.
