Loved this class! It was definitely my favorite class of the semester. Prof. Momii and all of the TFs are great and very helpful and knowledgeable. This class will help you think more deeply about the music you listen to, from the instrumentation to the vocalists themselves, and the importance of pop music in popular culture. You get to look at music from a musical angle but also from a cultural angle.
Great course. You don't need to come in with any background in music, just an interest to learn. Professor Momii deeply cares about his students and the course. That being said, class can sometimes feel slow, and you have to do all of the readings for the weekly assignments (even when they feel slow). Overall, a really fun course.
I highly recommend this course! I feel like I gained a lot from the readings and the assignments, and have a better understanding of how to interpret musical techniques in pop music.
It is a great course to learn the basics of music analysis. It is creative and also encourages critical thinking.
take this course only if you are ready to do a fair amount of work for a music course at harvard. extensive weekly assignments on top of lecture and section readings, and three papers just feels like overkill for a course that isn't taken as a requirement. the material is cool but the professor doesn't do much lecturing, every class is structured like a discussion. if that's your thing, would highly recommend, but otherwise, do not expect a typical theory lecture course.
THIS WAS MY FAVORITE CLASS I'VE TAKEN AT HARVARD SO FAR! I loved every aspect of it. I've not yet seen a class so well–structured and exciting. The topics are so interesting and the music choices are incredible. I would leave from every class incredibly mindblown. It really covers so many aspects of the music process, like the production and recording process, the music industry, the culture and sociological background, and it is done in a way that is very accessible to even non–musicians. The workload is very reasonable and the TFs are so amazing. If you are a musician takes this, if not, still take it. This class is definitely the highlight of my time here at Harvard and it has truly solidified my goal to be a musician for a career.
Take this class! So awesome, no matter your musical background you will learn a lot.
Really interesting course material with great assignments. Went into it with no experience in music and it was a bit painful but teaching team does a good job getting you up to speed but does require you to add in extra time
This course is great. It's very easy, but that is certainly by design. I took it to count towards my Music secondary. My TF was awesome is the sections kept me very engaged. I wish the professor was more engaging.
Your will hear a lot of great music and learn how to think about popular music from a theory perspective.
This class was super fun! It really helped garner my appreciation for different types of popular music and I enjoyed learning how to use different tools to analyze what makes a song so intriguing/enjoyable to listen to.
Good class! Weekly assignments can sometimes feel very tedious, especially with the LONG readings, but honestly they are not that necessary, so if you are not interested or don't have time you don't have to make them. Just make sure of going to lecture because they go over the content of the readings either way and of keeping a score of the material being covered in case it's helpful for you final project. The weekly assignments are based on the readings but they can be done by just skimming the reading material. The class was a good break, especially section, which basically consisted in listening to songs. The final project can be a good amount of work but it will be enjoyable if you choose something you actually care about (so choose something you actually care about!)
Absolutely take this class if you love music! I had such a great time and learned a ton. With that being said, you'll get more out of it if you have at least a little bit of a music background.
This was a super fun and intersting class! Would recommend taking it if you have some musical background and enjoy listening to pop music. The class went through a lot of examples from many different genres and helped me explore genres I never listened to before.
