I would highly recommend this class! Jesse and Vincent are both brilliant, and it was a pleasure getting to learn from them throughout the semester. You'll get out of the course what you put inthe check ins are a great way to get feedback on research ideas you're thinking about, and Jesse and Vincent are both happy to talk and provide feedback after class. I learned a ton and really enjoyed the breadth of material that was covered.
Excellent course that covers a wide range of topics within the field of political economy. The course is useful for anybody who is interested in PE
As an undergrad – if you're interested in Political Economy and considering going to graduate school, then absolutely take this class (at least as an auditor). The class covers so much interesting material and Jesse and Vincent are both phenomenal lecturers who engage a lot with the students throughout the semester. While the assignments might seem intimidating, the reading questions definitely help you understand the material better and doing problem sets in groups of three makes them a lot more approachable.
The class is simultaneously accessible to people not fully immersed in political economy, while also providing a lot of depth for those who want to really dig into the meat of it.
