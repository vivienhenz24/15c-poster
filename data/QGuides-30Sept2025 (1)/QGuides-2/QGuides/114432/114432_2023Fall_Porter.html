What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
TAKE THIS COURSE!! Professor Porter is amazing and truly cares to see his students succeed! There are few graded assignments, but Prof and the TF (at least David Sutton) are always there to support you! The class is full of grad students, but it is not hard to thrive as an undergraduate there. I learned so much from the course, and would recommend all Gov students take it.
I would tell future undergrad students to not be too intimidated by the number of graduate students in the class. I was unsure if it was the place for me after seeing the number of older students at the first class, but I was certainly able to keep up!
GOV 1540 is an amazing class – definitely take it if you are interested in the American presidential system. Professor Porter is a fantastic storyteller and lecturer. David, the TF, is extremely approachable and knowledgeable. I've taken many GOV classes at Harvard, and this has definitely been my favorite. The assignments are a little demanding, but they're intellectually rewarding to complete.
This is a great class if you’re looking to broaden your understanding of what it’s actually like to work in the White House and how to become a better person. Professor Porter and the teaching staff really care about the students and our learning, which is amazing.
