This class is great! There are a lot of readings but since class is weekly you'll have a fair bit of time to do them. The assignments were incredibly flexible and manageable. Professor Lufkin was great to have in class! She would sometimes have treats for us and make us some yummy fresh bread, but she also had good energy during class.
If you are looking for a relatively easy class, this may be the one for you. However, the readings can be dense/confusing, but these are balanced with assignments that are not incredibly difficult. The professor is quirky but fun and flexible, and supportive of students' needs and contributions.
Take the course! It is very interesting and you will learn a lot about not only tattoos, but also the history of people and their movements. Prof. Lufkin is awesome and so welcoming of new perspectives. The class is very collaborative, but not intimidatingly so. The readings can be a little long but they are all interesting and worthwhile to engage with.
You should take this class! The material is fascinating and the discussions will make you think about everyday things differently.
