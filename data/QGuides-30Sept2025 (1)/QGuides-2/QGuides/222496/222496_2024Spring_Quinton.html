Take this class! Dr Quinton is an amazing professor. She has such genuine love for the subject and for your interests as well. The readings can be a tad bit long, but the assignments are always fun and very manageable. As long as you are active in class and do the readings, you can learn so many amazing things in this class. You also attend performances so this was a wonderful class to be able to venture out for performances I would not have seen without this course. This class has changed how I view performance .
This class was amazing. I love performance and and this class was perfect, as you get to attend and write about performances. Class discussions are great and the topics are extremely interesting. Professor Quinton also gives great feedback and is a great professor overall!
It's a very fun elective for Hist&Lit concentrators or anyone remotely interested in performance. Dr. Quinton is amazing and fosters a wonderful class environment. You also get to see 4 live performances for free!
I absolutely adored this course! If you have room in your schedule and even a remote interest in the performing arts you should absolutely take it. Laura is incredible and kind, class is so fun and intimate, and the free trips to see performances around Boston are fantastic.
