Comments
Take this class!!! You will learn a lot of serious and very important history of business and imperialism in Latin America while also improving your Spanish skills. The class is not stressful or a big workload, but rather really focuses on fostering in depth discussions about exploitation, patriarchy, discrimination, corruption as well as activism & the fight for justice. The small class size leads to so many insightful discussions and a great atmosphere for improving your language skills and learning. This is one of the best courses I've taken here.

Also, try to get Wilnomy, who is the most INCREDIBLE teacher!!! Wilnomy was so knowledgable and supportive, and as a result I looked forward to every class.
This class should be called "Literature of Exploitation in Latin America," since its syllabus is almost entirely fiction. I signed up thinking we'd read history or policy (which, to be fair we did.... just only once), and instead found that this was a literature class. If you're excited to do that, then I'd definitely recommend.
This is the second course I have taken with Wilnomy, and Wilnomy is easily the best instructor I have had at Harvard. They bring an incredible warmth and vibrancy to the classroom that turns each lesson into an engaging and heartfelt experience, while being keen to making sure that each one of us are developing as academics and as people. Their approach to teaching is characterized by a sincere passion for the subject and a deep concern for student well–being. On top of that, I left each class feeling confident that not only my spanish improved, but also that there was someone genuinely rooting for me and my development. SPANSH61n is an incredible course with an incredible instructor –– take it!!!
If you're wondering whether to take this course, don't hesitate to take it! It was one of my most favorite courses at Harvard (I say this as a senior) and taught me so much about how the US and Latin America have gotten to their economic standings today. This course discusses ethics, history, imperialism, and culture in a very wholistic and sensitive manner!
I absolutely love Wilnomy. this is my second class with her after taking SPANSH 50, and I had such an amazing time learning from her again. She makes a genuine effort to engage with the class and encourages each student to participate! Her feedback is really helpful and also incredibly motivating. The readings and discussions from this lecture were also engaging and meaningful. You will leave this class feeling more confident with the Spanish language and more knowledgeable about the world itself. TAKE THIS CLASS!!
