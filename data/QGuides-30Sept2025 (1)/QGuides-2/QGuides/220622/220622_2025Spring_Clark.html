Take this class! It is a great opportunity to get credit for the hard work you put into choir, as well as to learn new pieces and perform them in masterclasses, a recital, and a jury.

The vocal coaching sessions are the most useful component of the course, and they can be obtained without putting yourself through the "4 hours a week" of rehearsal, which, in case you were wondering means more like 7 or 8 hours in actuality, with some weeks involving 15+hours of time commitment.
Take this opportunity!!!! Its a huge huge opportunity you wont get again after college to study with incredible voice teachers and get feedback from even more incredible teachers, and the environment is so supportive. You get to study what you want in terms of style and pieces. Its amazing. You do need to put in your own work.
You should definitely take this class if you're in choir. It improved your voice, your confidence, your professionalism while singing and you get to learn from some of the best! I personally worked with Carley DeFranco and I couldn't recommend her more: she was encouraging and so tentative to my way of singing and my preferences, while also providing a lot of advice from her experience.
Practice regularly outside of weekly vocal lessons!
