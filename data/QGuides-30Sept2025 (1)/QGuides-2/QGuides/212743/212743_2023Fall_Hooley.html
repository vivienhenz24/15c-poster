Final project is really interesting –– both my own and hearing about other people's. The rest of the class is pretty chill if sometimes not the most engaging.
This wasn't a difficult class overall so I would recommend it! I will say sometimes it was difficult to get clear answers from Prof Knutsen and I felt like the first couple weeks just going over the logistics of family therapy were a little bit boring. But the interview assignment is really fun!
If you want to learn more about family focused therapy then you should take this class. the course title and description implies more of a focus on psychopathology, but specific disorders were not really discussed, it was more about the family influences around disorders generally. not the best psych class I have ever taken but also not the worst and glad I stuck with it. the Prof is also super flexible and understanding with illness, other absences, so even though the class was at a very inconvenient time, I was able to make it work.
TAKE THIS CLASSS! Such a gem, and the prof is so passionate about the material, he will literally try his best to answer any questions you have in depth. Very sweet man and the class material is so relatable, and very inclusive to different types of family systems. I loved it
This class will change the way you think and talk about mental health. It also opens your eyes to the importance of the family system as a whole, and supporting all dynamics within that system instead of just an individual member to ensure everyone is as happy and healthy as can be.
Definitely take this class. You get out of it what you put in, but I think the readings are super interesting and worth doing. Class is more enjoyable if you did the readings and then feel comfortable participating. It is one discussion post a week which is very reasonable and I found the final presentation and paper to be a very enjoyable experience that also allowed me to get closer to a friend and gain a better understanding of their life and how I can be their for them. There is definitely a lot to learn from this class and it was definitely my least stressful class. The work is super super manageable and not hard/stressful, and the professor is very understanding.
