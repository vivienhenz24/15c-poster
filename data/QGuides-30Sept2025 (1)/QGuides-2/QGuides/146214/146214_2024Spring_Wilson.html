What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
interesting class and Professor Wilson is very kind – i sometimes wished it was more lecture rather than discussion (it is 70% lecture 30% discussion)
A great and intersectional introduction to the Canterbury tales! Great to know about even if you've never considered looking into medieval literature.
Taking this class on The Canterbury Tales will provide with three things: the wonderful experience of learning under Professor Wilson, a greater handle on the world in an unfamiliar part of history, and the gratification of thoroughly comprehending a legendary, complex work of writing.
GOOD CLASS. Also, middle English is soooooo cool. Reading in middle English sounds daunting, but Professor Wilson made it super accessible and engaging. Middle English aside, I hadn't taken an English class since high school and did not find it hard to adjust to this one; don't be afraid to give this a try! Professor Wilson made everything digestible. And her lectures are substantial and generally wonderful to listen to.

Professor Wilson is highly knowledgeable. Come to her office hours. Get her feedback on the final project. Ask her questions during and after class. You'll be glad you did. You'll come away from this class having learned that much more if you choose to engage with her. She's awesome.

Assignments are VERY fun. Discussion posts in other classes can feel like a slog, but trust me –– Professor Wilson's are thought–provoking, accessible, and quite nice to do. The other assignments were also very creatively designed. The final project was a joy to do –– something I'd be excited to go home and work on –– and it was by far my favorite final this semester.

ALSO, for your copy of the tales, GET THE E–BOOK.
Being able to search in the text was a lifesaver. Highlighting and annotation were a part of the deal, so there were no disadvantage to not having the paper copy. And I paid $20 less than I would have for the paper copy. Highly recommend. I'm sure the paper copy was good as well, but I was a big fan of the ebook.
You should take this course! I'm a STEM concentrator and took this as a pure elective because I love literature, and it was absolutely worth it if you put in a little bit of work to learn Middle English (it's not very hard) and do the readings, you'll be fine (the final project is quite fun if you pick the creative option) and have a great time. Prof. Wilson has carefully curated the Tales so that you're reading a manageable amount, and reading the Canterbury Tales teaches you a surprising amount about history and culture beyond just getting to say that you read the Canterbury Tales. She's also a great lecturer, so you will definitely not be bored. If you're looking for an English class to take, definitely consider this one.
