I have always been someone who felt like they missed out on literature because of my education up till college, and leaving this class I genuinely feel like a well–read person. We read from the entire world, talked about the entire world. Prof. Ghansah cares about her students like they are her own people, and it shone throughout the class. This class was a support system and in turn pushed me to write better than I have before. I have never thought about reading and writing like I did in this class, she made me want to write more. I would recommend beyond doubt to take something with Prof. Ghansah, or at least try to meet her. She is definitely someone you can't miss.
