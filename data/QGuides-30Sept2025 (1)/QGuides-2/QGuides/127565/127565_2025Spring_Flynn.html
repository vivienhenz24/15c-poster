The PSETs are difficult and long. Make sure you budget enough time for those and go to office hours for help. You also need to pay attention in lecture to do well on the PSETs, as the published slides are not information–dense.
If you are curious about RNA (whatever aspects of RNA, the mol bio side of it, the biotech side of it, etc. ), do take this class! The lectures cover every aspect of RNA and Ryan is super knowledgeable in this field. There isn't too much work, and no exam at all! Just come to listen to the lectures and participate in the weekly discussion – you'll learn a lot!
If you have a strong interest in RNA biology, take this class. Ryan is an expert in his field, which makes his lectures jam–packed and informative. However, if you have only a mild interest in the topic or are taking it as a requirement, I would consider taking another class. Lectures are dry and opportunities for active learning/student participation is negligible. Sections are standard for SCRB classes: present a paper cogently.
