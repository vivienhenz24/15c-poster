TAKE THIS CLASS. Ofrit is bright, lovely, and one of the BEST profs at Harvard. The class is structured so the last half is heavy work in terms of presentations, but all of it pretty much is presentations, and you will have a very clear idea of what you need to do to get a good grade. She really wants you to learn and grow, so the class is structured like that and not focused on harsh grading. I LOVED THIS CLASS SO MUCH TAKE IT!
This class was FANTASTIC. Prof. Liviatan is so kind, accessible, and excited. The class assignments were very reasonable and interesting to complete. And, the smaller discussion group was conducive to effective participation and friendship. Would highly recommend ***
TAKE THIS CLASS. Professor Liviatan's teaching style may be a bit unorthodox but it stimulates intellectual discussion, promotes collaboration, and essentially fires off every pedagogical cylinder. She is a great Professor, and an amazing human.
This is one of my favorite classes I've taken so far, mostly because the topic is so interesting and Prof. Liviatan is the absolute best. She made sure to give us thorough background knowledge on everything we were discussing, in–class activities were fun and helped us bond as a group, and presentations were also really fun to work on (whenever everyone would pull their weight). If you're down for a VERY engaging course that will require effort but is not necessarily difficult, this will be great.
This is one of the best classes I could have possibly taken here at Harvard. Professor Liviatan is incredibly passionate about what she teaches and she makes class very engaging. Everything taught in class has affected me very profoundly and I think about it all of the time, including in my other classes. The group projects were so fun to do and I loved being able to learn from my peers in the last few weeks of class, and the final presentation was a great opportunity to engage in something that's important to you while still having it connected to AI and law. This class was something I looked forward to every week and I cannot recommend it enough.
Take this course itll be the highlight of your week, semester, and college experience.
