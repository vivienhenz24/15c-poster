What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great class! You will get a solid foundation in developmental biology content, but more importantly a lot of practice in reading, thinking, talking and writing about developmental biology. There is a proposal component towards the second half of the class, so I would recommend taking this in the G1 year so it doesn't overlap with writing your PQE.
Great introduction to developmental biology, based on publications and actual research that people are doing right now
This class will teach you so much. I am so much more aware of what I do not know after this course since we had such a wide exposure stages and mechanisms in development – which is humbling and exciting in the early stages of a devbio research pursuit. This class will treat you like a professional: you sit in a seminar–style room and listen to research and topics resented by a range of professors, they suggest papers if you are curious to follow up on, conferences will teach you – after some time – classical patterns and approaches to tackling hypotheses in the field.
