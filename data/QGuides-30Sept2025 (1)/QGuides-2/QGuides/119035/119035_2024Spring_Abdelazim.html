This was a phenomenal course that provided a great opportunity to learn physical chemistry and its lab techniques! I would highly recommend it to anyone interested in chemistry research who is looking for a place to start while also pursuing publishable research in a class setting. Professor Khaled is also a phenomenal instructor who will provide you a lot of invaluable mentorship and help through research design + experimentation. I highly recommend this class for any chem concentrator for either the lab or physical chem requirement.
Out of all the advanced lab classes, I would recommend taking this one (unless you just really want to do organic synthesis or something). Khaled is genuinely one of the nicest instructors you will ever meet here. He will help you at any time of the week with data analysis for your lab report, and he is super flexible with due dates. 10 hours a week does take up some time in your schedule, but we rarely were ever in the lab for the full 10 hours. I'm not sure if that can be said for the other advanced lab classes. Lab reports aren't the most fun but they are the only assignments besides the final project.
TAKE THIS CLASS for your advanced lab requirementeven if not required, you should still take this class! The course staff are INCREDIBLE and so nice, they are very understanding with extensions and will help you bring your wildest experimental dreams to life. There is a minimal amount of busywork/wasted time, and the independent project is very flexible so you can definitely create a project you are passionate about. You get out what you put into this class. If you have any interest in physical science research, you will fall in love with this class just like I did!
