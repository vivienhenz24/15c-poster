TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Reinert is such an interesting man who cares deeply about his students. The readings are interesting. Lectures are engaging and are going to make you think about the world in a new way. We had so many interesting discussions in this seminar that I often left the seminar thinking about the topics long after the section. The way in which Professor Reinert frames and structures the class, first providing a broader historical and economical context and often pulling things from different areas of knowledge made me walk into the class every day excited to learn and think about the questions posed in the readings. This really has been my favorite course this semester and I would advise anyone who is interested in political economy or wants to learn more about how the forces of capitalism have shaped countries, companies, and our society, to take this course.
