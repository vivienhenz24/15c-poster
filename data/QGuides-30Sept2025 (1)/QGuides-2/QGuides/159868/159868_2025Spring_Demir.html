What a wonderful time!! Turkish is such a fun and interesting language to learn, and Meryem Hocam is such an enthusiastic and clear instructor who cares so much about your individual learning. It is so cool to have walked into this class (starting with Turkish AA in the fall) not understanding a single thing to now having built a very solid foundation. Over the course of this class, you will encounter many new grammatical structures via chalkboard diagram (and learn to enjoy this process!), gain exposure to Turkish music + culture, and learn many things about your fellow classmates through discussions and creative assignments (like poems and stories)! Having very friendly and dedicated classmates makes this class even more awesome. Take Turkish, you will DEFINITELY love it!!
