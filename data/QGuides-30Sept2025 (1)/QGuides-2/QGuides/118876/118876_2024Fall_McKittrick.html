Take this class if you're passionate about theatre! It can get kind of repetitive and boring, but it's what you make of it. Don't take it if you don't actively want to learn more about dramaturgy, adaptations, etc.
This course is amazing! It's so much fun and if you're passionate about theatre, the time really flies by. I feel like I was genuinely so ignited and involved with ever class discussion, and I really felt at home being able to discuss theatre and dramaturgy so freely. It's also such a privilege and joy to be able to watch the ART's process and become involved with their dramaturgy research.
Literally I would recommend this class to everyone. Great readings and it is a very self driven class. There are three main projects that you have so much freedom over. By far one of the best classes I have taken at Harvard!
This is one of the best courses that I have taken at Harvard! The opportunity to work with real productions at the ART is phenomenal, Ryan is a wonderful professor, and Annabeth is a great TA. Be sure to come to class having done the reading and ready to participate in the discussions. There is a lot of reading for this course (at varying amounts week–to–week), so be prepared to put a lot of time into doing those readings, but it is definitely worth it. I had theater experience coming into the semester, but not much knowledge of dramaturgy specifically, so it was great to get to know theater from another point of view. You have many opportunities in this class to see wonderful theater and there is also a lot of room for creativity in each of the projects. Definitely take this course!!!
This is a great class. If you love theater, are interested in theater making, or just curious about TDM, take it! Ryan is absolutely incredible. Such a unique and amazing opportunity to get to learn from someone with so much experience in theater. He is also just such a great instructor and is so kind. I loved all three major assignments, and this class is a great way to tap into things going on at the A.R.T. 11/10 experience.
