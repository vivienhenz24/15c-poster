If you like painting YOU MUST TAKE THIS CLASS! This class was an incredible and life changing experience for my art journey. From the readings to the site visits to the group critiques, I have never learned as much about art and what it means to create art until now. You really get what you make out of it!

Again, I absolutely recommend this class to anyone who is interested, it is truly amazing and rewarding and any reservations about the studio hours and difficulty should be acknowledged, but pales in comparison to what the course offers.
