This is a wonderful class, but only take it if you want to dedicate a lot of time to painting. Judith wants people who feed into the energy of the studio and who are committed to painting (more than to their other classes). If that's you, it's an amazing and incredibly rewarding class.
This was an incredible course that created a wonderful sense of community with my peers. The environment that Professor Judith and Scott created was amazing, fun, and supportive. The course is quite time intensive, but it pays off greatly. I feel that I have grown a lot as an artist and I would definitely recommend taking this course.
The thing about any art class is you have to be prepared for the time commitment. You can't cut corners with paintings – you'll succeed in this class if you put in the time consistently over the semester. The class was a good cheerful environment and good community, Judith and Scott are both great artists who do genuinely want to support you in your learning. I enjoyed the creative freedom we had as the class went on, and I'd say take advantage of these types of classes where materials are provided (it's such an amazing opportunity).
like most art classes, you will get out what you put in. the more time you spend with the paint in the studio, the more you will learn. i loved this course and learned a lot about myself and my art and recommend this class to anyone looking to get into oil painting with a good mix of support and structure to help your exploration!
If you love to paint, TAKE. THIS. COURSE. It is time consuming to an extent, but so so worth it. Judith and Scott are the best! I learned so much and had so much fun.
Take it!!!!! I was so nervous going in about the time commitment and the fact that the classes are so long, but this was my favorite class of the semester! Yes, the classes got really long, but everyone was in it together, and it challenged me to paint in a way I never had before. I really loved having access to so many supplies, and I loved the community that the class built together.
