If you want to understand the United States' founding and how we can use that history in our lifetime, take this course! It is an excellent course for anyone who is searching to critically analyze the Revolution and learn what is often not told as a part of our shared narrative.
Such an amazing class. The final capstone project opportunity is so nice and so much better than a final. Students had the chance to work on some amazing, real world projects for class credit. Lectures and reading was very engaging and informative as well.
This is a great glimpse into the art of public history and the ways decisions are made around telling stories and educating young people. There was not a tremendous amount of fact content in the usual sense, but a lot to be gleaned if you stayed keyed in and participated in discussions. The readings and assignments all felt worth their time.
Unfortunately, this class (with Professor Kamensky) will no longer be offered. It was a great class, and I'm sad future students won't be able to take it.
