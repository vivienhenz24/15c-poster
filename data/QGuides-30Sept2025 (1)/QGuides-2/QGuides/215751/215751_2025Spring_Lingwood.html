What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class doesn't have too many assignments from week to week with just a paper discussion every week. I think this is a class that you get what you put in. If you engage with the readings and lecturers, you'll be able to come away with a lot of cool insights into the immunology research done in the Harvard community. There is just one major assignment which is a grant–style specific aims page due at the end of the semester. This can feel a bit unapproachable if you don't have any experience with writing grants. I would start early so you can start brainstorming ideas and reading papers. You'll need to do a lot more reading than you think to land on a topic if you found a lecture particularly interesting that is a good place to start. Do not hesitate to reach out to the course director if you need more direction.
