What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
If you took AP Calc AB in Highschool this will be a breeze.
Take this class if you are confident in algebra but don't have a background in calculus. If you have a background in calculus and you are not particularly confident, I would still recommend that you take 1a over Ma. With your psets, READ THE QUESTIONS CAREFULLY. There are certain steps like writing down the goal for the problem that isn't really necessary for solving the problem, but they still require you to do. They're where most students get points off on the psets.
Do not skip section, just do not, and do your assignments the day they give them to you!
The math department really cares about the success of its students. If you put in the work and take advantage of office hours, MQC, review session, etc then you will succeed.
Be sure to go to office hours and MQC because that is the only way to really get the most out of this course.
