What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Phil 97 is a great class if you want to dive into a topic and practice understanding and forming arguments. I really enjoyed Pragmatism with Ian Martel, though it might not be taught again :(
Dr. Martel is an excellent instructor. If you are lucky enough to take this tutorial on pragmatism, you are in for a treat. I may be a little biased because I already like pragmatist philosophy, but the readings are a good selection that keep you interested. Pragmatism is deeply ingrained in Harvard history, and this unique class puts it into perspective. You can choose to focus your writing on more theoretical or more "applied" philosophy, which I liked because I do not find the latter that interesting. Either way, Dr. Martel gives feedback that constructively makes you a better philosophy writer. Discussion in class was focused on understanding the material and common objections, all of which feel well–covered by the end.
