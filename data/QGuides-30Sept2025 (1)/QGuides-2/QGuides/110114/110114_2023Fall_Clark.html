Comments
If you're looking for an extremely disciplined, committed choir, this is not necessarily the group for that. However, the community is incredible!! Every single person I've met in Collegium is so interesting, vibrant, and intelligent in a totally different way. And everyone is incredibly kind and supportive to one another, to a greater degree than I've experienced in any other group on campus.
AUDITION FOR COLLEGIUM!! Joining collegium was one of the best decisions I made since getting here and it has changed my life
If you want to sing in college, audition for the harvard choruses. SO welcoming, rewarding, and fun.
Collegium will have a special place in your heart. Don't shy away from becoming more involved in ways like applying for the executive committee or even just helping set up chairs or going to office hours to get to know the conducting staff. The people in the choir are also extremely friendly and fun to be around. If you haven't had much exposure to singing/music before, don't feel intimidated. There will be people on the other side of where you are, but there are also people who come from your background as well, and everyone is there to learn.
Please take this course!!!!!
If you sing at all join collegium, it's the best time :)
Take this class if you are in Collegium!
