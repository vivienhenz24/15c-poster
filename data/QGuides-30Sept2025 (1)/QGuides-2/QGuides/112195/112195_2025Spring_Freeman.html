What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
The professor is funny and enthusiastic, and the workload is very light.
Loved it!!! Not too much work but is totally what you make of it. This class taught me so much about economics of discontinuous change. Not just prior discontinuous change in history, but the change that is happening all around us. Made me think so much about the world around me and loved the class experience. Was also so fun to do a specialized research study. Meet and speak with professor freeman as much as you can. He is a great and very knowledgable guy.
If you are interested in exploring intersections of economic ideas and statistical and computer science methods, it is a great course. Start thinking about the paper early!
I would suggest future students to read a lot of relevant articles/publications/books during the course, this might help understand the concepts covered in the course better.
Id recommend this course as a lighter workload one! The take home midterm and final can be pretty long though (the midterm especially), so make sure to budget enough time for that. It helps to have lecture notes that you can look through for the midterm. Prof Freeman is super helpful and accessible for support on the paper so be sure to reach out to him for that.
