Great class! Not too much work, but very interesting readings. Spend time doing them so that you can engage in class discussions, I promise that it will be worth it! Professor Fernandez is amazing. They provide great feedback on assignments and are available to meet outside of class to discuss anything and everything. Take this class!
This class was a really interesting combination of science fiction and theory. Lectures were straightforward and highlighted key concepts well. Major assignments were interesting and flexible. I got a lot from this class that I don't think I could have gotten from other classes – super unique course. Professor Fernandez is responsive and knowledgeable. The theory can be a little dense, but it's easy to ask questions in class. Response assignments aren't due until after lecture, so you'll have the chance to clarify questions from the readings before completing them.
This class is very important and not only should social studies majors or cs majors take this course given the name of the class, but every single student at Harvard. It is such an important topic and these discussions need to be had.
This is the best class you could possibly take if you want to satisfy the Arts and Humanities divisional distribution and you're somewhat interested in tech, especially AI
