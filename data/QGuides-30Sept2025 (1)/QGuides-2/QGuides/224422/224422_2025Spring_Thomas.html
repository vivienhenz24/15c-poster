Comments
This was my second time taking a class with Louisa, and I can say that she is undoubtedly one of the most empathetic and fantastic instructors I have had the opportunity to learn from in my time at Harvard. The thoughtfulness, care, and structure she brought to the class were excellent, as was her selection of students, which made me look forward to coming to class each week so much. Our readings were engaging and very accessible and I come away from this semester having learnt so much and genuinely grown so so much as both a reader and writer. Can't recommend this class enough to anyone considering!
Louisa Thomas is great! The readings for this class were amazing. I love this style of writing and taking this class really brought me more in tune with the kind of writing I hope to pursue one day. This class is at its best when looked at through a preprofessional lens. If you are new to writing and just trying it out, maybe a different workshop would be better for you. The best classes were when we really got into the weeds with the super specific, rigorous analysis of pieces. Not everyone is interested in that, and that's okay, but take a different class.
Louisa is an INCREDIBLE instructor. One of the smartest, kindest, most thoughtful I've had in four years at Harvard. She brings not only a wealth of experience as a professional journalist from the New Yorker, but a keen, tasteful eye for prose, style, and structure. Her feedback was consistently sharp and constructive. If you are at all interested in nonfiction/magazine writing, this class is for you.
