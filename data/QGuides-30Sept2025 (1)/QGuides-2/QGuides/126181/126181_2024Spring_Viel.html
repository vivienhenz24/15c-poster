It does require a lot time outside of the class, but it is time that just flies by and is so much fun to do as you work on something of your own!
Take the class! If you are even remotely interested in the class, you won't regret it!
It is definitely a time commitment, especially for the final project if you want to make something you'll be proud of so plan accordingly! If you devote proper time to this class, it's an unforgettable / once in a lifetime academic experience. Ruth, Alain, and Michael and super supportive, flexible, and give you ample space to express your creativity.
If you like animation take it. It's sometimes a lot of work to finish an animation but it's really fun to watch your movies come to live. It's mostly learning my doing/making videos and receiving feedback, but you'll also be introduced to many programs/techniques you can use to animate. It's really cool to also see what your classmates are making. Loved this class.
