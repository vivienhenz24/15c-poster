I particularly enjoyed the walkthrough of the U.S. healthcare system (specifically how health insurance works + is bought) because it gave me an applicable understanding of how individuals engage with the system in the real world. Some may claim this seminar involves more work than others but I feel that contributes to immersing you in the content more. I am also not a fan of political theory by any means, but this course made it seem digestible and approachable without feeling overly esoteric, which I appreciated.
GREAT COURSE! The readings were at time long and a little confusing, and it can be easy to get lost in class discussions – but Gabe is an absolutely amazing professor and so supportive.
