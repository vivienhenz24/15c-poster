What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
It's rough emotionally but it's worth it, you'll learn a lot about writing craft technique and get writing done.
This class will ask you to work hard and be vulnerable, but if you put in the work you will get a lot out of it. It turns writing from something that seems totally abstract and unattainable into something that can be understood and trained. Be ready to put a lot of time into your pieces and have some incredible workshop experiences!
Take this class if it's offered again! The syllabus is a perfect blend of creative pieces and literary theory. You will read a very diverse canon of writers from Baldwin to Llosa to Edward Said.
