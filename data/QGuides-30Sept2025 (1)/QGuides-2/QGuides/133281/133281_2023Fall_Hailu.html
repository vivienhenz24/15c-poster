What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great class taught by Girma. You will learn a LOT in particle physics. Lectures zoom by in classic Girma fashion, but it will become clear after a while. Problem sets are fair and guide you through. Overall, the class manages to provide an amazing scope of topics with quite a lot of depth, without feeling overwhelming. It is a great class to take before QFT, and will also teach you many topics from 143b very quickly (not saying you should skip it though). The final projects are about researching a famous experiment, which is great for anyone doing research in high–energy experiment. Hao was a fantastic TF, both Hao and Girma are very accessible
TAKE THIS CLASS if you are remotely interested in particle physics. You will get out of this class however much you put in and will get to learn about the most modern developments in physics from both a theoretical and experimental point of view. Girma is an excellent teacher and will make you feel like you want to be a physicist every day. This is one of the most fun physics classes I have taken at Harvard.
The class provided a way to deeply engage with the material, towing a good balance between complex calculations and building intuition. The teaching staff was incredible. And the final presentations were a great way to learn more from peers.
