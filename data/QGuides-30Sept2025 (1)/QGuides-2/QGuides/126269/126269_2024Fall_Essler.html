Comments
Its a great survey on the foundational/recent past work in microbiology (focused almost exclusively on bacteria)
This class is a good review of pathogenesis and a deeper dive into specific microbiology concepts that may be applicable to any microbiology PhD. The class provides a variety of perspectives and microbes, so there is something for everyone. You should have a basic understanding of microbiology prior to the class, as this is more advanced paper reading.
It's a fun upper level course if you like discussing primary literature and engaging with bacteriology/immunology
