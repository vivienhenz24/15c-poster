Prof Patton is really awesome, hearing her speak about this topic which she is so passionate about was super fun. The subject matter overall is really interesting and fun to learn about. It also comes up in everyday life way more than I expected.
I won't lie and say that this is an easy First–year seminar, but it is definitely an interesting one. There is a significant amount of reading per week for a seminar (100–200 pages) but only one short discussion post. The content is incredibly interesting, and Prof. Patton is passionate and knowledgeable about the topics, so you will definitely learn a lot about mythical creatures. If you are interested in the content and can commit to the reading, then you will get a lot out of the course. I, personally, devoted time to doing all of the reading and found the course very rewarding, but I also know that there were some people who only read enough of the reading to do the discussion posts, and so didn't get very much from the course. This is definitely a course where you will get out what you put in.
Best class and best professor I have had so far, and the workload is actually fine once youre into it.
This class was amazing! I learned so much about the magical world and creatures around us, and I think about the creatures we studied all of the time now. It's super interesting, especially for anyone interested in animals in general or myths and magic. There was a decent amount of readings but they were all enlightening. Prof. Patton was amazing, too incredibly kind and cared about each of us individually. I recommend this class if you're at all interested in mythology or magic!
