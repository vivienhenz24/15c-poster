What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this seminar!!! Natasha was awesome and the discussions we had in this class fundamentally deepened my understanding of how race functions in art history and the intersection between race and aesthetics. I learned so so much from this class and it was a fulfilling academic experience.
Be prepared to engage meaningfully with very dense art historical scholarship paired with contemporary discourse and scholarship. This course will make you a better art historian, give you a better sense of art history's stakes, and is applicable far beyond black art as you will be able to understand a multitude of art objects through a deep orientation with a specific scholarly lens. You will leave this course with a deep understanding of the history of race as an aesthetic construct, as well as with a better grasp of a plethora of methodological approaches to art history that expand upon the foundation built through intro art history courses.
