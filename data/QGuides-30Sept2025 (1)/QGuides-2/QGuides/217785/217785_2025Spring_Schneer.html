What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great class, even if you have already taken a data science class and recognize some of the techniques. There will definitely be stuff you haven't seen, and seeing how it is applied in the world of campaigning/politics is interesting. Not a huge workload, allows you to focus on stuff you find interesting, especially in the final project.
This is an excellent course for anyone interested in how data science can be applied to real–world political challenges. That said, its not for complete beginnersif you have no prior coding experience, especially in R, the learning curve can feel quite steep. You start with the basics in the first lab, but by the end of the semester you're expected to work with advanced tools. The course is rich in real data and grounded in political applications. I was genuinely impressed by Professor Schneers thoughtfully designed materials. Hes approachable, supportive, and brings clarity to complex ideasjust like the TA.

This class is worth it ;)
