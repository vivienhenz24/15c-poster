There are also a lot of resources available to you. The TFs and Prof. Bruich are very friendly, a lot of people take it so it can be quite collaborative, and review sessions and practice exam questions (very similar to actual exam material) are also offered.

Overall a great Ec elective for anyone interested in gaining more technical skills and learning about social issues.
