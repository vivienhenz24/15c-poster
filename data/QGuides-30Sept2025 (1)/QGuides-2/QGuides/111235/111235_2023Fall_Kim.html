A massive jump from 120b. Hwanhee Kim is a fun lecturer, but prepared for the amount of work that this class has. Grading is also much harsher for homework assignments. But, it was my favorite class this semester, since Hwanhee really wants us to learn, and you get close with your classmates quick because the class is small.
Great instructors but the course structure wasn't great
This class is a lot of fun, but assignments really stack up near the end of the year. The rest of the class is manageable, and as long as you complete all of the assignments it should be easy enough to keep up. Section is much harder than regular lecture, but definitely rewarding! Grading is reasonable.
Unless you are a native speaker of have been studying Korean prior to college, you need to do a lot of outside work to keep up with your classmates. But it's worth it.
