What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is challenging but productive. The weekly write–ups will keep you on track. Discussion can sometimes be overwhelming but it's a rare chance to learn from peers in different fields in a class that I think is very novel.
This class has expanded my understanding of "environmental history" by asking the very simple question: what was environment in the past, especially the pre–modern era? This has allowed us to consider as a classroom both today's environmental conundrums but also the very different demands of ancient and early modern environments and the variety of human adaptations to them. This has been a great opportunity to learn from peers across the Humanities and Social Sciences at Harvard, and I am immensely grateful for it.
