I loved this class!! Eleanor is such a wonderful professor who truly prioritized providing space for us students to grapple with the readings of the course. As an Asian student, this course truly changed how I view the world and my own life – I cant recommend it enough. Chelsea is also a lovely TF and offers such spectacular feedback (to the point of reading my entire essay and offering feedback!). The readings are so fascinating in this class, and the assignments arent difficult at all. If you have space in your schedule and especially if you are an AAPI student, you should try to take this class!
Take this class! It is an awesome introduction to Asian American and Pacific Islander studies. There are assigned readings for each class which definitely need to be completed since the class is centered around group conversations about the readings. However, I found all the readings super interesting and found that I learned so much from this course as well as from my classmates as well! Professor Craig facilitates conversation so well and is extremely supportive!
Take this class!!! The readings are wonderful and Eleanor is a wonderful lecturer. The class is very student–oriented but the gradings on the essays could be a little harsh. I recommend going to office hours for them.
I know that this will be Professor Craig's last time teaching this course at Harvard, but overall very good course! I think this course shines through the content itself and the multitude of readings offered. I felt like at times discussions felt somewhat off or stagnated, but overall very fun class and the guest speakers who came in were my highlights of it! Also very chill course overall, and this course will be missed.
Loved it! So sad Eleanor is leaving but both they + Chelsea were so amazing. Really learned so much about myself + my identity + pacific islanders that has greatly changed the way I think about myself + our larger world.
