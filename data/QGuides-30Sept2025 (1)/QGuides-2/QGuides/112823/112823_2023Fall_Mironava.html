What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is one of my favorite classes I've taken at Harvard! The instructors are amazing and passionate about the material and you learn so much even in a short amount of time.
It is possible to go into intermediate Russian without taking beginning Russian. I enjoyed Ba since the professors care a lot about the students and the structure of the course is good. You also get pretty close to your classmates since it's a smaller group.
This course is a lot of time and work, but if you're interested in learning Russian, its definitely worth taking. The course ensures that you're engaged with the language every day, which is really important in the language–learning process.
As someone who took the intensive Russian A program, I would recommend this program as a continuation. It gives you more time to digest vocabulary and re–visit topics you thought you learned but just barely scraped the surface of.
