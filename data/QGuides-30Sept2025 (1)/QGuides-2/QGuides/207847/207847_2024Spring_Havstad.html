What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Incredible course that impacted me, and will continue to impact me for a very long time. Professor Havstad is amazing.
Please take this class! I really enjoyed the readings and the discussions for each week. Lilly is also a WONDERFUL professor who really makes class an open and collaborative space for everyone. You're going to have some of the most thoughtful discussions about student activism and also your role as a student at an institution in the larger higher education world. I especially learned about oral histories and you get to incorporate that into your final project, which I find meaningful.
This is a fabulous course for anyone looking to learn about student activism. It is relatively easy, the expectations on assignments are very clear, the professor provides prompt and useful feedback, and the materials are interesting to read. All of these things are rare at Harvard, so I would highly recommend this course.
