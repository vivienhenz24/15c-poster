Comments
This is a rigorous class and you will learn a lot. Professor Boylston is endlessly knowledgable and has an inspiring talent for communicating tough ideas. He is great at working on several themes and questions throughout the semester without limiting any outside interests. I would recommend you be willing to involve yourself and definitely take this class if you are interested in diving deeper into Sufi literature and theology.
It's a ton of reading but Dr. Boylston is great at guiding students through the material and how to tackle it strategically. This class has been the most rewarding and validating as a graduate level course, and I learned a great deal about the development of classical Persian literature.
