Definitely take this course if you are at all interested in criminal law! Do all the required readings – even if we don't talk to them in class, they are super interesting and relevant to the midterm exam. Make sure you know the key claims of the philosophers and stay up to date on the jargon/definitions of legal terms because it is fast paced.
