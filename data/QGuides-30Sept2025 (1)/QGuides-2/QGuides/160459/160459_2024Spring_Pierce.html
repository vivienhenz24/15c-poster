TAKE ANY VARIATION OF THIS COURSE! I know that Dr. Pierce will offer different versions in the future as she has in the past. I was fortunate enough to take Turtles and this was my favorite class I have taken at harvard by a long shot. Dr. Pierce and Catie both were so helpful, nurturing and attentive. This is a smaller, student led, hands on course which is perfect to really learn and go in depth on a subject. The assignments are very do able, there are no exams and the structure ENSURES your success. It is a no brainer no matter what department t not try and take one of these courses.
This class is so enjoyable. You will rarely have such an incredible opportunity at Harvard to dive so deeply into a subject with such a small group. The guest speakers are experts in the field, and throughout the semester you learn more about becoming a well–rounded scientist (the two larger assignments are a student lecture and a scientific poster) than you will about turtles, rodents, cats, or whatever the next one will be.
Take this course, if you are lucky enough to have it offered! Stephanie also plans to run similar courses covering different animals, so be on the lookout for those! This course, in addition to learning about the animal, also teaches you some important professional skills like presenting an hour–long slideshow on a chosen topic and how to make a scientific poster. These are valuable skills. Keep in mind that the student–run presentation and poster are very high in point value and can be graded somewhat critically.
