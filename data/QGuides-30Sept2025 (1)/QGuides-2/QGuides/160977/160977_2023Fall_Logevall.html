Take this class!! A great intro to HKS courses for those interested in IR –– Logevall is great, and while the class can seem a bit intimidating you ultimately learn so much from your peers as well as the Professor and readings, etc.
Logevall is excellent, and this course is thought–provoking but chill. Incredibly discussion–heavy, which can be good and bad.
Professor Logevall and the teaching staff are very helpful and knowledgeable about the topics presented. The homework contains a fair amount of reading but is doable and interesting. **
Truly one of the best classes I have taken at Harvard so far. Logevall is truly amazing and you also get to learn so much from mid career students. Grade–wise, it will be tough because the final paper accounts for 50 % of your grade.
As an undergrad senior, this is probably my favorite class I've taken at Harvard. If you engage heavily with the readings and class discussions, it can be extremely worthwhile. There is very little stress though, so this is perfect as a mid–tier investment class.
Readings are great. During the lecture, we just discuss the topic, so it's not a traditional lecture if you're looking for that. It is the Kennedy School though, so your classmates will be highly experienced and very interesting to hear from. Assignments are not particularly difficult.
I left with a much stronger understanding of not only foreign affairs but also of my classmates. This is a great class to get to know HKS students and other college students while learning really interesting subject material.
I would tell future students to 100% take this course. It's so interesting and such an amazing opportunity to learn from Professor Logevall but also experience a graduate school type of course as an undergrad.
