Comments
TAKE IT! It is what you make of it.
Please take this class. Certainly one of my favorite classes I have taken at Harvard – it challenged me in all the right ways. Become friends with your peers and Vijay and the TF.
Do the class and challenge yourself to stretch. Know what things you want to expand on.
This course completely changed my life in the best way by connecting me to a group of incredible musicians and collaborators and giving me confidence in my instincts and abilities as a songwriter. Coming away from this class, I feel so much more empowered to continue to write and share my music, and way more in tune with the necessity of collaboration and ensemble work in music making! I learned how to collaborate better and how to arrange tunes, but mostly, I got the opportunity to work with and also listen to the work of some of the most wonderful musicians and people I've ever met. You're really encouraged to show up and give your whole self and do your best, but you get to do so on your own terms, and pour your energy and receive support to execute the musical projects and visions that you have which is just so lucky and unique. Truly was a life–bettering course that I'm so grateful for. Enroll if you can!!
This is an incredible class if you're looking to explore your music making in a supportive and encouraging environment with a group of artists from many different musical backgrounds. It is what you make of it. Come to class with an open mind, listen to every group as much as your own, and learn from each of your peers, because they are all wonderful! This is a very special class.
Your experience in this course will highly depend on how dedicated your (assigned) group members are. Please do not apply for this class if you are not willing to devote time to the composing and rehearsal processes and prioritize this class equally to other commitments. People who took this as a gem/easy A soured the experience for me and others in the class who were more devoted
Take this class if you are looking for musical collaborators!
