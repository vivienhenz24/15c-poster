Comments
Professor Piccolo is a delight! He generates excitement about the topics and creates a really open learning space where students help lead the discussions. We're able to learn a lot from each other in this course –– especially because students have the opportunity to teach the class for one week. You get to learn about different mental illnesses of focus each week; the class emphasizes some underlying neuroscience, but there's also a lot of discussion about wider social connections and human experiences. The larger assignments will take a good amount of effort, but they're paced out well enough that by finals period, you'll likely have nothing to worry about. A very feasible and enjoyable class!
Mayron is a wonderful professor who cares so deeply about his students! Class was great, super engaging and assignments were fun but easy. Learned a lot!
