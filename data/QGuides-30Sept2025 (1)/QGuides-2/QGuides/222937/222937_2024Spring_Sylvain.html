Take this class! It is wonderful. The readings are really interesting, and the professor is amazing. One of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard.
Take it! Pretty easy, not too much work. Readings can be dense, but they are really interesting! Very fun and interesting discussions.
Prof. Sylvain is extremely knowledgeable and wants you to learn. Weekly readings can be quite long and heavy at times, but he breaks it down in class with really interesting points. I would definitely recommend this class.
This course is very good at covering topics from all angles––regarding geography, cultural implications (society, law, etc), religions, and representations (historically, literarily). Don't expect to just watch cool supernatural/sci fi movies, though there is a fair amount of that, but also understand broader histories related to them
I would not recommend this class. I liked some of the fiction we read (such as The Crucible and The Island of Dr. Moreau), but I don't think I gained much from reading it in this class setting. The historical context was taught in a confusing way. The discussions were mostly not stimulating. The professor had an agenda and wanted us to reach certain conclusions in a sometimes very forced way.
