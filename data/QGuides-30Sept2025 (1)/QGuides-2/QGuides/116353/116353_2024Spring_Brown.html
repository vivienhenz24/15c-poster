I enjoyed the topic of the class. I do want to emphasize that if you are looking for a class with minimal readings and day to day homework, this might be the class for you. However, if you are looking for a class with a good amount of interesting and engaging readings that prompt different opinions and open discussions, like I was, another expos might be a better class to take. This also might be an effect of the section I was in as people didn't often talk and I felt like I only heard the voices of a few people. Dr. Brown is super nice and gives great feedback, but it felt as if he were lecturing to us about specific texts rather than prompting discussion (once again, something that might be a product of who was in the class).
While the class and the topic are moderately interesting, it is DR. BROWN that makes this course STELLAR. He is truly one of the most amazing teachers and the most genuine teacher I have ever had! His feedback is very helpful so make sure to really revise and study that feedback well. This is a great class, don't be late and don't fall asleep!
take it! dr. brown is an amazing preceptor who provides incredibly helpful feedback during conferences. he really cares about the topic and is always open to help. material is interesting and i definitely feel that my writing improved.
Dr. Brown IS THE BEST! GOAT GOAT GOAT GOAT. I don't think Wastelands will be running again. However, please do take Dr. Brown's new EXPOS course. He is very interesting, engaging, and kind. His assignments and grading are more than fair and he provides extensive feedback to guide your paper to the grade that you wish to achieve. As someone who thought they'd hate EXPOS, I personally loved Wastelands. Although the course name makes it sound like landfills and nuclear disaster sites, you can really adapt the term "Wasteland" to whatever piques your interest :)
its expos! so its a little dull. but professor brown is really really nice, and its a gem. not life changing, but good as far as expos goes–––which is saying a lot, considering that prof brown has to make the most of a boring structured requirement course.
Dr. Brown is amazing! Readings and workload were reasonable and he provides excellent feedback. Would strongly recommend his section!

This course challenged me to look past stigma.
