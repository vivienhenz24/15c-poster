Based on my experience, I felt like the expos studio sequences are a good at helping students transition into college level writing. Pat was awesome and very understanding. I sometimes felt like some of the feedback that she made was unnecessary, but as I look back at the work that I have done over the semester, it is safe to say that Pat helped make me a better writer. It can be a bit time consuming though so start you assignment early. If you are thinking between expos 20 and expos studio 20 I would say do studio 20 because it allows professors to really get to know you and provide personalized feedback. It also creates a community within the class.
You need to do the readings, but make sure that the reading is important for the class, because some of the readings do not matter at all. Just make sure to skim them.

Take this class! Taking the Expos Studio sequence was one of the best decisions I made for classes this year! It significantly helped me develop my writing process and understanding of how to effectively communicate my ideas using adequate evidence, analysis, and counterarguments, as well as how to effectively implement topic sentences and transitions. The exercises that we complete throughout the class are also very helpful, and the focused feedback you receive significantly helps you understand what individual improvements to make.
