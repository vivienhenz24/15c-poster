Take this class! This is likely one of the more challenging Expos 20 classes but is incredibly rewarding if you are willing to put in an extra hour or two a week. Not only will you develop your skills in academic writing, but will get a comprehensive overview of privacy and surveillance. Dr. Sinnott is outstanding and very supportive as well. Unit 1 focuses on privacy theory, Unit 2 focuses on governmental surveillance conducted by the NSA (quite fascinating!), and Unit 3 focuses on surveillance conducted by private entities. All readings are very current ––– discussions and essays are on cutting–edge topics regarding privacy policy. It is quite hard to find a more interesting Expos 20 topic than this, especially if you are mildly interested in government / policy / law.

I also think the topic might not be interesting to some, so definitely consider that.
