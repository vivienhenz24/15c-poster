TAKE THIS EXPOS. Or any Expos Ian teaches. He is hands down the best writing teacher I have ever had. He is so funny and fun to have class with. I found the entire class block passing without having thought about the time once, because Ian manages to make topics that are often dry interesting. We watch a lot of movies/video clips, which helps. We also spend the first ~20 minutes of class answering an "attendance question," which is an icebreaker–type question that allowed me to get to know the other people in my class and just have fun sharing anecdotes for the first part of class. My writing has improved so much over the semester, especially because of Ian's feedback, which is both detailed and incredibly helpful. Ian is very clear about his expectations and grades fairly according to them. This was genuinely such a good experience and I would recommend it to anyone.
TAKE THIS CLASS if you are at all interested in cons, deception, start–up culture, social media, etc. It is such a modern course that examines so much of what we see in our lives, and especially on social media, every single day. Ian is an amazing teacher. He is ridiculously funny, always makes class fun, and genuinely cares and checks in on students. Definitely make use of the one–on–one conferences with Ian, review the comments he leaves on drafts ahead of time, and go into conference with questions about your paper. It is extremely effective, and Ian is really clear in explaining the purpose of each paper.

The workload of this course is very very manageable. We read one book, magazine articles, and watch a few movies. The workload definitely picks up the weeks that papers are due, but as long as you make good use of your conference and start your papers early, you will be fine.

I had an amazing time in this course –– highly recommend!
