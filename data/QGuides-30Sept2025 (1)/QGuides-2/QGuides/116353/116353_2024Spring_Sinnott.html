I loved this course. I do not recommend it if you are not interested in the course topic. Some of the material is dry but requires engaged reading to understand and write an interesting essay about it. Likewise, do not expect an easy A. Dr. Sinnott is definitely a fair grader and will have a very high standard for a full A on a paper. I think the class is worth taking for anyone interested in national security, privacy rights, and data use on the internet.
Professor Sinnott is a really great preceptor. She is excellent at moderating discussions and generating interest, and she is so so knowledgeable about the topic of privacy and surveillance. She is also really helpful in conferences and office hours, so make sure to go before essays even if it is not required. I genuinely feel like my writing has improved drastically while taking this course. I have heard that she grades more harshly than other preceptors, but it is definitely not impossible to get an A from her. And this is your one chance to just take a course to focus on improving your writing, so it is perhaps not a bad thing to be held to a higher standard and work to meet it. The one thing to consider before taking this course is whether you're truly interested in privacy and surveillance, especially in the context of America. A lot of the readings, especially in the second unit on the American government's surveillance, were really dense, heavy stuff on specific surveillance laws. This might be boring if you are not super into the subject matter. The first and especially third unit give you more room to explore other aspects of privacy and surveillance, but just think about whether you are interested enough in privacy law and security to spend a term writing about it.
I recommend this Expos!! Look, this is not one of the Gemmy versions of the course where youll get away with anything. Dr. Sinnott cares deeply about your writing and will work with you to ensure you submit a final draft that fully elucidates your ideas in a way that is organized and clear. That takes work! But it is worth it and you will be a better writer because of that.
The topic is very interesting and relevant and discussions are great. Readings are SUPER manageable and in my opinion are fascinating! Overall, take this if youre willing to put in the time on your essays and want a genuinely thoughtful and caring preceptor AND a great topic!!
Take this class! Dr. Sinnott is so kind, very willing to meet and very helpful. I don't know how the grading compares to other expos classes, but it was definitely fair. Once again, if you are interested in the subject, TAKE THIS CLASS
This class is very well run, but you truly must read the material. Dr. Sinnott is very kind and considerate but consider that not all comments will be as constructive as the grade will be low
This course can be very interesting to take and helps you learn a lot about government surveillance. However, there is not a lot of freedom in this course. I felt very restricted with the topics I would write (even in Unit 3 when we could "pick), the writing style (Dr. Sinnott has a particular style she wants and grades harshly otherwise, sometimes even the ideas expressed. This class was more frustrating and difficult than it needed to be.
