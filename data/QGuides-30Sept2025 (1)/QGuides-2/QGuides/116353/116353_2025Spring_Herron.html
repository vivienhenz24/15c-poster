If you are interested in the topic, definitely take this expos. The class isn't a gem in terms of workload but isn't difficult either (~30 page readings for each class, a week to work on paper drafts and revisions). Professor Herron is excellent at facilitating discussions and providing feedback on assignments. He is approachable and constantly open to criticism. Make sure to take advantage of one–on–one conferences for every assignment.
Dr. Herron is very passionate about education. Ask questions and read the news: current events make the class way more relevant.
Please take this class. You will gain so much from the readings, which are not strenuous at all and you go over them during class. Furthermore, Professor Herron gives such valuable and constructive criticism that applies to papers you write beyond this class.
This is a great Expos! Very interesting topic.
If you want to engage with your expos class, in discussions and one–on–ones with the professor, this should definitely be your first choice. Not only is every class interesting and effective in its goals as set up at the beginning, but the content is intriguing and relevant. It also helps the Professor Herron is one of the best professors at Harvard.
TAKE THIS CLASS! If you are at all interested in wealth inequality, the ruling class, the education system, robber barons, elites, etc., you will love it. Dr. Herron is a fantastic professor and the discussions are really interesting. It is worth it to invest/annotate the reading so you can fully participate in discussions, but annotations are not a required assignment. The essay drafts are a great way to have an environment of less pressure since they are ungraded. Also the conferences with Dr. Herron are very helpful. I highly highly recommend.
Great class – clear and helpful feedback, nice grading, and innovative use of the not–so–fast EXPOS bot. Must take if you are interested in social science!
Take this Expos!! This class will change your perspective on how you see Harvard and the world! I think this Expos is unique in that you will be able to engage in super interesting materials and content under Professor Herron who has immense knowledge on the subject, and you'll have the opportunity to delve into social sciences research and work along with improving as a writer. The workload is very manageable, and I'd recommend taking advantage of feedback on drafts. Highly recommend this class!
