What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this course with Professor Parson!!!! He is truly one of the kindest, most empathetic people I have met on this campus. He makes everyone feel welcome and guides the course and in–class conversations perfectly. The draft feedback was very useful, and he truly cares about you succeeding. 110% would choose Expos 20 Studio over any non––studio version of this course.
Definitely take Expos Studios 20 if you are looking for a slow paced, attention focused writing course.
If you are unsure on whether you have a very strong writing background, definitely take it. However, take into consideration your possible major, I think it is a great class, however, there are other courses I would have liked to take too, instead of taking Expos two semesters. If you do not mind, then definitely take it. Sections are small so you get the chance to get close to other students and I think you can form friendships from it too, I still talk to my friends from Expos Studio 10, which makes me not regret anything from the experience. If you are choosing between Studio 20 or regular 20 (if you already took Studio 10), I think definitely choose Studio 20, you get more feedback and more time to really perfect your essays for the same writing credit.
