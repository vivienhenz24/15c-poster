This class is incredible! The readings are interesting and a good mix of planning works/creative works, and Prof. Carvalho is the best. He is clearly passionate about the material and leads discussions in a way that gets students engaged and thinking critically. The assignments are also very chill.
This is a great class, and the topic is so broad you could easily take it three times and never repeat a reading. Bruno is an excellent lecturer and facilitator, and the workload is very manageable.
