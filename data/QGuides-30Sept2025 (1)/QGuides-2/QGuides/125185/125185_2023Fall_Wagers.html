What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Very useful and well–organized course if interested in aging–related fields. Contents could be challenging but the final takeaway from this course would be very helpful for any work in related fields.
This is a great course that's been taught for many years in the SCRB department! You'll come out of it with quite a comprehensive survey of the aging field, and I think most people will learn a lot about the causes and diseases of aging – it will make you excited to learn more about the field. Dr. Wagers is a wonderful professor – she is very approachable and very clearly cares about her students. She is also very knowledgeable in the field – hardly a question that can stump her. Overall great way to fulfill a SCRB requirement, or even if you just want to learn more about aging!
This class can be tricky, but you will learn a lot and the teaching staff really does want you to succeed! If you have an interest in clinical research or biotech you will love this course. I feel I gained a lot of skills that were directly translatable to my research experience, especially in experimental design. The lectures are also very interesting and have a true clinical focus. If you want to learn about what is going on in the scientific field currently and modern research directions, take this course!!
Great class, highly recommend!
Professor Wagers is an excellent professor – learned so much from her!
This is one of the best classes I took at Harvard, and the original reason I became an HDRB concentrator. If you have an interest in aging biology or aging therapeutics, you have to take this class before you graduate. You learn a lot in an organized fashionProfessor Wagers can single–handedly do what teams of teaching staff do for larger courses!
Take this course, even if you do not have a particular interest in aging! It is very engaging and what you learn is applicable broadly beyond the field of aging. It offered me a completely new perspective on disease and how disease can potentially be treated in the future. The papers you will read are interesting and rewarding. Definitely a highlight of a course!
Great Class! Pleasant, enlightening, and very well balanced in terms of workload and educational benefit.
TAKE THIS CLASS! I am a senior with a lot of biology under my belt, which is definitely helpful for this class. I am so glad I ended up taking this class this semester Prof. Wagers is a kind and incredible professor. The topics covered in class give you a great background on an up–and–coming field of biology, and the frequent discussions of how different aging therapeutics are brought to market is very interesting. The class makeup was really fun too about 50% undergrads and 50% grad students, in an MS/MBA program. Their perspective on biology meshed with business was very valuable for class discussions. The course's assignments are also the perfect balance between meaningful and approachable... I wasn't fighting an uphill battle at any point but I was also not doing meaningless work. The final project is really fun! Prof. Wagers and the TF are super approachable and responded to my emails very quickly. :) Take it!!!
