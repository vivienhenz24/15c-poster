What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class will train you to consume a large volume of readings efficiently; provide you with an excellent foundation in historiographical analysis; and teach you a lot about the history of Eastern Europe. Professor Plokhii is phenomenal and it is worth taking the class, in addition to all the content you will learn, in order to get to know him as a scholar and person.
Definitely take this course if you want to become stronger in understanding historiography. Do not take this course if know little about the region.
This course is a tour de force of Eastern European history, from early modern times to the present. If you are studying the region, you will gain new perspectives for your research, be introduced to debates, and have the opportunity to write a thorough literature review.
If you get the opportunity to take it, dont pass it up!
