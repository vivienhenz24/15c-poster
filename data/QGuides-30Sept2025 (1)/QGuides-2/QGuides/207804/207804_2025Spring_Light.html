This was an okay class. I don't know why the reports from previous years were so positive. The class isn't bad, but it's not great. The readings felt repetitive and the discussions felt like they went in circles. A few readings were interesting, but I mostly felt checked out. Also, attendance is a way bigger deal than any other class I've taken. Not the kind of class to take as a senior like myself.
TAKE THIS CLASS. I genuinely got the most out of this class than any other class I have taken at Harvard. I'm so serious. Professor Light is so so so incredible and genuine and gives amazing feedback and advice academically, but also cares so much about her students and gets to know everyone personally. The material was all very interesting, and while you should definitely prepare to do a lot of reading, as long as you keep up, it's not incredibly difficult. Class discussions were so interesting and really expanded my perspective. I can't say enough amazing things about this class. Best part of my year!
This course was incredibly interesting, and students are often thoughtful.
TAKE THIS CLASS!! Professor Light is extremely knowledgeable and great at facilitating discussion. She generates enthusiasm for the subject as well. All of my classmates were brilliant and brought up such interesting points in class, leading me to look forward to this course every week. The reading materials for each week were also expertly chosen and extremely thought–provoking. We were given detailed feedback that helped us improve, and Professor Light is flexible with office hours.
This class is absolutely amazing! I often struggle to voice my opinions in other classes but found it much, much easier to do so in this course because of Prof Light's constant encouragement and the supportive voices of my peers. People consistently come to class prepared, so while you might need to spend more time on this class than others in order to ensure you understand the readings enough to engage, you will feel motivated to do so.
