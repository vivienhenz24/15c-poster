This course was phenomenal and Sage is one of the best professors that I have had at my time at Harvard. She is incredibly well versed and clearly an expert in the field. Throughout the course Sage showed a clear care for her students and wanted us to succeed and understood when we were having tough times. The content of the class was very interesting and Sage made the class that much better. I highly recommend this class!
Sage is a great professor and you will have a lot of fun in this class. However, sometimes the guest speakers and trips to the learning lab can make it feel as though you are not diving into the readings in depth, which makes it hard to feel like you took a lot away from the course substantively. However, this class is still a great introduction to the media and protest movements in history.
Sage is amazing and if you have the opportunity to take any class with her, take it! This class truly changed my understanding of history, and I learned a great deal even if we weren't going over history.
Come prepared to read some excellent texts and discuss challenging, but relevant, issues. It is definitely more focused on the media aspect so be prepared to think about the role of the press/TV more than the historical elements. Sage is awesome and the texts she has chosen are meaningful and eye opening.
This class is not a knowledge–based class on the issues it covers. It is more of an analytical–based class that attacks these issues in different ways. If that floats your boat, then definitely take it. Sage will also definitely improve on the course for next time.
Professor Goodwin is incredibly accommodating and friendly. She made a serious effort to bring speakers at the forefront of press research into our classroom, in–person and virtually, allowing each of us to ask them questions. She also took advantage of the Bok learning lab, which was an exciting way to convert our final papers into digital media projects. I wish we had more time to discuss the topics in class; between the assignments, speakers, and the learning lab, we had very little time to actually confront the course material, particularly towards the end. Thus, did not challenge my thinking as much as I wish it had, but still innovative, well–run course!
Take this class, Sage is a great instructor! The content is challenging since we are jumping from movement to movement each week but there is a lot of guidance on reading and writing strategies from Sage. The section will be engaging as social movements have become more popular. There is a lot to learn from this class both theoretically and practically.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!!
