What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take it.
Professor Hudson is a very kind and compassionate person and a wonderful teacher. His weekly preparation was extremely thorough, and he provided us with excellent materials, including meticulous handouts that were painstakingly composed. What impressed me so much is his obvious passion for the texts and scholarship, and the depth of his knowledge. He would often go the extra mile to research questions he couldnt answer immediately in class, and truly cared about his students opinions and took them seriously. He also facilitated excellent discussions, gave helpful feedback, and curated a very focused curriculum that was highly thorough but not overwhelming (a difficult feat!). He was also always happy to meet outside of classes, and made himself available to students in a generous manner. I dont know if this course will be taught again by Professor Hudson at Harvard, but it has my strong recommendation!
This is a fun course on a fascinating author. You will learn a great deal about Varro.
Nobody is better than Varro for understanding the literary climate of the 1st century BC, so I recommend the class to anyone interested in that period.
