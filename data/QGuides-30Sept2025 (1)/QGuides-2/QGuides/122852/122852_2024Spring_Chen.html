Make sure to give yourself plenty of time for the weekly homework assignments and be sure to attend lectures. As long ad you are engaged and willing to learn new things, you will be fine.
Dr. Chen is fantastic and very accessible! She will gladly help and her office hours are always available. I would recommend this class to anyone looking to refine their Arcgis Pro skills. I was worried about the coding component, but it didn't turn out to be too bad! Dr. Chen makes it very approachable to those with no experience, like me.
