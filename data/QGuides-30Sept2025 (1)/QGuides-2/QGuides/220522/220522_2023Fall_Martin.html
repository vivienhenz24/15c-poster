What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Loved this course! The professor is amazing and very accessible outside of class, I looked forward to discussion every week, and the assignments were all geared toward producing the final paper which made the process very manageable. In addition, you're able to write about anything related to the themes of the class for the final paper, which is helpful for exploring what you may want to write about for your thesis. I really enjoyed the readings, particularly as they were focused on more modern times towards the end. The methodology of this course is historical, which is useful for every social studies thesis and an added benefit of the course in terms of thesis prep. Overall, you leave with a great understanding of the history of economic growth and development & I strongly recommend this course!
