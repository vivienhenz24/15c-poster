What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I honestly was not sure what to expect from the class as there were no Q guides but it was honestly fascinating. The works are unique and hard to encounter anywhere else. Professor Skakov is an expert in the field and all of his comments were enlightening. If you have even the faintest interest in modernism/art, Soviet culture, understanding power and control in modern day Russia I would definitely recommend
If you are not well–versed in Slavic literature, you can certainly take this course and enjoy it, but you should know that most of your classmates will be substantially more experienced than you, and that can be frustrating. Otherwise, the readings are very interesting and worth stepping outside your comfort zone for.
