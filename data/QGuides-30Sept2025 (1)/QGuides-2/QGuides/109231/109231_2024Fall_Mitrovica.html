This class is awesome! If you find geophysics even remotely interesting, I recommend checking this class out. Content is presented in a way that is very easy to understand, even if you have no prior geophysics knowledge. Jerry also made this class one of the bright spots of my semester. At the end of the class, everyone works on a research project together, with the goal of getting it published.
take this course!!! class mostly centers around discussions, which are very engaging and thought–provoking. jerry is a fantastic professor and is very willing to chat about whatever is most interesting. on that note, while problem sets can be tough at times, you will be absolutely fine and understand everything if you go to office hours.
I learned a lot in this class and it was relatively stress–free.
This course is great! The material is extremely interesting, and Jerry is a great instructor. Your peers end up becoming a great support network, so just be kind and stay engaged :)
This is a fantastic course! I had not taken any Earth–Planetary Science classes before (but am a chemistry–physics concentrator, so have a scientific background) and found everything to be incredibly accessible. Jerry is a great professor and spending time with him is very enjoyable! I would highly recommend taking this course to anyone interested in the subject!
How all intro courses should be. Manageable workload, lots of help available, no mandatory section, and genuinely interesting lectures! Jerry and Natasha are great. Jerry's anecdotes about research and being a scientist make the class worthwhile without even considering the course content. That being said, the course content is fascinating. Highly recommend for physics/engineering/EPS students to take as a light concentration req, or for anybody who wants to expand their world view a little!
