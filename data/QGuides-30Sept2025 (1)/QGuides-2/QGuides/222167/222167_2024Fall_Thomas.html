The Thomas lab is AMAZING!! It is such a nice community. Both the PI and the mentors are fantastic and incredibly supportive. You will learn a lot not just from your mentors, but from all the other lab members. If you are interested in developmental psychology and/or social psychology this is the perfect lab for you. It is a big time commitment because you have to do 10hrs a week, but it is honestly fun work in a comfortable environment so it didnt feel like a burden at all. You will also be doing actual meaningful work and contributing to a research project beyond just being assigned busywork or trivial tasks.
The Thomas Lab is such an amazing cultural environment in which to do research. The culture Ashley cultivates is so welcoming, and all of the researchers are so kind and intelligent.
