This is a great class and Paul is an absolutely amazing professor and person. You will get a ton of feedback on your writing throughout the semester and become a much better writer and reader/critic of fiction by the end of the semester. Go into the class willing to devote a lot of time and effort into your piece of fiction even if it seems like there aren't many assignments required by the class and you will get a ton out of a semester in this workshop.
This class is amazing! Paul facilitates such an encouraging and welcoming environment. It is a great opportunity to develop as a fiction writer or get back into fiction writing in a low–stress environment. Can't recommend it enough!
I truly cannot recommend this class more. This class reignite my passion for writing, and the group of students in this class is so wonderful. Paul creates an incredibly welcoming and nonjudgmental environment that makes you excited to write, even if you havent written a single story since middle school. Start your story early!
This class is INCREDIBLE for anyone interested in fiction writing. Paul Yoon gives the best feedback and facilitates an amazing rapport in class. It really feels like a safe space to share your writing and grow.
Yes, a creative writing workshop is an experience you really have to get in at Harvard. I've bonded with a lot of people through the workshop, and there is a certain vulnerability you have when sharing a piece of your work and fiction to other students. Paul is incredibly kind and looking out for us, and I think that seeped into how we treated each other in the workshop. For this specific workshop, I think I've gained a newfound appreciation for short stories, and how they're able to focus on the really small, concrete things compared to other forms of fiction. The workload for this workshop is essentially write your own short story and choose a week to present it; on a weekly basis, you essentially read ~2 other people's stories and write them feedback. I think this is a really incredible class to round out your schedule!
THE BEST COURSE AT HARVARD!!!!!! paul is the best person ever. TAKE TAKE TAKE!!!
Absolutely take this class, it's the best course I've taken at Harvard so far. I came in with little experience in creative writing, and I feel like I've gotten so much better. Paul is a great teacher and also an amazing person.
Paul Yoon is a wonderful instructor and this is one of the best classes Ive taken at Harvard, but please dont take this course if you sit in class the whole time shopping online. Take this class if you really are interested in talking about other peoples stories, learning from their different ways of seeing the world, and becoming a better writer. Too many people get denied from this class for someone to sit and shop online (or read emails, etc.) the whole time. In fact, I recommend printing the stories so you dont have to be on a computer at all.
