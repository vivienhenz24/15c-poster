The course content is very interesting but felt a bit dry at times. But I loved the assignments, a lot of room to explore things that interest you: e.g. we had to go watch a sports event/live performance and write about it! also the quizzes were pretty fair, i would not label them as quizzes though that was a bit misleading on the first one, it's definitely an exam and you have to prepare a decent amount. If you take notes in lecture and skim the abstracts of the readings then you are set. I would highly recommend the class, there is no section and most of the assignments are open to write about whatever you please! The professor is also super kind and willing to answer any questions you may have and give helpful suggestions on the assignments.
