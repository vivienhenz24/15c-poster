Take advantage of office hours — meet with Jessica and learn from her incredible experience!
Great and very chill course. Jessica is so knowledgeable and enthusiastic, and I honestly just enjoyed listening to her get excited about something new in biology or some really cool concept – she's a very interesting person to learn from. Half of the class is her teaching, and the other half is student presentations/discussions, so everything is very laid back. She really cares about the students and understands that everyone is very busy or applying to medical school, so she's super flexible. Overall, she really just seems to want students to learn and get excited about the material above anything else.
It's a great class, I highly recommend it, there are a lot of assignments but they are fun to complete.
This is a course with a light workload and if you are looking for a class in which you will likely do very well without putting in a lot of effort, this may be a good option for you. However, I don't think I learned very much in this class and I don't think that this is a class that will stick with me after I leave Harvard. Additionally, I've always felt that one of the best things to have come out of my time at Harvard to be the strong relationships I've built with my faculty over the years. I unfortunately cannot say that I have built any kind of meaningful relationship with Jessica. I would have appreciated more one–on–one office hours and openness to getting to know students.
