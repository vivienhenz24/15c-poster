This course is fairly overwhelming time–wise, with three problem sets a week and little break from these problem sets even during exam weeks. Watching videos on concepts prior to section is advised, as the course format plunges directly into problem solving when you arrive. If you haven't taken calculus before, this can make you feel tremendously lost. I wish that the workshop component of the course was taught a bit more lecture style, as students often need this introduction to be more formal. I highly recommend supplementing the teaching with Khan Academy or The Organic Chemistry Tutor, as section often is not sufficient for learning.
This class is a nice transition from high school math to college math. It is not hard, but there is a lot of work because of three PSets per week. Once you find a routine (and start going to MQC on MW) it gets way easier.
