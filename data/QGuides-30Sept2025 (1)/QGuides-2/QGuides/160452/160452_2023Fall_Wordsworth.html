This class has potential to be really good, but I would hesitate from taking it if they continue with the flipped classroom format. The material is very interesting, but it's difficult to teach yourself that material solely through videos and quizzes watched outside of class, and group assignments done in lecture. The quality of your understanding will directly depend on the quality of your group members. The final project component is actually really interesting, but it's a slightly difficult jump to go from learning about satellite engineering concepts in videos to proposing your own satellite mission, so I would say to take this course if you have significant interest and background in space. Guest lectures were really great.
