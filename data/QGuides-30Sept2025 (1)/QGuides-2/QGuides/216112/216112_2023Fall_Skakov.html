What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
TAKE IT!!! I am not exactly sure if this class will be offered next year, but if it will, take it for sure if you get it as your seminar placement. It was such a fun course, and I enjoyed every second of it. It really changed the way I view art, so if you care about (avant–garde) art even the slightest bit, I highly recommend it. Professor Skakov is absolutely amazing. His teaching style is very interesting, and he is clearly so passionate about the material. He personally knows a lot of important artists in the contemporary art scene, so it is very cool to hear more about it whenever he mentions them. Overall, my favorite course here so far by a huge margin!
This is an incredibly nourishing class for both intellectual needs and personal needs as the professor is very passionate for the world and art.
Come in with an empty mind. Whatever you think you know, leave it at the door. Ask questions, build on the foundation of the material presented to you, and learn from others before you shoot down what they're trying to tell you. Professor Skakov is a brilliant professor, if you listen. Speak with confidence and always always be prepared. Academic pursuits that aren't graded on a scale and are scrutinized are the purest form of learning. Don't abuse that, take full advantage of the growth that's here. Create and push yourself to start to look at things differently. And, have fun with it.
