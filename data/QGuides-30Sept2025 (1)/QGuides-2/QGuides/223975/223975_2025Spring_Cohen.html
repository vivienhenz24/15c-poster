This class is so engaging! Jessica and Marema clearly really care about discussing topics that matter to students, and there is a great diversity of maternal and reproductive health topics that they cover. This class also felt very timely considering all the funding cuts under way. The assignments were also different from the ones you usually get in classes here, which was rather refreshing.
The guest speakers were INCREDIBLE. I so recommend even just for that! Jessica and Marema created such a wonderful classroom community. While sometimes lectures could be a little dull (as a pre–med student, I found the more economics lens to be less engaging), the assignments were wonderful and really allowed me to dive deeper into issues I cared about. I would highly recommend for a great community, stress–free way to fulfill a Social Sciences distribution, and as a wonderful holistic introduction to maternal and reproductive health. We took a global and broad lens that definitely informed my way of thinking – and the community and guest speakers were, again, incredible.

One of the best courses I've taken ever! We learned a lot of maternal health in terms of global health and health policy. Jessica is a great lecturer and kept things interesting by bringing in amazing guest speakers from many different fields from OBs to Health Ministers from Sierra Leone to researchers from the Public Health School, they were so relevant and fascinating to hear from. Readings each week are very manageable, very interesting and actually helps for lecture the next day. Three assignments overall, which are pretty chill and Marema (TF) will help you if you ever have questions. I highly recommend this course, it was such a fun time!
