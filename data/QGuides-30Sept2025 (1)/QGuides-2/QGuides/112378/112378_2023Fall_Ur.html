If you are interested in a unique mix of archeology, anthropology, and history, this course is perfect. You will learn so much about both intellectual and scientific methods that are used to uncover and verify historic events, items, and narratives. This class is also highly grounded in reality and society, which means many of these methods will be applied to meaningful examples, such as discussions surrounding Confederate statues, museum repatriation, etc. This course fundamentally teaches critical thinking and the ability to reason rigorously about what and how certain claims about society and the past can be found and verified. Take this class!
