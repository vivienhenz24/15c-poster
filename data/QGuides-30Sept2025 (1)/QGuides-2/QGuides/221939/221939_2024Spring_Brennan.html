TAKE THIS CLASS!!!! I don't usually write long reviews but I have to give KB and Brian their flowers. They are the absolute BEST teaching team and this class was the best I have ever taken at Harvard. They create a delightful classroom environment (warm, engaging, creative, relaxed, fun) and their focus on learning as a journey rather than a destination with set checkpoints allows you the space and freedom to fully explore your interests as you work on your semester–long project. The work load is super manageable as class only meets once a week and depending on your personal project, you choose how much time you put in. And you end up putting in the time because the work is so fun! The weekly design cases are so interesting to read and the collaborative classroom culture allows you to participate in other people's projects and be inspired by their work as well. There are no tests or exams, just the semester long project, but the course has plenty of built in check–ins and scaffolding to keep you on track. This class single–handedly made me excited to be a CS concentrator again (burnout is so real) and introduced me to the Education Studies department which I will now be taking more classes in. This is a class that just feels like what college SHOULD feel like. Literally no cons, no negatives, no complaints. Take this class then thank me later!!!
This was the best class I've taken at Harvard if you can take this class ensure that you do!! KB and Brian are so supportive and the absolute best. Plus snack cart is awesome.
You might not find a better class at Harvard than this one. It doesn’t matter if you’re majoring in CS, education, economics, philosophy, or french cooking, this course is for you. It was a true joy to show up to class every week, entering a classroom buzzing with energy from a diverse range of students so passionate and engaged you feel humbled and privileged to be a part of the action. The teaching staff are absolutely incredible, so supportive and caring and just want to help you develop and thrive on your ideas in any way they can. The workload is so unbelievably chill, the activities we do in class are super fun, and the work you and everyone else tackles in the class via a final project (which can be about pretty much anything you want and you work on throughout the entire semester) feels truly important like what you’re doing can actually help a lot of people in the world. You WILL NOT REGRET taking this course. It is a just a joyful journey the whole way. Take it, sit back, and enjoy the process.
If you get in, you should definitely take this class!! You will have a really fun time exploring CS and education in a very supportive and inspiring class environment.
This class is hands–down the best class I've taken at the College. Since the class size is small, it fosters great community and a space for real inspiration and passion. For the sake of the course's overall effectiveness, I highly recommend to only take this course if you are very passionate about CS education (or education in general) as this is the foundation of the class community and work.
You should absolutely take this course!! It is, without a doubt, the best class I have ever taken. KB and Brian are two of the best, most supportive people I have ever met. I ordinarily never take 9ams, and I looked forward to this class every week. You can do practically anything you want for the project, and I can't recommend this class enough!
The best class offered in CS at Harvard. HIGHLY recommend. The class builds an incredible community of wonderful people, and it also covers incredibly interesting topics in an engaging, exciting way. This was a class that allowed for me to continue pursuing my passions and reminded me of why I am passionate about CS education in the first place!
GUARANTEED ***A*** FOR YOUR GPA. That's entirely true, everybody I've talked to (20+ students) have gotten an A in this course. Now that I have your attention, CS196 is the EASIEST and MOST ENJOYABLE 100 level CS course you can take, and I'm sure about that. This class is an absolute GEM with a light workload and amazing teaching staff. The only real work for the entire course is the final project, but it's actually quite fun since you can decide what to do for the final project (I really mean that, students have done things only tangentially related to CS education and it was always allowed). There's only one meeting a week and it always has a cart full of free snacks + drinks (you can request which snacks you want). You should absolutely apply, no matter who you are. I'm a graduating senior and this was the BEST course I've ever taken in my ENTIRE LIFE, so hopefully that gives you an idea of how mind–blowingly great it is!
If you are at all interested in the intersection between Computer Science and Education, this class is a must. The worst part of the class is how few applicants get admitted (and surprisingly there is no preference given to seniors, which I might change to be honest). KB and Brian are absolutely amazing at their job and inspire innovative new ways of approaching and thinking about K–12 CS learning. The class community is pretty tight–knit and by the end you will be pretty familiar with everyone and their passions in the field. Each session is almost 3 hours long but it usually flies by and it's always a great time! The case studies seem to be carefully chosen and each of them offer a unique perspective on how CS learning can be designed. This is maybe one of the greatest courses that Harvard has to offer and anyone who has the privilege of being on the roster should never take that for granted.
