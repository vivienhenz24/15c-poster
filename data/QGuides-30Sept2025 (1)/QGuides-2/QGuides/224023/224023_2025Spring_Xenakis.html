Really good class for an introduction to this type of work! While I haven't taken a lot of stats or math courses, the assignments felt very approachable. Lecture can be quite technical in a stats–sense, but it is possible to keep up with what is being discussed with a bit of effort. Section helps a lot with understanding lecture and the assignments. Overall, very enjoyable and relaxed class! Dr. Xenakis is extremely understanding of people's situations and is willing to accommodate while still being highly encouraging.
This is a great introduction to bioinformatics – Id encourage future students to start thinking about a topic for the final project early on so you can get the most out of it!
TAKE THIS COURSE! Professor Xenakis is seriously amazing, and I have not met a Professor like him that really cares about his students and their learning. The contents of the class can be tricky and PSets can be difficult but Professor Xenakis will work with you and make sure that you get it done (again he actually cares and it shows which is a trait that isn't often reflected in other Professors).
This course is forgiving schedule wise, but the problem sets can be daunting. If youre not well–versed with R or struggle with concepts from Stat 110 or 111, expect to spend a lot of time at office hours.
I would highly recommend this course if you're a stat concentrator looking for an interesting elective or if you're doing the stat BCB track. James is an extremely accessible professor who is always willing to help, and he makes the class accessible regardless of your prior biology background. The final project is also a great opportunity to explore a topic you're interested in. Finally, as someone who had never used the cluster before, I really appreciated how this class taught how to use the cluster and how to use shell, as these are very useful skills I hadn't been taught before.
Great course and James is awesome. He really cares about making sure you actually understand what's going on –– a classmate & I once spent probably 4 hours in his office hours, and he patiently helped us through the material the entire time. Also such a cool and fun guy!! Very passionate about his field and has lots of fun stories whenever you chat with him. Very rewarding and cool class and I really felt like I learned new things!!!
I think this class is an absolute must for anyone studying Statistics on Bioinformatics/Biostatistics and Computational Biology track. It is a chill class (for a stats class) but you will learn a lot of interesting and useful things and bioinformatics tools. Take it with Professor Xenakis if you can! He is the best teacher ever!!!
You will get out of this class what you put into it. If you put in the time and effort, this class can really enable you to have more freedom with your learning and explore what you want to study. However, I think you should go into this class with some flexibility because it is not the most organized and assignments tend to get delayed and be changed very suddenly. But because James gives so much grace, it ends up being not stressful. Lenient grading, and a pretty chill class overall.
