First–Year FGLI:

Take this course if you get the chance to!! Professor Yoda is well–articulated, knowledgable, and understanding. I didn't get to interact with her one–on–one a lot when I took the course, but I knew she was very passionate about teaching the course. I will be frank, as a first–year I was intimidated by the amount of knowledge and ideas of discussion being thrown at me, and I never really knew anime had so many layers to it. I think there is a slow start at the beginning of the course, but overall it ramps up as the weeks go by. Analyzing anime that are outside of the box and even mainstream like Sailor Moon and Gundam was very interesting, and led to very interesting discussions.

If you have never taken a film analysis class and want to take on that is somewhat an introduction to it this would be it. If you love anime, or want to learn more about why people are obsessed with anime, take this class. If you need a class to fill your senior schedule, or fulfill your Arts GenEd, this class is perfect!
