What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course is very dense in its subject matter, but if you want to learn about physiology or are required to, this is the class to do it in. Dr. Sieber is an excellent professor who really clearly cares about the material and study learning. You will be challenged in your ways of thinking about health processes and occurrences, for the better. In terms of coursework, there are three long homework assignments across the semester and three exams (timed, open–note). The weeks of the assignments and exams were challenging, but the course is on the large enjoyable.
attend lectures IN CLASS, and try to stay focused during the lectures, it will save you a lot of time
