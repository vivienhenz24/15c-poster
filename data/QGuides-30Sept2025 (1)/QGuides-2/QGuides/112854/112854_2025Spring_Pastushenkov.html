What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is an amazing course and you should most definitely take it! Because of its smaller class size and more section–like nature, you will get plenty of practice speaking Russian in an academic context with both your professor and peers. You will also get very well acquainted with popular Russian culture and will get to explore the film, music, and literary scene from the 90s up until the present day.
Take notes when watching the videos and doing the assigned readings; it helps during discussions and final exams.
