What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is an important class that asks important questions. Beyond the academic foundations, which are crucial to the study of the African continent this course encourages us to interrogate our own positionality and complicity in relation to the best and worst aspects of the field.
Engage in the discussion as much as you can. It is the most rewarding part of the class.
To come to this class with an open mind, and ready to share your experiences if you feel inclined to.
Be open to the unlearning, humble in your inquiry, and sure in your relationship to what you study.
I would encourage them to take the course and go in ready to learn, discuss, and engage.
This course is essential to any student contemplating a secondary field or a concentration involving African studies in general.
It is an interesting class, and a nice opportunity to get familiar with some topics related to African studies.
