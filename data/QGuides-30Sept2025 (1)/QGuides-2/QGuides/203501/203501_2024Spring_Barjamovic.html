This is an AMAZING class. It's unfortunate that this will probably be the last year offered. Thisis the best class I have ever taken at Harvard. The content has been exciting, connecting various topics, including business, government, ancient history, anthropology, etc. It is incredible to take an exciting class in a low–stakes environment. We have an open dialogue with guest lecturers and get interesting, unique insights in various sectors.
This class was an absolute gem. Anyone who was able to take this class while Gojko still taught here was extremely lucky. He has no problem going back and forth with whichever guest he invited to speak to us that week. He always kept things interesting, and never put too much pressure on us. The low stakes that come along with a first year seminar allow you to just take in the information. Although this seminar may not run now the Gojko has left us for Yale, if there is one like it, I would recommend.
Best class ever
TAKE THIS CLASS!! It is an extremely interesting deep dive into a wide variety of topics: from how trade worked in Mesopotamia to the beginnings of economics to corporate fraud. Gojko is one of the best professors I've met at Harvard and I feel like I learned an incredible amount in the class even though there was very little work outside the class itself.
Gojko has left Harvard, but if he ever returned, it would behoove you to take a class with him
