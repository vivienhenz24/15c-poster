What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Dana Malhas is the BEST LANGUAGE TEACHER. I would literally do anything for her. She genuinely cares about her students and holds us to high standards, and her years of experience are well–used to keep us engaged. Huge difference between taking classes with other teachers and taking Arabic from her. ***If you are thinking of studying abroad in Jordan, this class with her is a MUST, it is the only class where you will really gain an understanding of speaking and dialect as you will hear in Jordan. I took just first year Arabic and was able to go to Amman for the summer and feel very comfortable speaking.
Dana is a phenomenal preceptor. She is more receptive to questions than any teacher I've ever had, and she takes the time to focus on what you want to get out of the language. The one note I would give is to speak in Arabic in the class frequently, as most of the practice with the language is written–––you definitely can grow a lot as a speaker, but you have to take initiative to do so, because you won't be forced to speak in Arabic super frequently.
Be ready to learn a lot and put in the work. It really is 2 hours a day but as will be the best decision you have made. Super fruitful.
