This is the best course I have taken!! Ziblatt does a great job of letting the conversation naturally flow and is very knowledgeable about the topic. It was great to have the opportunity to work on the final paper and have him refine ideas and help me produce some of my best work yet during my time at college. Having never taken a socstd class this was a great way to explore a discipline outside my concentration and YOU MUST TAKE IT if you can.
Definitely recommend this course! Professor Ziblatt is a great teacher. He's one of the top experts on democracy, and his knowledge definitely comes through in his teaching style and the readings he chooses. He guides discussion well while also leaving room for you to form your own opinions on each reading. This class feels more like a gov class than a social studies class, but that's not a bad thing. Definitely recommend lotterying for this course, especially if you're interested in democratic theory/democratic transitions or hope to write a gov–y thesis. If you're a gov student, you can also lottery for this class (there are a limited number of spots reserved for gov majors) which I also recommend!
A very good class that I would recommend, especially for those who operate well with less deliverables and more opportunity to plan when to write the essay when you want to. Professor Ziblatt is very approachable and gives good feedback.
Ziblatt is a gem!!! Take this course and meet with him. It's very theoretical, but he wants to teach you anything you ask for. Only great minds around the table tootap into the community.
This course was incredible! Professor Ziblatt is always willing to take time out of his busy schedule to meet with students, and it was awesome to discuss liberal democracy during an election year/liberal democracies experiencing stress around the world. Seminar discussions were always engaging, and we were encouraged to connect our readings with ongoing political developments. We also had several opportunities to workshop our final paper with Professor Ziblatt and our classmates, so I felt prepared throughout the process.

Professor Ziblatt is immersed in liberal democracy, inside and outside the academy. Few other professors do the sameI would encourage anyone interested in learning more about liberal democracy to take a course with him if you have the opportunity to do so!
This is a super great class, and if you have the chance to take it, you definitely should. The readings are super interesting and Prof Ziblatt facilitates incredible discussions every week. It isn't too heavy workload wise, except for the final paper at the end of the class.
