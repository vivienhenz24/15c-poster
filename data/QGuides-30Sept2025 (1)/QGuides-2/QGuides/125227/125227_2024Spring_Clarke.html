This was my favorite class I have taken at Harvard thus far (something you may hear often about Expos 40, but it's true). In January, I dreaded the day I would have to speak on stage and constantly worried about the opinions of others around me. But by coming to class with an open mind and a genuine buy–in, I developed a comfortability with public speaking that has trickled down to every part of my life, even simple interpersonal relationships. I gave speeches on topics that were at times emotional, occasionally funny, and even controversial, and learned so much from my peers' efforts. This course taught me to be confident, to try new things, to worry less about others' opinions and live in the moment, and what it takes to connect with a cohesive message and my audience. This was achieved in large part due to Kate, who was universally loved by my section; words cannot do justice to the impact she has had on my life. Her energy, honesty, approachability, and warmth, absolutely made the difference for me. I have to admit that it was a bit strange at first to start each class making strange sounds with my voice and movements with my body, but it showed me just how freeing it can be to make the most of every second and just have fun. I truly did not want this class to end, and it was an absolute joy from start to finish.
BEST CLASS EVER. If you have the chance, absolutely 100% take Expos 40. Kate unfortunately isn't teaching it next year (take Expos 45 too, she's teaching that now!) but regardless, you'll have an amazing experience with any instructor in the class and will also become very close friends with the rest of your section.
Highlight of my Harvard experience. Left with 14 new friends and a greater ability to speak in all situations such as speeches and skits etc but also interviews and conversing meaningfully with peers on difficult topics.
One of the best classes to a) build community and make deep friendships b) buy in and express your weird side and of course c) learn how to present in front of audiences. Absolute must – it should be required! Amazing class
BEST CLASS I HAVE TAKEN AT HARVARD BY A LANDSLIDE. You must take it!

This course did a remarkable job at community building. Through Expos40 I made 13 new, close friends I wouldn't have gotten to know otherwise. Also, this course helped me become a way better speaker. All the speeches and exercises were terrific at helping me practice, see, explore, and learn about "good" public speaking (I put good in quotes because it assumes that there is one way of doing public speaking well which is not necessarily true). Through expos40 I became better at telling stories and connecting with people. Expos40 uncovered a strength and passion of mine: speaking and talking to people. It also sparked a curiosity in me for comedy, theater and acting. This class does a tremendous job at helping you learn from your experience by having goal setting exercises and reflections along the way.

The class is not overwhelming at all in terms of work but what you put in is what you make out of it. It is true fun! Kate (the professor) is the best! However, I have heard great things about other section leaders too so it is worth taking.
