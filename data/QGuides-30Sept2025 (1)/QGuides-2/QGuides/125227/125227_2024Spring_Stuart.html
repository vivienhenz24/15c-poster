This was one of the best classes I've taken in my time at Harvard. You quickly become great friends with the people in the course in a very authentic way. While the speaking part is scary, it's only 3 speeches, and the course is essentially designed to be exposure therapy; by the end, giving a 10 minute speech is much less daunting than you think.

Zack is one of the most kind, understanding people I've ever met. He put in so much effort to make the class a comfortable space for everyone and was more than understanding if conflicts ever came up. I can't speak highly enough on his behalf; when I think about the greatest teachers I've had in my time here, Zack is one of them. This is an amazing senior class I would highly recommend to anyone who wants to have a great way to wrap up their time at Harvard.
TAKE THIS CLASS. I had so much fun, and it's probably one of the few courses at Harvard where you actually learn a practical skill that will help you both in work and life. I think that people's sections tend to vary by section, but I would recommend that you take it with Zach. He created such a wonderful sense of community in our section the whole class always ate breakfast together and made the classroom a judgment–free zone to experiment.
Great class! As a senior, this is a class you should take. Zach was my TF and he made the class amazing. We all got to know each other and had a lot of fun.
This course is amazing. It honestly stops feeling like a class and becomes another fun activity in your week.
I strongly encourage you to take this course if you have specific aspects in your public speech skills that you wish to improve! Your peers truly provide some role model strong features on any of the skills that you lack.
This class is a must–take!!! Coming from someone who has always hated public speaking, taking this class instilled in me a new sense of confidence and appreciation for public speaking.
This is an amazing class to take. Lottery for it if you can! One of the rare classes where you don't need to put much in but you get a lot out of it. The class environment is chill and the bulk of the work is creating your speeches which is very manageable. My public speaking didn't radically transform, but I got noticeably better and was far less nervous on stage by the end of the class. Having a safe space to practice speaking in front of an audience helped me tremendously––more than analyzing persuasive techniques or reading about speaking tips, which we all generally already know. Zach was a great instructor. Super friendly and engaging; highlights your strengths and gives good feedback for improvement; makes a 9 am class feel lively and something you don't mind waking up for.
This was a fantastic course! Would absolutely recommend, especially with Zach.
