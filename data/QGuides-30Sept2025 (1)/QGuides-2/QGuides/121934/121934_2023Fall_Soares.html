Marcia is the absolute best and if you have taken a previous Port. class with her then you should already know how engaging she is. This course personally felt like it had a couple more interactive assignments that were fun to work on. The speakers they have for this class are amazing and interesting as well. Marcia is a fantastic teacher, and this class is so much fun! You will learn how to speak Portuguese while learning about and engaging directly with the Lusophone world. Highly recommended.
Marcia is an extremely enthusiastic and friendly instructor who obviously cares deeply about the topics discussed in the class and the learning of the students in the class. The themes discussed in the class are interesting and relevant for people interested in learning about societal issues in Portuguese–speaking nations as well as what communities are doing to try to foster change. There are a variety of different and slightly numerous assignments throughout the course, but the workload is by no means overwhelming. We also received visits from people working on issues we discussed in class, which was very cool and engaging. I would highly recommend taking this course if you are at all interested! And if you are not, it is an opportunity to learn a lot of new things from a great instructor!
