Professor Gordon is a very welcoming person that is genuinely passionate about & exceptionally well recognized his field of research, which is Japanese labor history. It makes discussions in lecture and office hours extra engaging, and I feel that I genuinely learned more in Hist 1026 than any other class I've taken at Harvard thus far. Highly recommend.
