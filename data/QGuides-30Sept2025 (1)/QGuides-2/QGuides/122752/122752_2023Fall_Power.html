This course is somewhat misleading. While there are roughly 4 written assignments dispersed throughout the semester, there will be much more work entailed since you are expected to continually revise your assignments throughout the semester. On top of that, there are regular problem sets that are run through the general Ec 970 class, and you will probably need to attend Office Hours to complete those. The final paper is also looming over your head, but there is little preparation until the semester's end. Just keep in mind that there are a lot of moving parts to this course that you need to be on top of.
