Professor Johnson King is a great teacher. I learned a lot and this course was also a lot of fun. There are a decent number of assignments (weekly question posts, 1 in–class presentation, 2 short papers/paper idea sketches, and a longer final paper), but Professor Johnson King is a very reasonable grader and spaces the assignments out over the course of the semester, so it does not feel like too much. Professor Johnson King is extremely knowledgeable about ethics and moral psychology, and she is also quite approachable and very willing to make herself available to students in office hours. She has a great sense of humor and does a good job of making class discussion engaging without sacrificing philosophical rigor or depth. I highly recommend this class, especially if you are interested in ethics, value theory, or praise in general.
Absolutely take it! This was my favourite class this semester by a long way. ZJK is an incredible professor and I promise you will not regret it.
I think this was a truly fantastic course and extremely well taught. Prof. Johnson King was extremely knowledgeable of the topic and connected it to many other areas of philosophy which I found extremely helpful. We had to submit weekly discussion questions, which helped a lot to make discussions in the seminar structured, productive, and fruitful. Prof. Johnson King was also extremely helpful when it came to course assignments and papers. She provided thorough feedback and was happy to meet to discuss paper ideas or just the course material in general. In addition, something I really appreciated was how Prof. Johnson King did not only focus on teaching us about the course material itself but also about how to do philosophy more generally. I think I learned a lot about how to write better papers, choose a paper topic, and how to figure out what other literature to engage with. Prof. Johnson King is a truly fantastic professor and I thoroughly enjoyed this class!
