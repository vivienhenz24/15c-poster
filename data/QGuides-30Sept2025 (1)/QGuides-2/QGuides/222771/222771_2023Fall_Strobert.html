Comments
This class probably won't be offered again, but Kianja and Kathryn were incredible teachers. Empathetic but still steadfast in their critique. Because the class was new there were some pacing issues where the first few weeks felt too slow and the final weeks felt too fast. But, overall, you can't go wrong with taking a class with Kianja and/or Kathryn. Kianja is very experienced and gives very helpful feedback and useful assignments. I wish I could have spent a whole year with her!
Take this class.
Please, please only take this course if you have the time and energy to do so. It is so obvious to me (and probably the teachers) when someone is taking an art class because they think it’ll be easy or relaxing. If it is easy or relaxing, that means it’s not rewarding and furthering your skills; you’re doing it wrong.
This class was an amazing introduction to studio art classes. I never knew I could learn so much in such a short period of time while having so much fun. Each project was designed thoughtfully to introduce us to different ways of thinking about painting and looking at the world. I was encouraged to trust myself more than I had before in regard to painting, and learned so many concrete painting skills as well as lessons about creativity and surrealism. I spent so much more time than I expected to on the projects, but it all felt rewarding.
Take this class! I learned so much and it was the perfect introduction to oil painting. Kianja and Kathryn were so patient and encouraging and really wanted to see all the students grow. I enjoyed all the time I spent in the studio as a release from my other stressful classes, and I made good friends in the smaller studio class as well. Overall, this class was a wonderful experience and I'm incredibly happy I took it.
If you're interested in learning how to work with oil paints, take this class! This was my favorite class of the semester and as a senior one of my favorite classes at Harvard. It is beginner–friendly– Prof Strobert and Kathyrn are so so helpful and constructive in their feedback. The only downside is the time commitment out of class depends on your speed as a painter– so if you have no experience at all expect to spend a decent amount of time outside class. My experience in this class was wonderful, and it exercised a part of my brain that other classes at H have not. Take this class, I cannot say enough good things about it!
