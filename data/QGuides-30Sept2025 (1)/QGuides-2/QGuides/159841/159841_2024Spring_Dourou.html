Comments
The Elementary Modern Greek series is excellent! Once you've developed some skills through MODGRK AA (or an equivalent beginner–level experience), you should absolutely give AB a try; it is so rewarding to piece together your vocabulary and grammar knowledge, and begin to put together useful sentences and conversations. I really enjoyed the approachable, collaborative style of this course (not to mention the related extracurricular opportunities available through the Classics department, such as a talk at the Greek consulate), and I hope other students continue to take advantage of Harvard's excellent Modern Greek program!
Another banger of a class from Calliope. Despite having it 4 times a week, I always looked forward to Greek. Calliope fosters a great environment that doesn't even feel like a class, just a group of students coming together to have fun and explore the beauties of the Greek language. Low workload, but there are enough materials and content that you can dive into Greek outside of class, as well. Could not recommend more!
AMAZING COURSE. The professor is the most kind and understanding teacher that I have ever had. She will really help you get your Greek to the level that you want it to be at and is always making sure that each student feels welcome and safe to learn in the classroom.
Calliope was the BEST ever, she made this class so fun and amazing. It was the perfect language to take, you become conversational by the end of the year but there's not too much stress.
Take time to learn the basic grammar cause everything builds, but its a great language to learn!
I am immensely grateful that I got to take this class with Prof. Dourou and the people who were in this small class for this year. This is a class that is NEVER difficult to go to. Even in the most stressful/busy days, this class ALWAYS made my day better. It was definitely way more than just a language class. As a language class it was phenomenal too –– we always had different styles of lessons that would always keep us engaged. Prof. Dourou went above and beyond, often bringing in her children's toys to make it possible for us to learn the language in the most memorable, fun ways possible. Amongst the fun lesson plans, there were also lesson plans that were effective and actually felt like they helped build a community in the class. When we read stories from the Greek textbook, we actually got very invested in the characters of the story, and some students would often be the reader/speaker for the same characters. We watched a lot of Greek ads, and the ads were very memorable often having a storyline as well. To make the ads an even better lesson plan, we would have competitions on who can spot out the relevant words in the ad first. Absolutely amazing class. Truly no class, and no professor compares to prof. Dourou.
This class is amazing, I am devastated that Calliope is leaving, but I still think this class will be amazing. The alphabet is hard at first, but you get used to it eventually.
