Definitely take this class! It's a gem. There are just 3 essays which are super chill. Daniel is a very supportive and understanding person, never makes anyone feel uncomfortable and he takes a lot of interest in what people have to share. The topics are fun (although some of the UFO stuff got a bit repetitive at the end), and it was pretty cool to look at things with a folklore lens. The readings are functionally optional since you don't get quizzed on it during class or anything, but for your own benefit and enjoyment of the class, do the readings so you can participate and better appreciate what's going on.
This course is so cool. You learn so much amount fairies, elves, UFOs, aliens, and other other–worldly beings. Daniel is very nice, attentive, and available and cares deeply about his students opinions and well beings! The readings are great in number but also very short and interesting lore you probably would not encounter otherwise. Definitely take this course!!!
Take it. It's a great course.
This class has a lot of reading each week, but it is manageable if you do it throughout the week, and the topics are interesting.
Dr. Frim is a great professor and really cares about the course material. You will learn a lot but the course is almost entirely what you make of it.
Great class to take if you are interested in folklore or UFOs and enjoy writing papers. I had a great time in this class discussing the readings and our own thoughts on folklore, superstitions, aliens and more. The feedback Daniel provides is really amazing and has definitely made me a better writer. If you are ever considering dabbling in the folklore and mythology department, I think this class is a great choice!
