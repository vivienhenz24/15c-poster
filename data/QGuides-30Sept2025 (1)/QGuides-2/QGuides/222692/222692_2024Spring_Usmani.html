I highly recommend taking this class!!! It forced me to sharpen my skills across my written work and discussions, and pushed me to be a more critical and thoughtful social studies student. Adaner was an amazing professor and I would 100% tell any social studies student to rank this as their #1 choice for a junior tutorial. Outside of the actual class material, the ability to work on a junior tutorial paper related to the thesis was invaluable, particularly with Adaner's feedback throughout the brainstorming, researching, and writing processes.
Take this class! Adaner is amazing! Some of the readings will blow your mind! I rest my case.
This is a remarkable class. Adaner is by far the best professor at Harvard. Not only does this class push you to think about various theories of capitalism, equality, and fairness, it also challenges students rhetorically to develop their argumentation. I've never been in a class of students so excited to participate and so completely dedicated to doing all the reading and putting forward their best work. There is a lot of reading, but the assignments are fun and fair, and you never find yourself doing any busywork. Adaner is not a harsh professor and he really wants to see everyone do well.
Adaner is the absolute GOAT – take everything he offers, you will not regret it

This class is excellent, especially if you have engaged peers, but be prepared for some challenging work, and lots of dense reading.
