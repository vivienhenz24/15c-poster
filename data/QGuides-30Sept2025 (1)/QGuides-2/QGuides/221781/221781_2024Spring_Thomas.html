This is a fantastic course, especially for any sports fans or aspiring journalists. The readings were all super engaging, we often got to speak to the authors themselves during class, the writing assignments were productive and not overdone, and the final paper is a super fun project to end on. Prof Thomas is really really great and I would highly recommend this class overall to absolutely anyone – this was my only real elective during college and I dont regret it at all.
Take this class! If you are at all interested in sports, writing, or sportswriting, then this class is great. Even if you are not a sports fan, you can get by just fine. This is one of the few classes where I did every single reading without finding myself bored at all. The guest lecturers from the world of sports journalism are so cool! They answer any question you can think of, and it is awesome to learn from such successful professionals in the field. Professor Thomas is amazing – I would recommend this class to anyone!
100% take this class if you are interested in some kind of sport/s. Every class felt like there was something positive to look forward to. Also, there are weekly speakers that came in via zoom or in person to chat with the class which was really insightful and interesting.
If you're interested in sports and/or writing take this course. Louisa is great and really helpful, she wants everybody to succeed and pursue topics that interest them. The workload was more than manageable and most of the material was really interesting.
A really great course if you are interested in sports and creative writing. The course is not very difficult besides a couple hours of reading a week. Professor Thomas is super helpful and nice. Highly recommend if you are interested in sports in general. You'll grow as a writer for sure.
This is a must–take creative writing course if you're at all interested in sports or even if you just want to learn how to write better essays. Between analyzing pieces of sportswriting from well–known publications like ESPN, the New Yorker, and Sports Illustrated and listening to guests talk about their own journeys in sportswriting, you really learn a lot about this important, but underappreciated, part of journalism.
