This course is ALMOST (and emphasis on the almost) a gem. If you want an easy workload, a class that doesn't demand all of your academic focus, or a last class that will be a stress–free addition to your schedule, by all means take this class because it's not that bad of a workload. You truly get to learn how to be creative and an entrepreneur, and by every measure the final project in this class was one of, if not, the top highlight of my semester (academically–speaking). You collaborate to create a unique venture with three others that may allow you (attempt) to be the next Zuckerberg or Gates. HOWEVER, be warned of the 3–hour lectures, arbitrary grading, and minimal communication from the teaching team that awaits you when you enroll. It is a very awesome and unique class, and if you have the elective credit to use, by all means, TAKE IT FOR FUN.
