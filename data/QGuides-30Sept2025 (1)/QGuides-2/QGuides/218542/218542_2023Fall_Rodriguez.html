Comments
Lots of reading (about one book per week)! But I loved the course and discussions.
This course was amazing! The texts were all varied and approached the topic from a variety of perspectives. The class was small but very engaged and the assignments were fair in grading and manageable in workload. The one book a week could be tough at times but it was generally spaced out very well. The professor was also very welcoming and if you're not 100% confident in your French, it is a supportive environment to improve too.
The syllabus is great, if a bit depressing at times; class discussions could be made a bit more dynamic.
