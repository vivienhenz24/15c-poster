It has the potential to be very interesting but it's heavy on the readings and a big picture of why we're discussing something or the importance of our discussions is often missing
Take this class! You will learn how to think by engaging with contemporary and classic theories. You will also gain a coherent perspective on several broad topics within social psychology, and dive deeply into methods on a few papers. Make sure in your response papers that you focus on advancing an argument rather than summarizing the readings, and dont skip by sections of your argument too quickly. Speak up in class, the atmosphere tends to be intellectually generous and the instructor takes you seriously as an academic peer.
work load is very reasonable in comparison to other proseminars for graduate students; the final paper is an opportunity to think through research ideas that you may not have had a chance to work on in your lab (e.g., either because you have been focusing the bulk of your time on other projects with your advisor, etc.) given the expectation is that we don't use recycled work/work we have already been writing so we have to come up with some new writing for it. which is hard but potentially worth it in the end (but i wouldnt know yet because we havent received the final feedback for the assignment yet!) –s o will have to put a pin in that one for now
