don't get behind on any assignments, manage time well, and READ THE SYLLABUS
This class is a LOT, and it is very easy to fall behind. There are 4–5 assignments per week and they can be extremely long and tedious. However, it is easy to secure an A in physics if you simply do all the work. The assignments for the week are posted every Thursday, so you can dedicate one day on the Friday or the weekend to get all the assignments done and then you don't have to worry about the class outside of class time. You have to go to class every single day because class time consists of teamwork and presentations where you share your answers to your assignments with a TF. DO NOT FALL BEHIND. if you get a bad score on an assignment request a makeup IMMEDIATELY, if not you will be stressing at the end of the year and running out of grade upgrades. IF you miss one single assignment or get a bad score on one assignment your grade will drop from an A to a B– so please pay attention and stay on top of this class.

Do note that the class does demand a lot of time outside of class to complete the many weekly assignments. The projects can also get very stressful as the last two projects were crunched into very little time so the class is very fast paced and can be overwhelming at times.
This course requires you to invest some time consistently throughout the course, rather than bursts of effort, but it is worth maintaining that as it makes you feel much more familiar with all of the content.
