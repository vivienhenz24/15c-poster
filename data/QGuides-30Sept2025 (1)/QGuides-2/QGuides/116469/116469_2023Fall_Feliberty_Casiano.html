This class was one of my highlights of the semester. You learn a ton of Spanish in a fast–paced setting. Considering I had no Spanish background, I was shocked at the progress I had made. By the end of the semester, I feel like I could at least understand all types of Spanish speakers, and have the ability to communicate the necessities to get around in a Spanish speaking country. I would not say I am fluent by any means, but it is an amazing start if you are serious about learning the language. You also build a community with the small classroom setting––we were all somewhat close by the end of the semester, and I can say I made some actual friends in the class :). Only downside to the class was meeting everyday at 9AM, but it helped build a routine. Would definitely recommend this class to anyone looking to learn Spanish.

Take this class!!!!!!!!! The hardest part is waking up at 9 am every day, but Xiomara does such a good job at engaging everyone and creating an energetic, positive atmosphere so early in the morning. Learning Spanish through fun activities and interactions with your peers is so much more refreshing and engaging than just taking notes on slides every day in my opinion. Having an exam and presentation/project every other week isn't that easy, but Xiomara isn't strict and they're not that bad if you've tried your best and focused in class! Lastly, Xiomara is such a kind, understanding, patient professor– you won't find someone like her anywhere else. <33
