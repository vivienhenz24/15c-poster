A wonderful course! Relatively long hours in the lab (6 hours/week), but compensates with very little work outside of lab. The time in lab also flies by. Entirely lab–based course, so if you are looking to learn about basic biology there are probably better courses. But you will come away with a lot of confidence in the lab, and also learn some really cool experiments (expansion microscopy is really cool)! Course staff is absolutely wonderful – Fei is delightful, and he really does care about student learning/success. Also his research is very cool! Overall great way to fulfill a SCRB requirement. I had relatively extensive lab experience before this course and still took a lot away from it.
I highly recommend taking this class! As a non–bio major (still very interested in biology but I doubt you're considering this course if you're not) without much wet lab experience, I found it very accessible and learned a lot about experiments and the intuition behind them, which was really fun. The specific experimental techniques you get to learn and do are just so cool (e.g. mouse IVF, expansion microscopy, planning and executing your own independent project – super rewarding). And the teaching team is AMAZING; the professor and TFs are so knowledgeable and kind. Also, since this class is pretty small, I think there's a great community aspect as well. all in all 10/10 recommend!
I loved this class! It was so fun and all the experiments were super interesting! Fei is also an amazing lecturer and just an all–around amazing person, I loved all the random conversations our class would have during lab. It really does not even feel like 3 hours, the time just flies by. Kevin and Michael were also amazing at keeping the class running smoothly and always willing to help/answer questions/make themselves available. Truly one of my favorite classes I've taken at Harvard.
You should absolutely take this class. This is such an incredible course. It is very small which gives you a chance to get to know your classmates and work closely with the teaching staff. Additionally, you learn some very cool techniques in the labs and it's not too much work outside of the class. The final project seems daunting at first, but is very fun in the end. I cannot recommend this class enough!
This class was extremely useful for honing your lab skills and learning how to use microscopes in a variety of assays. The teaching staff are wonderful, and you'll enjoy your time here.
Take this course!! Fei, Kevin, and Michael are the best and you become so close to everyone in the class. Workload is mostly done in class, it amps up a bit near the end with the final project but that's pretty much it and you get to learn about so many cool lab techniques and try them for yourself! If you're a hands–on learner this is the class for you, and if you like learning the why and how behind things then Fei is the teacher for you!
TAKE THIS CLASS!! Literally my favorite class I've ever taken. You do a bunch of experiments over the course of the semester, with minimal lecture segments interspersed during the lab work. You get to do mouse IVF, which was so incredibly cool to see. The final group project is super independent which is a good experience to have. And of course Fei is the most amazing professor, super knowledgeable about the field and just a super fun person to work with!! Kevin and Michael too were amazing! And because the class is so small (we had 9 people) we got super close and had so much fun! Best class ever
