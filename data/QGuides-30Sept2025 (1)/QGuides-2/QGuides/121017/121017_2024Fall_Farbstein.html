Professor Farbstein will guide you every step of the way and she will lead the class with an enthusiasm I have rarely ever seen before.
Such an incredible seminar. Take it! Take it! Take it!!! Professor Farbstein is the most wonderful instructor and human being. So fascinating to tackle the complexities and criticisms associated with the human rights movement and human rights law.
I highly recommend taking this seminar. It gave me a very good picture of what human rights work entails and it helped me identify nuances about human rights advocacy that I wouldn't have otherwise know of. Professor Farbstein is just fantastic and her work is truly inspiring.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! I'm fully a STEM major but I enjoyed taking this class so much. Prof. Farbstein is insanely smart, talented, and kind. Readings are incredibly interesting and discussions are very engaging. If you are at all interested in international law or human rights, this is such a good class to take.
If you are looking for a course to take, this is a great option. This is course is for EVERYONE, regardless of your major and it really helps you think critically about past and some present human rights violation cases. It is an eye opening course and Ms. Farbstein is an awesome professor, anything you speak about, she will listen attentively and have a great conversation with you. This course is a great option if you are looking for a first year seminar option.
TAKE THIS CLASS! PROF FARBSTEIN IS THE BEST! YOU WILL LEARN HOW TO BE A RESPONSIBLE HUMAN RIGHTS ADVOCATE THAT HELPS COMMUNITIES HEAL, ACHIEVE JUSTICE, AND EXPERIENCE AGENCY!

Take advantage of all the readings and time you get to spend with Prof Farbstein, she is incredibly knowledgable and compassionate (one of the best people at Harvard)!
Though the readings might seem daunting, they are more than worth it for the discussions that you get to have in class. I couldn't recommend this course enough.
