This is a great course! And final presentation was a great learning experience on the topic of your own choice relevant to the class materials. Highly recommend this course to any advanced undergraduates who are interested in pursuing condensed matter theory.
Quantum phases of matter was a really fun and worthwhile course. This class answered so many questions about modern condensed matter that I've had for years.
Covers essential research–level material in condensed matter and many–body quantum theory. Course is mostly self contained, but some QFT intuition is certainly necessary and expected, The material is demanding and the course requires some commitment. Problem sets are usually short in terms of number of questions, but they take some time.
