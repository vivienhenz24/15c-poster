The trips were a lot of fun, and I got to see some parts of Boston that I haven't seen before. If you are interested in maps or history or cities or just want to learn something new, you should totally take this class.
TAKE THIS CLASS. It will truly make your entire freshman year. We go on field trips/excursions, and Professor Ur is genuinely the kindest professor. He makes an effort to carefully plan fun and intellectually rewarding field trips and activities. He even invited us to his home for dinner with his family! Professor Ur is also very accessible he encourages everyone to go to his office hours, and he develops a one–on–one relationship with all of his seminar students. By the end of the course, the entire class grew very close, and we walked away not only having taken the best First–Year Seminar, but also having developed a more nuanced view of Harvard and Boston's place in American history.
