This seminar is such a great experience and would highly recommend to anyone looking to concentrate in HAA or just is interested in art history at all.
Great class and a fantastic introduction to the department! As someone who was only considering concentrating in History of Art and Architecture (HAA), this class truly made me want to pursue it further. Maybe it was the small seminar setting, which encouraged in–depth discussion and allowed for a close, thoughtful engagement with the works. Or perhaps it was the course content and readings themselves. Either way, this class is a testament to how well the department utilizes its resources.
