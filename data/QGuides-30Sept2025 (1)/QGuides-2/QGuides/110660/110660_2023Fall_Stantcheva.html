It would be helpful to do pre–class readings before going to the class, attend office hours, and review sessions to maximize gains from the course.
Excellent class, by far the best I have ever taken! Optimal tax policy is so cool and Professor Stantcheva knows absolutely everything, I'm so glad I took this!
The section slides are great for understanding derivations.
If you're at all interested in optimal taxation or public economics, this course is a must! You should be comfortable with microeconomics and calculus, but section 1 will provide a very useful recap and sufficiently thorough if you feel rusty. Classes with Stefanie are amazing with ample mix between theory and empirics to both be interesting and challenging. You'll come out of the class with a thorough overview of everything related to public economics and be more than adequately prepared to engage in academic debate and early stage research. Beware, PSet 1 is useful for making sure you understand the derivations from class in detail, but can be a bit tedious and is not directly applicable to the exam.
