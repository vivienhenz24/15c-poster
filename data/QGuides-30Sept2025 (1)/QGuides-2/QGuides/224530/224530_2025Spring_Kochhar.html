Comments
This class is awesome! Each week you are truly challenged by Professor Kochhar to think deeply about the topics presented that week. Readings can be somewhat difficult but extremely rewarding.
Take this class. Professor Kochhar is one of the kindest, most thoughtful instructors I have ever had at Harvard, and I always looked forward to having class with him. The readings are fascinating, and even if you are not into the life sciences from a technical perspective, the classroom discussions often focussed on the broader cultural or societal implications of science, which meant anybody with an interest in how science impacts our daily lives and values had a valuable perspective to contribute. This was one of my favorite classes I took at Harvard, and I was seriously impressed that this is only Professor Kochhar's first year teaching he is an incredible asset to the department and he has a unique ability to connect with students and foster an inclusive and open classroom environment where everybody's opinion is valued.
Take this course! You will learn a lot! Rijul is great and is very enthusiastic about the subject matter at hand.
This course wasn't always exciting, but I learned from it either way AND it is relatively light. If you put effort in to understand the readings, you leave with a better understanding of science. If you don't do the minimal work required, you're probably better off taking something more engaging.
