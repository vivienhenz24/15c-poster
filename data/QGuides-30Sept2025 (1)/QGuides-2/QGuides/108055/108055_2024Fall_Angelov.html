Comments
Super fun class! Dr. Angelov is really good at facilitating group discussion. You learn a lot about how interconnected the medieval world was. Assignments are fine, as long as you try. I was always excited to come to class and hear everyone's thoughts on the text of the week. Readings weren't too killer, but some of them were long.
Take this class! If you like medieval history you'll have a lot of fun reading primary sources and discussing them. This is not the course that will teach you the most about the Byzantine Empire because the focus is on the travel documents. However the travel documents offer a great window into the medieval world that makes everything feel more real and immediate than if I were learning historical facts in a lecture. You don't necessarily need a lot of background knowledge to take this class, although it does help. The section feels a little bit weird because there's no super clear model for what should happen in section for a seminar class, but overall the class is great.
This is a hard class, but very rewarding. I went into this class with ZERO knowledge of the Byzantine Empire, and I left feeling very confident about this topic. I loved professor Angelov and Gayane, and they are both amazing teachers and mentors in this class. More importantly, they both really care about their students.
The class was wonderful! Prof Angelov is truly a great professor and mentor who cares deeply about his students. He genuinely was so helpful and went above and beyond every single time. While background may be helpful, I personally had no background and was more than fine. Definitely a great class! While I sometimes felt lost, I feel like I was always able to get context. and Prof Angelov and Gayane were always there to help.
