This is a fantastic course! Prof. Katz is great and knows so much about education and economics as a whole. Highly recommend this course!

If you want to write an education–related thesis and want to start working on it early, taking this is a good idea, given you start EARLY and work on it throughout the semester. Prof. Katz is a top economist in his field and has a lot of insight to offer, and the TF, Victoria, is really helpful.
