What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is an incredible opportunity to intensely sharpen scholarly skills through deep engagement with essential texts in the history of literature. Cannot recommend enough!
Take the course! Respond to the text sincerely and you will be prepared for an exciting and provocative class discussion. Do not be afraid to come to class with a strong reading and try to defend it.
The amount of reading is not light by any means, but it does get easier to do over time. Your German reading skills will improve tremendously, and you will be prepared to read most any text, old or new. Overall, I'd really recommend this class — you get to choose which weeks you have a writing assignment due, so you can build a schedule that works for you, and the discussions are really edifying.
If you have interest in or use for understanding a very classical period of German and Western literature, this is a great opportunity to do so thoroughly with a dedicated and accessible professor.
If you want to think critically about a moment in literary history—which is not always easy to find—this is an excellent class.
