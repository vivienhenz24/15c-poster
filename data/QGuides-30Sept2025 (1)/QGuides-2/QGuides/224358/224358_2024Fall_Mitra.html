Take this class! Professor Mitra is extremely knowledgeable, and I left each class in awe. All course–takers are super invested and bring so much insight, helping me see the readings in a different light. I looked forward to each discussion.
If this course is offered again, you should DEFINITELY take this class! Professor Mitra is an incredible instructor and does an amazing job engaging the class with the readings. She led each discussion with so much enthusiasm and energy that the two hour (sometimes longer) session never ceased to be exciting and thought–provoking. She also had a great teaching attitude and brought humor into the classroom. We had several sessions in the Schlesinger Library, which I thoroughly enjoyed. Being able to touch and study the manuscripts and materials collected by important figures, some of whom we read, was a rewarding, unique experience that I hadn't had before this class. The readings can be long during some weeks so plan enough time to get through those. Our TF Poorna was also amazing and easily available outside of class. She helped me think through each assignment and explore a multitude of perspectives or ideas that I hadn't thought to consider before. I 100% recommend taking this course – you will leave a different person!
TAKE THIS CLASS. Professor Mitra is one of those professors you just HAVE to take class with at Harvard if you're passionate about women's issues, understanding imperialism / colonialism, reproductive histories.... This class had something for everyone. She is THE expert and she is so good at moderating class discussion (which I've often found not to be the case in many seminars I've taken).
Take this class! Professor Mitra is a superstar, leaving Harvard without taking a class with her would be a mistake! The assignments were genuinely interesting and fun to complete in addition to building on important skills, the readings were captivating and relevant, and class itself was eye–opening.
Professor Mitra and Poorna, the TF, are a fantastic team, and really worked to cultivate a supportive and engaging learning environment, particularly around the difficult topics which naturally surfaced in the course. Their creativity in coming up with assignments and selecting readings made for a deeply meaningful experience, and one which filled a MAJOR gap in my education, particularly in the field of WGSId take this course again in a heartbeat.
If you need a WGS foundational class, take this course! Professor Mitra is amazing, and the readings are interesting and not too hard to understand. The assignments are unique and a nice change from the typical WGS essays.
Take this class ! Professor Mitra is a fabulous professor and you'll learn so much about the ways many issues in the world are interconnected and specifically how women are historically marginalized in many of them

Do yourself a favor and take this class!
