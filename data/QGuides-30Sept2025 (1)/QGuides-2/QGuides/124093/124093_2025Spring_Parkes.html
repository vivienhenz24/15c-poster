What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
TAKE THIS CLASS. Language classes are so underrated (?) at Harvard (I know many people who simply take a placement exam or never take language in college) and German was probably my favorite because of the community, events, and class structure. Even though it meets everyday at 9 am, it was super fun and I looked forward to it everyday. Due to the nature of the class and the daily homework, I wouldn't take it if you were thesising or have a heavy course load or else it might seem like a drag. Otherwise, take it!
I highly recommend this course. I took it as a second semester senior with a desire to try something new, and I absolutely loved it. It requires a good amount of time and commitment to language learning outside the classroom, but the teachers are so lovely and supportive, and I learnt so much in such a short period of time. Professor Chip is just incredible. He is so engaging, makes sure that everyone is included, and challenges us constantly to improve our German. He teaches us a lot about German culture too, which adds so much texture to our learning. He also takes such an interest in our lives, such that our German becomes authentic, true, and un–formulaic. Take this course :)
Be grateful for your instructors! They are always willing to support you! Don't be afraid to ask for help!
