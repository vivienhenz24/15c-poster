TAKE THIS CLASS if you can. Dr. Fischer is an absolutely wonderful teacher and human being. This class taught me so much, not only about skin and dermatology, but healthcare as a holistic field, paper reading, and presentation skills. The class is small (10 people) which allows for really engaging discussion and reflection on different topics. You only present every other week with a short (1 pg) reflection due every week, so the course load is quite light. The final project also has a lot of flexibility and really allows you to explore a segment of dermatology in depth. You grow so close both to your classmates and Dr. Fischer, who is really such an amazing person to talk with and hear about his own path. If you are pre–med I would 100% recommend this class, and even if you aren't it is such a great experience and a class I looked forward to going to every week.
Very chill seminar. Professor Fisher is a great teacher and extremely knowledgeable about dermatology. He explains concepts so you can see how basic biological principles (i.e. those you might see in LS1A) affect skin in real life. Highly recommend.
This course was wonderful and I would highly recommend it! Dr. Fisher is the nicest man and the best professor. He gave us the freedom to learn about the content on our own while still providing interesting contributions and insights from his own career in dermatology. He is so supportive and you can see how much he cares about the students in the class. The class itself is incredibly interesting and combines so many different topics – biology, chemistry, medicine, dermatology, cosmetics, cancer, hair, etc. The course doesn't require too much outside work, but I definitely still learned a lot and had so many takeaways; it was the perfect example of what a freshman seminar should be.
If you are at all interested in learning about the skin, take this course. Dr. Fisher is incredibly kind and truly wants everyone to succeed. Course work is light so that the focus can truly be on engaging with the class content. As a pre–med, this is a unique opportunity to take a course that is more focused on the patient aspect of medicine, not just chemistry and biology that most undergraduate classes teach. As a first–year especially, I definitely recommend this seminar, since the content was so interesting yet likely will not be covered until med school.

I learned so much from this class, and it was a highlight of my first semester. Do prepare for the fact that the students give presentations each week about the topics, although it is an entirely reasonable amount of work :)
Dr Fisher is extremely knowledgeable and gracious. His class encourages learning in a relaxed environment. Definitely take the class to better understand how we interact with our environment through the skin.
Dr. Fisher is an amazing teacher that guides students and encourages growth along with learning. He gives direction but allows you to choose where to go with that. This is definitely a chill class and a great gateway to dermatology!
