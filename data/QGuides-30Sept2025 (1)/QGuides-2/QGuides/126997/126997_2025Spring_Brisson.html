TAKE THIS CLASS. I have met one of my current closest friends through this class but I have also learned so much. Dr.Brisson is one of my favorite instructors I have ever had the privilege to work with and she is truly such a passionate, brilliant, and understanding professor. She provided us with many opportunities such as meeting the producers or even the authors of certain films we watched and she always makes every class very engaging and interesting. She has helped me improve my French writing and speaking skills and she is always very understanding of outside circumstances or stresses of being a Harvard student in general. I took this class to fulfill my language requirement but it has inspired me to continue and pursue a French citation in hopes to study under Dr.Brisson as well. The work load can be at times plentiful but it is never busy work and again Dr.Brisson is very understanding and is always willing to accommodate with her students. This course also does not just focus on one French dialect or culture making it even more enjoyable as you get familiar with so many different cultures and backgrounds. Overall, this has been one of my favorite classes I have taken at Harvard and highly recommend anyone to take it. Dr.Brisson is a wonderful and passionate instructor who truly loves what she does and the course material is very interesting and captivating.
