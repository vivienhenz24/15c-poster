Take this course! It's a great language, a great class, and it has a great instructor with great vibes
If you are even slightly interested in learning a South Asian language, Tamil is the way to go! Jonathan is so amazing at teaching you everything you have to know from start to finish through Tamil 101A/101B. You really will walk out being able to watch Tamil movies without subtitles and still understand almost 80% of the movie from the tools you learn in the class. You also learn so much about Tamil culture. Jonathan lived in Madurai for years doing non–profit work there, and he often shares stories about his experiences and teaches us lots about culture!
Take this course! There's much less grammar, a lot more reading, and a fun, intimate community! It is useful to have taken 101a, because Jonathan introduces grammar terms in that class that carry on to this class, but it could be doable without 101a too.
