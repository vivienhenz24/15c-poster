This is such a great course!!! Genuinely one of my favorite classes this semester. The readings were so interesting and I really loved the seminar environment. This was my first HistSci class, and it made me decide that I wanted to be a concentrator with the department. Professor Kochhar is great and genuinely cares about his students. I'm so glad that I was able to take this course, and I would highly highly recommend to anyone interested in learning about different therapeutic rationalities and exploring developments in the history of science over the past two centuries. It was so cool to learn about how different events ultimately influenced changes in our healthcare system that we see today. Could not recommend more!
Take this class! Dr. Kochhar is so knowledgable and understanding. He truly wants you to learn and feel comfortable learning. You take what you put in for this class.
This class is sooo nicely taught and the professor really cares about getting to know the students! The discussions were always enriching, but related to the readings. You have to do the readings tho since the discussions are based on the readings themselves––HOWEVERR, they are all readings you enjoy. I was able to read materials from various cultures and their approaches to Medicine for the first time at Harvard––from Soviet to Chinese to Indian to Middle Eastern. He is also soooo kind, definitely take this course. I usually don't even leave feedbacks but I thought I had to for this!!!
Absolutely phenomenal class and professor! Considering this was the first year the course was offered, I thought it was executed amazingly. The readings were sometimes long and dense, but it was clear that they were carefully chosen to provoke intellectual engagement and challenge our preconceived beliefs. The readings complemented each other very well, and I was consistently impressed by how the discussions from one week would connect to or resolve lingering questions from the previous week.

Professor Kochhar did an excellent job facilitating class discussions, designing exercises to help us engage deeply with the material, and adapting to the needs and curiosities of the class. His openness and flexibility were key to making this class one of the most memorable of my entire college career.

If you're interested in topics like medicine, public health, psychology, systems of healing, or similar fields, I highly recommend this course. The content far exceeded my expectations, and you get significant freedom with your final paper, allowing you to explore a topic you're genuinely passionate about. Overall, I can't recommend this class enough!
TAKE THIS COURSE! Dr. Kochhar is a very knowledgeable person whose insights will stick with you forever. I left this course with a new perspective on Western medicine and strongly believe this should be a class every pre med student is required to take.
