It's a very rewarding course! The prof and TF are very supportive as well. I think anyone who is interested in pursuing history seriously would benefit from it.
This course is amazing. Professor Blair piqued my interest in academia for the first time since being a Harvard student. She inspires her students and provides a ton of support and grace when needed
Really enjoyed this course, Professor Blair and Saffron are both great. "Intellectual history" is a bit amorphous so you spend a few weeks establishing a shared foundation, but then you get to focus on some specific themes for later weeks. I really enjoyed the readings on the Haitian Declaration of Independence. 97 is a requirement, yes, but this is a good class!
TAKE THIS CLASS! Prof. Blair is one of the best professors you'll get to meet at Harvard. She's super knowledgeable about a variety of topics (even tho she specializes in French intellectual history) and has always been so delightful and willing to help. Saffron is a wonderful tutor and this class is much more fun and approachable because of her. One thing I really appreciated about this class was the amount of feedback you get. First you attend a 4–person peer review session every 3 weeks and get feedback from your classmates and very detailed comments from Saffron. After you submit the draft you get another detailed feedback from Prof. Blair before you actually submit the final version of each essay. The final paper also allows a lot of freedom when you choose the topic because intellectual history is super broad. Really happy that I was able to spend about 2 months on a topic I'm super interested in and eventually craft and polish it with my classmates, Saffron, and Prof. Blair.
PLEASE enroll in this course. Professor Blair is one of the Professors at this school that you want to have met. She is incredibly kind and a gifted teacher. She builds great community and will make you a better writer. You will take away so much from this course
This course is necessary and very helpful for the history academic discipline! Keep on top of your research (set your own deadlines), and it should be very doable.
Read, and make sure to read your essays out loud.
History 97b will change you! Your skills as a writer will improve so much and you will learn a lot about being an historian. You will get to enjoy the mentorship of the wonderful Professor Blair, while Saffron Sener is a dedicated TF who will support you every step of the way.
