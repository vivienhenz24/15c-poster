TAKE THIS CLASS!!! Especially if you have any interest in teaching (German or another language) in the future! This course provides both official qualifications (through the Goethe Institute) and consistent teaching opportunities at the Boston International School –– opportunities hard to encounter elsewhere. You will build meaningful relationships not only with the students you teach, but also your co–instructors. Professor Parkes is also phenomenal; she creates a very welcoming, supportive, and low–stress environment in which we frequently brainstormed and troubleshooted as a group.
This class is a lot of fun, and a great way to fulfil a requirement for a language citation, secondary, or concentration. Dr. Parkes is really sweet and usually pretty flexible, and the workload is very manageable, so long as you keep on top of it (which can be more difficult with a more lenient teacher!) My one criticism of the course is that it could be a little disorganized at times; but if you're willing to just go with the flow, everything will turn out okay. Would definitely recommend!
