This class was always a delight to go to! Richard–ji is certainly one of the professors that genuinely seeks to know his students better, and he always directs the class with good humor and empathy. If you're a native speaker, this class may not be the most fulfilling for you, since I was expecting us to spend more time on the basics of the language I didn't know, but we move through that pretty quickly so everyone can read at the same level soon enough. If you're more interested in the grammatical aspect of the language as someone who is already fluent, this class may not be as interesting, but it is still an enjoyable class to be in. I would recommend you actually do the nightly assignments, and try to do the weekly assignments as soon as you get them––Seton–ji would usually go over most of the assignment when we got them on Thursdays, so staying after class to finish is a good idea.

Please speak to him during office hours or at the end of class personally to align your motivations a bit.
