What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Amazing class!
If you are at all interested in African American Studies from a humanistic perspective, take this course. Like you may expect, this course will exercise your chops as a student of literature and history as you dive through a canon of Black American plays dating back to the antebellum era. Via this Black lens as well, you will gain a special appreciation for these works and the theater medium, more broadly. Moreover, you will also be given invaluable experience working with theater critically, an opportunity which is often missing from academic spaces.
Wonderful professors, who both offer unique perspectives. The intimate class size and engaging topics always led to an interesting and informed discussion.
