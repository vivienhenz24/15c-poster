DEFINITELY TAKE THIS CLASS! ANTHRO 1853 is an amazing class, and Damina is an great professor. She is very supportive of her students, very kind, and accessible outside of class. Whether you are an anthropology concentrator or who is interested in learning more about anthropology or ethnographic writing, I would highly highly highly recommend taking this class. I feel like I learned a lot about the anthropology of the everyday, especially thinking about different aspects of it such as space and time, and learned a lot about ethnographic writing.
