What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Peter Galison is a gem. However, because this course was run as a full–group discussion this year (e.g. "break out into groups and report"), I ended up hearing much more from other students than from one of the best professors in the department. Grading will not be a problem.
I'd highly recommend taking this course if you're interested in the topics! The readings are super fascinating, and Prof. Galison is an amazing teacher. I wish I had taken more time for the readings, because I would've gotten so much more out of the class, so be prepared to spend a lot of time reading.
Anyone with an interest in the history of science should take this course. The readings are interesting and vary greatly from week to week. You will read about everything from Galileo's drawings to recent Mars rover missions. You will also have great intellectual freedom to pick a final paper topic and explore it. I highly recommend this class.
