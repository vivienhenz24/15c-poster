This is a great class! Professor Craig and Maya and awesome instructors who really care about students and are very responsive to feedback and to the classroom environment. This class moves at a different pace from other classes at Harvard, which some may find frustrating but can be really cool to lean into if you choose. All of the assignments were super interesting and intentional, as were the readings, and none of it was an overwhelming amount of work. Good theories and methods for Social Studies concentrators who are interested in race/Ethnic Studies, but know that you won't receive a handbook of exactly how to do x method for your thesis... you'll get a much more broad and critical understanding of methods!
For future students interested in this class, I'd say definitely take it! The course is very enlightening even if some of the readings are long/difficult to break down it's worth it.
