This is SUCH a great course! If you get the chance to take it then definitely do! Diana & Ryan are such wonderful professors who care so much about musicals, and more importantly, about sharing their knowledge and enthusiasm with the students. We have such great discussions about different shows and as we get towards the end of the class we get to pitch a musical to them. This class is a MUST.
This class is beyond fascinating and fun. Ryan and Diane are so kind and are wonderful professors. They have the craziest and coolest stories to tell, and it is such a privilege to take this class at the ART, using the archival material and going on amazing adventures to New York and across the hall to the Loeb Main Stage!!!!
take this clas!!! It was phenomenal, so fun, and such a rare opportunity to dive into the professional world of theater at Harvard
If you love musicals and are even remotely interested in a life in theater, I'd recommend this class as you get to learn from people who've really done it, analyze musicals and how they work, and develop your own ideas with like–minded individuals.
Even if you have no experience at all in musical theater, take it!
If you have any interest in pursuing musical theater as a career or are curious about how productions are built from adapting source material to bringing it to the stage, there is no better course at Harvard (or perhaps anywhere). The gem of this class is learning from Ryan and Diane, both of whom are practitioners of theater who have built successful careers bringing shows to the stage. They are inspiring, gracious, and incredibly passionate people who articulate musical development extremely well and foster a passion for the arts (and the many careers it contains) in a way I have not previously experienced at Harvard. This is by far one of the most impactful and inspiring classes I have taken and will help build connections with real, practicing artists in the field. To actually prepare for each class, the readings and viewings of musicals can take several hours each week, so the class is not necessarily the easiest if you're looking for a gem. However, if you do the work and participate, this class will soon become one of your favorites. I looked forward to it every week.
TAKE THIS CLASS! If you love musical theater, this is basically a dream class. The sheer amount of knowledge, wisdom, insight, and stories that Diane and Ryan have to offer about musicals is incredible. I have never felt so excited and engaged in a class. The opportunities this class offered us, ranging from a free trip to New York City to a Zoom Q&A with Sara Bareilles, are unlike any other class I have ever taken. I would encourage people to do all the reading and watching every week if they can because this is one of those classes where I really believe you get out as much as you put in.
This course is absolutely incredible! It is a once–in–a–lifetime experience to get firsthand behind–the–scenes exposure to some of broadway's most notable productions. The teaching staff are renowned professionals in their field and put so much care into making the course enriching and special for the students involved.
