What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class was really interesting! Even though it seemed like Professor Li was teaching this topic for the first time, the materials were super engagingthe syllabus spanned from ancient Chinese texts like Biographies of Exemplary Women to modern research on medicine, women, and gender. Professor Li explained everything in a way that was easy to follow yet intellectually rigorous, and she consistently encouraged us to speak up in discussions. We could really feel her expertise and charisma in every class. Thanks to her approach, the atmosphere was always open and lively.
Take this class if you are at all interested in Chinese literature about women!! Professor Li cultivates an incredible reading list that is both wide–ranging and deeply engaging. This was one of the classes where I genuinely looked forward to weekly discussions and reading the stories or poems assigned. Professor Li is also incredibly nice and open to helping students do their best in their class.
I really loved the readings in this course. Professor Li makes class discussions so engaging that the two hours just fly by. The other students also bring great perspectives, making discussions even more enriching. This class is accessible to people outside the concentration, and youll learn a lot if you keep an open mind. Writing papers and discussion boards was surprisingly enjoyable, especially since you get to share your thoughts. Theres no class quite like this one on this topic, and Id highly recommend it if you get the chance!
