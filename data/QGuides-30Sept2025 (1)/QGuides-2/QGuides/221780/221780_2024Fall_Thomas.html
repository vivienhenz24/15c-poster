This workshop has easily been the best course I've taken at college. The assigned readings are amazing, the workshop discussions are genuinely interesting, and the class atmosphere is warm and inviting. Louisa is a wonderful instructor who will give you fair, objective feedback on your writing.

The weekly homework is to read the assigned readings (usually 2–3 profiles/essays/book chapters) and read/give feedback on two classmates' pieces. You write two pieces for the workshop (~3k words) and then submit a revised version of one to serve as your "final" piece. You'll get what you put into the course!
Take this class! Louisa is an incredible instructor and gives great feedback as well as a good introduction to the genre as a whole. A super approachable way to share your writing and receive feedback in a classroom environment.
Take this class! It's so great! It isn't a lot of work week to week and Louisa is very practical, down to earth and passionate. It's a great intimate course and you really get to know your classmates.
Amazing class!! Readings are not too much and are always super interesting. This class has helped me develop my writing skills and learn much about what goes into a good piece. Louisa is amazing and always very helpful!
I deeply encourage students from all backgrounds and concentrations to enroll in Life Writing! I valued my experience interrogating what makes a non–fiction writing piece effective, successful, captivating, or candidly, none of these things. Furthermore, a significant takeaway from my Life Writing seminar experience was my strengthened confidence in my own writing and in providing useful, supportive, and constructive feedback to colleagues' writing. I feel much more secure and prepared in the vulnerable process of sharing working drafts of my own and fruitfully supporting drafts of my peers. Finally, I gained a foundational literacy in life writing devices and can now identify these advanced devices in published works that I read or in my own writing beyond Life Writing.
Please please take this class! Louisa is phenomenal and this class and her genuinely attract some of the best, most thoughtful people you will meet on this campus. This was my first time taking an English class and I am SO glad that I chose Life Writing to be that. Everything about this class is exceptional: the readings were super accessible and thought–invoking, my peers inspired and pushed me and I felt like I grew as a writer and learner.
