It is important to make sure to memorize and learn the new vocab each chapter.
This course is fantastic! Professor Broome is phenomenal! This is exactly how a language course should be taught. I had 4 years of Spanish in high school but sadly didn't have AP Spanish and so couldn't test out of Harvard's language requirement. I've always had an interest in traveling to Sweden though and had heard that this was a great course. If you take it, I can almost guarantee the following: you will learn a shocking amount in just a short time and you will have a great time doing so – despite the 9AM time slot! Professor Broome is engaging, funny, brilliant, patient, and doesn't judge when you might be having trouble grasping some of the tougher aspects of the Swedish language. The exams are tough but fair (study hard for a few days, not just the night before and be sure to stay up on the vocabulary). The creative project as a final exam is a great low stress (but high effort, learning intensive) way to finish off the semester. Highly recommend!! Definitely a highlight of my time so far at Harvard.
Take this class! The small class size fosters a welcoming and supportive environment and the professor is very amazing! The course is also well–structured with regular but well–balanced homework.
Great class! Super chill and Agnes brings cookies and Swedish snacks sometimes! Agnes always makes it a fun and comfortable space to learn and practice Swedish!
