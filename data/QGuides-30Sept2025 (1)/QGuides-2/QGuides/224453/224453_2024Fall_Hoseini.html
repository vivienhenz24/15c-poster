What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class challenged me to think about drawing and painting in vastly different ways from before. Take it!
The best thing about this class is it will make you try a number of shorter assignments in several different mediums before you have the chance to choose one or two to focus on for longer–term projects. The immediate feedback provided during in–class assignments is very helpful!

Start your final project early –– it took a long time, and will be more rewarding and less stressful not to rush! If you're unsure about the assignments, ask for clarification and they'll fill you in
Don't let the first half of the course deter you, especially if the feedback can be more critical than what you're used to. The goal of this course is to really experiment with mediums and go outside your comfort zone. You present your work from outside this course so Maryam and Julia know your style already and are finding ways for you to expand it. I would say the second half of the course gets better because there is more freedom in what you decide to paint versus the narrow drawing prompts in the first half of the semester.
Take this class!!! Its a great, structured way to explore your artistic side, build a portfolio, or just do something new. Maryam and Julia were fantastic, and offered great assignments to sharpen your skills! I highly recommend. Only take, though, if you are willing to put the time and effort into a painting course.
Class met once a week, so the initial assignments had plenty of time to get done. Though, once the assignments were shifting to painting portion of class, things would start to piling on depending on how detailed one wants to develop their work. A break is given in the middle of class so thats nice. Most fun part was working with a range of mediums from charcoal, to acrylic, to ink, so this class would be great for someone looking to practice with various materials and have creative direction for the final project to have everything come together nicely. Chill teaching staff as well.
