This was a great class. Professor Jijon is truly wonderful. You can tell that she is just so passionate and knowledgeable about the content she teaches. She is so good at facilitating conversation and she always finds a way to turn a student's comment into a good and relevant point when they speak up in class...there are genuinely no wrong answers in her class. Easily my favourite class and professor this semester. As for the content, everything was super interesting and we read some really cool articles! If you get the chance to take a class with Professor Jijon go for it!!
Do the readings and participate!! The final paper and your research will be easier to complete if you have done the appropriate amount of reading. Professor Jijon does not just assign reading to make you miserable but to teach valuable lessons about research and the way that people view money and their morals. Because the class is 2 hours long and is discussion based, not reading makes things much worse than is necessary.

That said, everything we did in class just built up to out final essay which I was so grateful for when finals came around. I found a topic I really wanted to write about which I think was super important for getting through the writing. We also had readings each week which took up time but were manageable. Professor Jijon was absolutely amazing, super supportive, and gave incredible feedback. Overall, definitely work but I loved the class.
