This is an amazing class. Professor Liviatan might be the best teacher I've had at Harvard. She's exceptionally talented at leading conversations and helping students master the takeaways. Students were more engaged in this class than any class I've been in at Harvard.

Great reading list too. Ideal class for people interested in law, multiculturalism, sociology, political theory, and related topics.

The assignments were great, too. Prof Liviatan really encourages us to choose a topic that is personally or professionally interesting and inspiring to us. And she emphasizes presentation/multimedia skills.

I highly, highly recommend this course.
Take this course! It is pretty cool, you'll learn a lot, the readings are interesting, and prof. Liviatan is extremely engaging
The best GOV94 you can take, especially if you are interested in going to law school one day! I cannot recommend this course enough.
I definitely recommend taking this course! Dr. Liviatan is a caring and enthusiastic instructor, and has a lot of interesting perspectives on and passion for this topic. You'll definitely learn a lot from the class –– the readings are interesting and the discussions are really engaging. Her big assignments are two group presentations, one final presentation, and a meta–reflective essay on your final presentation. I think the workload for this course is certainly manageable, but it's important to stay engaged during discussions. She loves when you ground the points you make in class in evidence or thoughts from the different readings and concepts we've discussed throughout the course. Overall, the class was engaging and I developed a lot of my presentation skills (which was a refreshing change from primarily essay–based Gov classes).
This class is great. Especially if you are someone who likes to present and discuss, this is the class for you. You have to participate every class and all of the assessments are presentation–based. Other than that there are not too many assignments except for weekly readings which are sometimes long but not too bad.
This class is SUCH a good class. I could not recommend it enough. Dr. Liviatan conducts class in way that is so engaging, and the 3 hours flies by so fast. I learned so much in this class about course content, but it has changed the way I approach both my career and life at large. EVERYONE should take this class and will benefit because you learn real life skills. You will leave the class a better student, but more importantly, a better leader. 10/10 class.
This course was extremely interesting! I found the readings a bit difficult, but with practice and through unpacking them in class, it was very manageable.
Professor Liviatan is one of the most energetic and passionate teachers at Harvard. She truly cares about each student's learning rather than memorization common in other courses. As someone passionate about the legal sphere this class helped me question many facets of it. The final project allowed me to apply all the tools from the course to a policy proposal I was passionate about and although it needed a lot of research, it was research I was directing and passionate about that it made it such an interesting project. At first the course can seem theory heavy, especially if you haven't had as much experience as other students, but Professor Liviatan is open to all discussion comments and by the end of the course the connection of all the theoretical frames to specific cases and legal institutions is evident and provides you critical tools for other courses. This is a great GOV 94 to take and I highly recommend it.
