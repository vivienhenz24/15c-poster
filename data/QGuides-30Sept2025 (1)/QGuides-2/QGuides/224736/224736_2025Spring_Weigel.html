TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Weigel is truly so amazing, passionate, and funny! The course material was genuinely SO interesting, especially if you have ANY interest in media, tech, or social movements and activism. Very light/ manageable course load, and Professor Weigel is very understanding. We learned about everything from the Black Panther Party, the Young Lords, TikTok, and so much more! I really enjoyed the fact that we got to watch movies as assignments and visit the Houghton Library to view archives and media related to our course topics. I definitely wish I had contributed more to our class discussions, they were truly so insightful! We also had amazing guest speakers, one of whom worked at Facebook and Meta, and another who did research about TikTok and activism on the platform. I couldn't recommend this class more.
Prof Weigel is a true expert in these topics, has assembled an incredible syllabus, and encourages all students to discover what aspects of media and technology matter for their social change studies.

PLEASE TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Weigel and the course TF Sophie truly do care about their students and it shows. I gained so many incredible takeaways from this course and its made me so excited about possibly pursuing media studies further, despite being doubtful about that career path going into the course. I think everyone at this school should be required to take this class.
