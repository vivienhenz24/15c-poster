Take this class! Not only is it super goofy to tell people you took a course on poop, but it's actually interesting too. You learn about the history of poop recycling and waste management across the globe, and where it leaves us today. This is such a relevant course because everyone poops. Professor Howell is great and also has a sense of humor, so you're in for a good time. Readings are tough on some weeks but all in all the work is super manageable, this was such a light and fun course to finish out senior spring with.
If you have the room, this is a great elective course to take! The professor is awesome and its such a niche subject matter you can really become a master of the subject matter which feels great. It was a great experience.
Take this course! It will largely change how you think about human waste. And it is filled with laughter!
