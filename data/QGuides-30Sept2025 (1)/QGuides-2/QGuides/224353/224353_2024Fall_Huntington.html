Students will learn a lot and be challenged to use a diversity of methods to approach Buddhist traditions and images respectfully. They will get excellent practical experience in seeing Buddhist images and manuscripts up close in the museum, and learn about hidden traditions around images.
Extremely well–structuredthe support throughout the course is unparalleled. For anyone interested in learning about Buddhist art, I highly recommend taking this class with Professor Huntington. The components of the course are well integrated, not only presenting the material efficiently but also illustrating how one can work with visual archive in many ways studying art history.
Walking away I can say I'm glad that I took this course, although it might not have been my favorite. Professor can be a harsh grader sometimes and assignment rubrics can be pretty subjective. However, the topics were super interesting and I definitely learned a lot.
Take a course with Prof. Huntington. The lectures are very enjoyable, there is always space for questions and discussions and he is very open to walk you through points that are harder to understand outside of class.
