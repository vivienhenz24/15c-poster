TAKE THIS COURSE!!! I love Anbo with my whole heart he's so sweet and caring and really funny and he never makes you feel bad for messing up. This class is really just you get what you put in. If you practice ASL and work on your own and try to get consistently better you will leave this class with a wonderful breadth of knowledge and the ability to have a brief conversation with deaf people in almost any environment that it would come up in (giving directions, introductions, light small talk, etc.) If you just are there for the language requirement and you don't practice outside of class you can also do that but then the class is much less enjoyable.
