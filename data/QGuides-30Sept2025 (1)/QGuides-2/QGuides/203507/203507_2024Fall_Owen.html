Take it! ASL is a very easy language to pick up and one that I think all people should take the time to learn. The assignments aren't particularly difficult (just 2–3 writing assignments that are fairly long (2ish pages double spaced) but they aren't formal). Prof. Nora is a great instructor!
Take it! As a student who has struggled learning languages, taking ASL has been the best decision.
Different sections had different assignments and were run very differently (some classes had quizzes and a final exam, while others had a final project), so it's kind of luck of the draw about whether you get in a class that matches your preferences. Other than that, it's a great class!
Take it, everyone should! Especially with Professor Nora. They really made it a priority for us to learn.
This class is an easy way to fill your language requirement and also engage with really amazing content and world views at the same time. I think what made it easy was the fact that ASL is so different from spoken language, and it was always fun to learn, which made the learning curve feel less like a challenge and more like a project. Would definitely recommend Nora did an amazing job at running an engaging course.
Incredibly interesting, fun, and useful class. Well–paced, reasonable assignments and exams, and Nora is a great teacher.
You should absolutely take this course! The teaching staff is terrific, and the language is so fun and interesting to learn
