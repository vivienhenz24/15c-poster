If you like interesting subject matter, and are okay with spending 2 hours and 45 minutes at a big table and proceeding to be utterly bored then this is the class for you. The final paper essentially has no guidelines which gives you a lot of freedom, but if you are like me this level of freedom and lack of guidance may scare you (especially if you are not a concentrator). That being said, Professor McElroy is very responsive to email, very friendly, and very willing to explain concepts he probably thinks are common sense.
