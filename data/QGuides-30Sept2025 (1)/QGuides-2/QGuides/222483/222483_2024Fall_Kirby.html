What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I highly recommend this course! While its not one of those guaranteed "easy A" classes, it challenges you to deeply engage with one of the most overlooked and misunderstood regions in the country. Professor Kirbys thoughtful and nuanced exploration of the South made me feel seen in ways I had never experienced before at Harvard.
Learned so much from this class! Students were very engaged and clearly cared about the class material, so we always had good discussions. Got extremely helpful writing feedback from Dr. Kirby.
While this is a super interesting course, I would take it pass/fail if you are taking it as a fun elective. The grading is pretty strict and there is not a lot of lenience. However, I always enjoyed classroom discussion, however, and the topics we covered were all fascinating – that is definitely where the strength of the course lies.
Definitely recommend, especially if you aren't a southerner! But if you are a southerner, the class will most likely be full of other southerners (or at least my iteration of the course was), so you'll feel right at home. The reading and assignments are certainly manageable, and you get to go on a very impactful field trip. Just make sure that you start your final project early (or at least be thinking about it early) so you're not slammed during finals season.
