Amazing course. The readings are informative and Alisha is such a great discussion leader. If your are interested in Latin America, take this course.
This is an amazing course and I would strongly recommend it for anyone with an interest in Latin America or the politics of development. Alisha is the best professor one could ask for. She pushes us to think deeper about our arguments, cares about her students and is very generous with her time and guidance. The reading load is quite heavy but the class discussions are so enriching that it makes the whole investment super rewarding. I am so grateful I got to take this class with her.
Prof. Holland has been one of the best instructors I have had on this campus. It's a great way to get to know the canon in Latin American politics, while also seeing new research. In addition, the seminar component of the class is very engaging .
It's a great class, but if you are not familiar with the region (Latin America), I would recommend taking some time at the beginning to read up on the history of each country.
Probably the best class I've taken in the PhD. Alisha is an amazing lecturer and seminar leader, knows this material super well, and is just a great person and very generous. If you are interested in Latin American politics you should definitely take this (irrespective of your program), and if you are a grad student you might still get a lot from this if you are interested in topics like political economy and representation.
Alisha is an incredible teacher, I liked the class dynamic. There's a lot to read, but definitely worth it!

I learned a lot about Latin America's development, institutions, and politics. Also about political science methods, reading qualitative papers, crafting arguments, creating diagrams. It was super helpful for my research, and got a nice project out of it, in great part thanks to Alisha's detailed feedback throughout the course.
Professor Holland is an incredibly kind, enthusiastic and accommodating professor, who cultivates an environment of rich and respectful class discussions. She has an astonishing knowledge of the subject matter, and is great at explaining it.
