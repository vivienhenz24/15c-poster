This is a great course! The lectures are well–paced and the assignments are directly applicable to actual exoplanets research.
definitely take this class if you want to understand astronomy at a high level. prof john johnson is an excellent professor. he always asked what we wanted to learn about in the course, was very helpful at office hours, and had 5min breaks in lecture so we could get up, walk around etc. he is also one of the most successful people in his field. the material is super interesting and johnson explains it in a way that is detailed, yet not too hard to comprehend. this course isn't really that hard, if you pay attention in lecture you will be ok. but, johnson does NOT do lecture notes so you MUST come to class. a lot of the learning in this class is done in a group setting, it's very discussion–based. this is nice because sometimes one person in your group will understand the material better than others, and can teach the other students (and vice versa). also helps for forming friendships so you can collab on psets
