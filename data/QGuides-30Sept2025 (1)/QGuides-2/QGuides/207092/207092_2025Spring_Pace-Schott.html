This class is interesting in terms of its material and it gives you a lot of freedom to explore topics that you might be interested in and their connection to sleep. The final projects spanned a variety of different fields and they were really interesting to listen to. However, the class itself was boring, in my opinion. It is a 2–hour seminar where the professor just clicks through a slideshow and there are very few opportunities for participation. I did not find it engaging.
This is a great class even if you don't have much neuroscience knowledge! You'll learn a lot and a huge amount of that information will be applicable to your own life and habits. The workload isn't stressful while also not being boring.
Loved this course! There were weekly quizzes but these were graded on completion (also open book) and the only other big assignments were the presentation at the end of class, and open–book 24hr quiz, and two short writing assignments. But this topic is very interesting and I would recommend this course if you're into sleep!
This class literally helps you sleep better. As a college student who still has 2 years left, you learn about a lot of things that can help improve your sleep. The material covered in the course is really interesting and you learn a lot about your body and your mind and how to sleep healthily.
