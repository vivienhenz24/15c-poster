Comments
The class sounds really interesting from the course information, but it just doesn't really achieve anything that special. I found it to often feel monotonous and boring.
A solid course for those interested in urban studies. I had little knowledge going in – and it seemed like a lot of my peers were a bit more acquainted. But honestly I feel like I caught up pretty fast! I was talking with a friend of mine who majors in Urban Studies at Brown, and it seems like we covered a lot of the most important texts.
I really enjoyed this course. It gives a really good background to the various theories of urban politics, and then applies these theories to several different key issues facing cities today, such as housing, climate, and racial equity. Professor Weitz is very knowledgeable about the subject matter and also is very kind and understanding. I would recommend this course to anyone interested in public policy.
