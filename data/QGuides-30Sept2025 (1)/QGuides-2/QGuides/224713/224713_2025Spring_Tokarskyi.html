This class is a standout!! Its amazing how fast two hours can fly by when youre genuinely enjoying what youre learning. Bohdan is an incredible professor who puts so much work into selecting quality content. I loved that this class prioritized quality over quantity and we had less readings but with more substance. The assignments for this class were creative and fun and often deepened my understanding of myself. Could not recommend this class enough!!!!!!!!!!!!!
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! You dont need a background in poetry or philosophy. This course is about how we think about what it means to live in the world around us as ourselves. This course helped me rethink what I want out of my experience at this school and in my life. Part of the reason I changed my major. The PERFECT course to start your Harvard experience with – in a campus full of strivers, its a great reminder on how to be yourself instead of who others think you should be. I came away from it with new friends I look forward to seeing on campus, and I looked forward to it every week.
Prof Bohdan is great and creates a great environment for learning. The field trip was super fun as well!
DEFINITELY TAKE THIS FIRST–YEAR SEMINAR!! Come to this class with an open mind and a willingness to be vulnerable. This course will ask you to reflect honestly on your identity and what it means to be authentic, which can be challenging but also incredibly rewarding. Take advantage of the small class size to build genuine connections with your peers, as those conversations are some of the richest parts of the course. Embrace the creative assignmentstheyre designed to help you express complex ideas in new ways and deepen your understanding of the readings. Through this seminar I learned that authenticity is not about perfection or a static ideal; its a journey of self–discovery that this class helps you explore thoughtfully and poetically.
