Be ready to read a lot!
Professor Roxburgh's class is challenging but well worth it. I would recommend this course to any who are even remotely interested in the arts of the Islamic book as well as the Qur'an as an art object. You can survive without Arabic, but any Arabic training will definitely prove useful!
Take at least one course with Professor Roxburgh if you get the chance! Having some previous experience with Arabic was very helpful/made me enjoy the class more (but I know other students who did not have a background in Arabic who also enjoyed/could be successful in the course).
Any class with Professor Roxburgh has been the best. He is so knowledgable (encyclopedic even) for his areas of research. It is a treat to hear a lecture from him. Be prepared to do a lot of difficult readings. The first few weeks may be rough, but they get significantly easier as you get used to the style of learning.
