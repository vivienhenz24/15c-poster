Start your research paper early!! You’ll get so much more out of the experience that way. And come to class ready to talk about the readings because they’re truly so interesting and it’s so much having a dedicated safe and stimulating space to discuss them with Bo–Mi and the class.
If you like interacting with various theories, learning about different factors that affect society, such as technology, and don't mind an unstructured experience, you should take this course. The readings are foundational, yet fresh. You won't have a sense of your grade in the course, but you will sense intellectual development. Bo–Mi leads a solid tutorial and is great to talk to.
this is a really cool course! you probably won't get to discuss the subject matter in another class, and the readings are excellent. I do wish that we discussed politics + tech more (only spent one week on it, felt like it could've been two).
