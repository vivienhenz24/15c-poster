This is a pretty chill class. Even though there are 11 psets, all of them are very chill. They can all be completed within 1 hour during office hours. The content is also interesting. The most interesting proof (that I feel like) was proving Pascal's Theorem using Algebraic Geometry. If you enjoy projective geometry/method of moving points/complex bashing in math olympiads, then you should definitely take this class, you will greatly enjoy it!
The material is definitely pretty confusing to learn for the first time, but if you're thoughtful about studying then you'll end up understanding it, and once you understand it it's interesting
An essential class for one who is interested in math.
This years instructor did not do the class justice. I was left confused after most lectures, lectures were messy, and the instructor oftentimes used different terms for the same concept. The psets were interesting, but I never got the big picture of the class.
