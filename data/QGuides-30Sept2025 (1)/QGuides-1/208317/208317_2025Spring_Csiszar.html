Comments
This is a great course that introduces you to new ways of thinking about information and information technologies! Professor Csiszar really cares about tailoring the course to student interests. Class is very discussion based and often led to very insightful conversations. Assignments were very flexible and give a lot of room to explore topics you are interested in. I highly recommend!
Take this class! The course content is interesting and relevant for current affairs. Professor Csiszar is very passionate in this class, and you'll both get to challenge and learn a lot about the development of information as fact or objectivity, which we often take for granted. Assignments are a great place to critically explore your own interests as they relate to these topics.
This is a fantastic class covering lots of timely topics, and Professor Csiszar is the best!
Mixed opinions about this class. It was pretty theoretical and the readings could get dry at times and I wish there had been a bit more structure in class. That said, you really get out what you put in. If you do the readings and come ready to engage, class discussions can actually be interesting and rewarding. But you could also kind of coast through if you wanted to. Its not a class that forces you to keep up, so it depends on how much you want to take from it.
