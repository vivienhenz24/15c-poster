I definitely recommend the course it was intellectually stimulating and since its not graded I think it makes focusing on learning the subject for the sake of interest easier. As someone who does immunology primarily I think learning more about neuroimmune interactions was fascinating and it opened a new field for me to explore in the future.
This is a fantastic course! Each class session starts with a lecture by a different guest speaker professor and then concludes with a paper presentation/discussion. The lectures by the guest speakers are all really interesting and each cover a different area of neuroimmune interactions in health and disease, together providing a varied and broad look into this area. The paper presentations are also interesting for reading in detail about particular examples of studies in the areas described in the lectures. Overall this course was very interesting and informative. Definitely a great course to take if you are interested in neuroimmune interactions in health and disease!
