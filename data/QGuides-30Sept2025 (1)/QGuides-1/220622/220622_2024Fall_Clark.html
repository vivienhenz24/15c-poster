What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Great opportunity to develop solo vocal repertoire with a vocal coach, subsidized by the generosity of the Holden Voice Program. Incredibly nice that you get to perform in the acoustic space of Sanders and get a recording too. Communication for the course was a bit muddy but it seems like they were trying something new. Assignments could use some more specificity and unclear how the actual jury performances get graded.
I would deifnitely take it! It's an awesome opportunity and you won't be disappointed. It's something I'd definitely consider continuing.
I would only recommend this class to people who have an extremely flexible schedule. Because the meeting times and days change so much, it can be difficult to accommodate all of the meeting times.
They're still fleshing out what this class looks like, so they're not entirely sure of the structure and requirements yet. Maybe the class will get better with time. That being said, getting vocal lessons is always great and you can learn a lot about different styles of singing. It can be great to get some individualized attention if you're used to singing in a group.
After this course I became a singer. I have sung before in choir but always felt the sensation that other beautiful voices cover my ugly voice. I had moments of confidence, but they would always fade away. Now I can strongly affirm that I truly am a singer, and a good one! Having been recorded as well and after having shown it to my family and gotten good reactions, I also feel like a singer in their eyes too. It is truly amazing since this is a very old dream of mine. Learning from my teacher has been inspiring and having so much help and encouragement from the staff has helped me grow. I will DEFINITELY take this course again!!
Fun addition to Glee Club. Took vocal lessons. Performed two songs. Would recommend to anyone wanting to really improve their vocal technique.
This is an easy way to get credit for singing, and definitely encourages you to improve as a singer more than taking voice lessons without credit and without a jury.
Take this if you want to start / continue voice lessons and receive credit / a grade!
Take the time to rehearse outside of class and vocal lessons, and you will be very satisfied with your improvements as a musician.
Take this course if you are already taking RCS, Glee Club, or Collegium. This course gives an opportunity to perform which is nice. There were a few issues with the course like how many songs we had to sing for jury. This was likely because this was the first year this course was offered, but it wasn't communicated well. There were many emails sent to us, changing the number of songs/requirements. Also, there is a syllabus, but it is very vague. Overall take the course. I imagine, they will fix these problems in the second year of this course.
