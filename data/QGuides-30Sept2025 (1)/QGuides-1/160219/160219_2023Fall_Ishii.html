What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! We get to watch movies every Tuesday night and then judge them together the next day and talk about why certain natural–disaster events are unrealistic. We also have class–related games every week with candy prizes, and sometime we do cool projects. My favorite part of the class was a homework assignment where we were split into teams and had to design the best system to defend an island against storm surges, taking into account cost, appearance, effectiveness, etc., and then we presented it to boards of fake island residents. It was so cool!!
This was my favorite class this semester! I feel so much more knowledgeable about everything natural disaster, and it was a perfect opportunity to explore some of my interests in natural science while also having fun!
This class was easily my favorite class that I took this semester. The content taught in the course was very interesting to learn about, especially if you like learning about natural disasters. The movies that are shown, even if they're a bit cheesy, are unironically really good. The Tuesday evening movie night quickly became the highlight of my week. The assignments aren't very difficult. A lot of the class activities are a lot of fun, and do a job of helping us learn content. The atmosphere of the class is very welcoming, and it had a lovely community. I can't recommend this course enough.
Take good notes during the movies! The more active you are in watching the movie the easier the assignment is for the week (and the more likely you are to win candy in class)
