TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor is awesome :)
This class is amazing! If you have any interest in clinical psychology, this course is great to learn more about the field and what clinical psychology is. Prof. Lauren is amazing, she is so patient and makes every class great to be in! She is also super accessible outside of class, and gives great feedback on assignments!
Would definitely recommend the class. It is generally pretty low stakes and very informative and practical. The information is easy to digest and the professor frequently brings her own knowledge and experience to the table. Mindfulness exercises are a nice way to start every class.
You will learn so much in this course, but you may not be able to participate in discussions in the way you want to. Students in this course were actively told to stop raising their hands if they were speaking too much. Lauren is super nice, and she will provide you with all the information you could ever want about a career in psychology, but you might not be able to get to know her very well. With all this in mind, the workload is very normal for an advanced psych seminar, and this would be a great opportunity to learn applicable knowledge that you won't get in other courses.
Take this class!! Dr. Santucci is an amazing teacher, and you will learn so much about clinical psych without it ever feeling overwhelming. The workload for the class is quite light, but the final essay gives you a chance to delve deeper into a topic of your choosing. I would especially recommend this class to anyone interested in clinical psych as a career–– Harvard has very few opportunities to study applications of psych and this was an excellent experience.
