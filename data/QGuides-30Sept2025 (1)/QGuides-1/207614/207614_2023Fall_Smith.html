It is most helpful if you are working on some sort of writing over the course of the semester.
The coursework was not difficult but since most of the assignments are related to a final deliverable of a paper/statement/proposal it is a situation where you entirely get out what you put it.

I think this course would be very helpful for any scientist who wants to communicate effectively or who wants to lessen the intimidation of writing some work up.
I even found that the process of writing the paper as laid out in the course was helpful for me to plan future research efforts and streamline my day–to–day work as a graduate student.
