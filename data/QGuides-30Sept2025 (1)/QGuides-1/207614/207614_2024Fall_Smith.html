A wonderful course to take with great instructors! While not every part of the course might be relevant, I would say they were all useful. Plus you get to just work on one of your own writing assignments – definitely take advantage of this course! Not a huge time commitment either!
Definitely take this class! Suzanne is wonderful, and you will gain so much practical knowledge throughout the semester about different topics (submitting publications, fellowships/grants, preparing for faculty jobs, mentoring, etc.). The workload is pretty chill, especially if you already have a piece of writing you're working on.
This class has a low workload relative to the benefits gained from the class and the skills and resources gained. I would highly recommend.
This course was a good motivator to start my manuscript and make good progress on it. Without the course, I wouldn't have made progress on my research and it helped me hone my research path.
This class is really a broad introduction to scientific writing–– big ideas and approaches. The class is less focused on granular details like style, and more on familiarizing yourself with the breadth of scientific writing. Suzanne is super compassionate and has great insight on everything. She will give you as much attention as you need, in and out of class. Workload is light, but effective. Appreciate that this class is supplementary to my work and not a huge time suck. Would recommend, but know that if you have a lot of English writing experience some of the content may be review/very familiar.
