This in depth GIS course is thrilling not only because of its hands–on component but because of the support of Professor Ur and Jeff Blossom, who complement each other well in offering both technical and theoretical advice on any kind of Geospatial project. I thoroughly recommend, even if one doesn't work with archaeological data.
The course was extremely useful in teaching you how to operate the ArcGIS program. Take this course if you are interested in GIS or think you might use it in future projects!!! Professor Ur and Jeff Blossom are incredibly helpful and will sit down and help you for as long as it takes until you understand. I loved this class
