What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The Haitian language is quite different from European languages, even though it shares a lot of etymological roots and may sound quite French at times. It is a very rewarding experience learning how to think differently in this new language. So many of its grammatical and idiomatic formulations are incredibly elegant and concise. It is not a language which wastes words. Patrick was an excellent instructor this term and will be missed next year, although we will be continuing to use his materials with our new instructor, Roosevelt.
If you aim to turn ur audible understanding of Haitian Creole into a full comprehension, take this course. you will be immersed in Haitian culture through food, language, and history.
Professor Sylvain is amazing! He creates all his own content and genuinely cares about improving your skills in the language! I absolutely loved this class, and it was my favorite on my schedule this semester!
