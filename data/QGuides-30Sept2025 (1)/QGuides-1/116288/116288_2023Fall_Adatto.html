This is a great junior tutorial. Prof. Adatto is extremely bright and very helpful with the final project. The class covers a wide range of topics while maintaining common threads throughout, creating a syllabus in which everybody can find something of interest. The readings were numerous but shorter and less dense than other tutorials, and every unit had connections to current events that made the discussion really engaging.
This is a great tutorial! There is a nice balance of theory, history, and current events throughout the course readings. Kiku is also an amazing professor. She does a great job stimulating discussion in class and including everyone in the conversation. She also provides really helpful feedback on drafts and is always happy to engage with students outside of class. Take this class!
This course is wonderful and Kiku is a great teacher. I would highly recommend this course as the material is interesting and it touches on all facets of life. Kiku also places an emphasis on sharing work with the whole class – you send all of your writing to everyone – which I think was very beneficial for my writing. The only reason I would consider not recommending this class is if you are looking for a lot of scaffolding from a 98 tutorial. There is not that much scaffolding involved which works for some people, but doesn't work for others.
Take this course!!! Kiku creates such an amazing learning environment and facilitates great conversations. She also is an amazing advisor, providing thoughtful and insightful guidance throughout the research process! The readings in this class and the case studies discussed are super relevant and interesting! One of my favorite seminars
Take this course!! One of the best Social Studies tutorials. The topic/material is so interesting and grapples with a number of important contemporary issues. Prof. Adatto is a phenomenal professor and drives discussion very well. The course load is very reasonable and well organized.
