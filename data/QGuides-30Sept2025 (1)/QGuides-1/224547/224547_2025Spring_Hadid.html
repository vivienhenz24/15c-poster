This class with Tala Hadid and Madison Howard (TF) is incredible. The mini assignments we have before working on our final films help a lot in developing our understanding of the filmmaking process with low stakes and super helpful feedback. They do a really great job of showing us a variety of films. And theyre super helpful as we work through the process of making our final films. Both Tala and Madison are incredibly kind and passionate about their work. If youre interested or curious about filmmaking in any capacity (fiction or not) definitely enroll in this course.

You learn the basic fundamentals of cinematography, sound, editing, and color correction while also learning how to best execute the story you want to tell. Id say go into the class having an open mind and some ideas of what film you want to make.

The last thing Ill say is Tala brings incredible directors and producers as guests to speak with the class. If for nothing else, having the opportunity to speak to people who work in the industry full time is pretty awesome especially if thats what you want to do.
If you're interested in filmmaking, I highly recommend this course. The early assignments give you the opportunity to build your skills with using the camera and other tech and then you apply these to your final project, which could be about anything you'd like. I chose to make a film that I was really passionate about so it turned out to be a passion project. We got multiple rounds of feedback from our professor, Tala, and TF, Madison (who were absolutely incredible!) which allowed us to refine our films.
Take this class! Tala is a skilled filmmaker with excellent in her expertise.

Tala and Madison were brilliant and gave wonderful feedback throughout the filming and editing process. It was a tremendous introduction to film, both critically and practically. The course was a highlight of my semester. A great class where I feel like I began developing a lot of useful skills take it!
