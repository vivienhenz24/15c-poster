Comments
Don't be afraid to ask for help, or what a word means, or how to say a word. It's all about learning the best you can. For compositions, first think of the story and maybe write it in English, then translate (could be easier than trying to write in French off the bat as you think of the story).
Take this class! Wonderful teaching because you'll likely be in a section with only 10 other people, so your TF will really get to know you. Great engagement with language, and I loved the virtual reality portion of the class, where you get to use the VR headsets in Cabot Library to see the scenes of Paris yourself.
