The course itself was pretty cool with a wide range of readings so that you are bound to be interested in some of them.
I would definitely take this course again! Dr. Thornber is a truly knowledgeable lecturer, and she is also really good at identifying texts that resonate with course themes. She is receptive and perceptive. On top of that, the students who take this course are all heavily involved in the field of comparative literature and know how to talk about course topics in meaningful ways.
