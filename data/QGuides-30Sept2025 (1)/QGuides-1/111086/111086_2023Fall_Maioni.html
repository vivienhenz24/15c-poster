This is undoubtedly the best class I have ever taken. This is a true gem of a course and courses like these are what make a Harvard education so worth it. Professor Maioni is just an unparalleled professor. She is both kind and empathetic to her students, and leads the class with brilliance, poise, and a real commitment to helping her students understand healthcare. Additionally, some of the coolest speakers I have seen at this school were guest speakers in this course and Professor Maioni facilitated incredible discussions with each and every one of them. This course does take some time—it is a lot of reading and a paper summary each week in addition to other assignments—but it is worth every hour you put into this course and Professor Maioni provides thoughtful and timely feedback on every assignment you submit in this course. This course made me really grateful to be a student at Harvard where we have access to courses like this and I can only hope that I will have an opportunity to take a course like this again.
This was truly one of the coolest classes I've ever taken. It had so many interactive elements that made it an unforgettable experience.
Take this course! Professor Maioni is a tough grader and definitely will push you, but you will learn so much during this course it makes it worth it.
Professor Maioni is knowledgeable, kind, and enthusiastic. Her comments and conversations throughout the course were wonderful, and our small class size also helped facilitate an environment of creative learning. I loved the variety in this course, from guest speakers, to teaching my own class one week, to a completely personalized final research essay. I cannot recommend this course enough – I feel like I have shifted my career path because of it!
GOV 2114 a very engaging class! As a premedical student, I never thought I would take a government class, but I don't regret it a single bit! I think it is one of the most rewarding classes I have taken at Harvard. Dr. Maioni is so sweet and is an amazing professor. She really is there to support you! She gives constructive feedback on assignments and makes sure you are improving in your writing, critical thinking, and understanding on the content! Definitely take a class with her!
