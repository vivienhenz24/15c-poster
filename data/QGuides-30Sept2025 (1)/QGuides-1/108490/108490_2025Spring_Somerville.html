Amazing course, I would highly recommend that you take it when it is brought back!
I would recommend this class to anyone–– whether as an elective or if you need a PSY credit. I learned so much about emotions and about people in general. This course fostered a greater understanding of intervention strategies anyone can use to foster greater emotional resilience, clinical perspectives on therapeutic treatments, and more on the neurobiological basis of certain emotional expressions.
This course had really interesting lectures, manageable assignments, and reasonably difficult exams. Its content will also be super relevant to your life, especially its discussion of emotion regulation strategies! If you have any interest in emotion science, I'd highly recommend it.
Take this course! It is really fun to learn about emotions and theories behind them.
I think this is my favorite class I've ever taken at Harvard (coming from a non–psych concentrator senior!). I really enjoyed all the lectures and research we looked at. The workload is very chill and the quizzes are fair + open note. I also found the section discussion questions to be very thought provoking and fun to discuss, and I'm someone that usually never spoke in section until this class. Not sure if this class is being offered next year, but you MUST take it when it is.
Def take this class! Prof is super sweet and final paper topics are flexible so you can write about whatever you are interested in.
AMAZING CLASS! BEST CLASS I HAVE TAKEN HERE SO FAR. Just pay attention in class (and do readings for extra knowledge), do the assignments, participate in section, and review the notes before the exam and you will do absolutely amazing in this course.
