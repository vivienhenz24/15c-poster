This was a transformative course!! Professor Cohen has truly changed the trajectory of my academic career. I have never had an educator more invested in my personal goals and research interests. Professor Cohen taught us everything she knew and was always accessible to us. She gave stellar feedback on all assignments and went above and beyond always. I am so fortunate and honored to have been in this class, and I truly do not know where I would be without this course experience under her mentorship. She would sent articles and relevant documents through email throughout the semester, and she was so invested in each of our projects! Additionally, Nora Spicer was truly the best TF for this course. She is one of the best writers I have met and was the most accessible person for meetings and office hours. From the minor to major parts of editing, she really helped me develop into a stronger writer and thinker. I appreciate both of them so much, and I am eternally grateful. From the bottom of my heart, thank you, thank you, thank you!
Course material is super interesting. There are assignments every week. Grading is very difficult so you need to put a ton of effort into every assignment.
it is very challenging. the biggest lesson is learning how to learn, and also what to care about. You may just not be able to do a great job reading every single word of the readings, and there will come a point where the paper just has to be submitted. it will not be perfect and you may not get a a great grade. but you'll learn and that's what it is about.
I would tell future students to not become discouraged by this class because you will gain many takeaways from the course if you stick with it and not become discouraged by the harsh comments or feedback––it's just solely to help you hone your writing and become a more analytical thinker.
This is a great class. Professor Cohen is an amazing professor, a talented historian who is able to bring her own experiences to the class in order to help students improve. It is a lot of writing, but is broken down into manageable chunks.
Professor Cohen is fantastic and Nora gives great feedback on writing. This class can be a lot of work and you have to commit to it if you want to get the most out of the class.
This class is a lot of work. You will be writing for this class most weeks and each paper kind of builds on the last so its very intense with no real breaks. That said, your writing will improve a lot. Mine certainly did and Im very grateful for that. Also, I feel much more comfortable writing longer papers now. I had never written anything longer than 10 pages before this class and suddenly, the 16 page final paper felt like a breeze and I definitely could have written more. Biggest tip for the class is to go to office hours with your prof and TF and to start working on your drafts as early as possible so that you can get better feedback in tutorial. You will thank yourself for this big time. One last gripe is that this class is NOT really a history class. Your teachers wont teach you much about history at all. It is mostly just an intensive writing course. I think overall, this class is very good. I didnt like it all the time, but it is well structured with lots of support and I am grateful I took it.
