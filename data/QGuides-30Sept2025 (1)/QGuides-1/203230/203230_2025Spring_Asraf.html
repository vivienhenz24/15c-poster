What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Greek 10 is a wonderful way to secure your Greek and get more fluent, especially if the texts are as interesting and fun as they were this semester. Sarah is wonderful––she really cares about her students and has great insights into reception especially, but is also very helpful with and good at explaining grammar.
Most people who take this course will probably need it for their language requirement. It's a really fun, collaborative, and informative class if you put in the time. Read the assigned sections before class and review them after and you'll do great.
Greek 10, as with all other languages classes, will depend greatly on your instructor. Sarah was an absolutely wonderful instructor who managed to pace the class very well (a very hard thing to do when reading Greek with multiple students). She also took the initiative to organise multiple extra review sessions and provided us with handouts that supplemented our in–class discussions. The final project was also well–planned and engaging! Overall, it was a very productive semester and if you want to have a fruitful time learning Greek in the least painful way possible, this is the way to go!
