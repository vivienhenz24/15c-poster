This is a great class! It covers a wide range of topics, and the amount of information can feel overwhelming at times, but the topics built upon each other nicely and made things make a lot of sense.
This is a great course if you're interested in stratigraphy and Earth history! The class size is small with a good mix of undergrads and grad students, and the learning environment is very open and seminar–like. Definitely expect some unfamiliar topics (would recommend taking another Earth history class before), but the course staff are all super eager to help. The workload is reasonable, and the field trip/final project are great for gaining experience in data collection and field work.
This is an incredible EPS class!! It is the first time Prof. Drabon was teaching it, and it was incredible! Exams fair (a bit challenging, but due to the large amount of content very fair). Lectures were interesting and the content manageable with studying. Oyku is an incredible TF and Nadja an incredible professor!! Highly recommend this class as an EPS elective!
