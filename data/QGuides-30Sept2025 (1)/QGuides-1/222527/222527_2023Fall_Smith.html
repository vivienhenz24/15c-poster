This is a fun class to take at Harvard and obviously just an amazing opportunity; however, the workshop will never go too far in depth and its pretty low commitment but absolutely take it if you get in
Prof Smith is so inspiring as a discussion leader and will completely shape your thoughts on what poetry is, how it works, why it's important. She speaks so beautifully about poetry, and the class is worth it just to hear from her and be in dialogue with her. The syllabus is wonderful too. Having a pre–existing creative practice in another medium might enrich your experience in the class, particularly for assignments which encourage you to break form, but it's not necessary.
I learned so much about poetry and poetic strategies. I really enjoyed the immersive nature of the course and learning with my classmates throughout
Tracy is a phenomenal professor and workshop leader, and this class was such a nice reprieve from the rest of my courseload. I really felt that I grew close with my fellow workshoppers and I learned so much from them. If this class isn't offered in the future, please still apply for any of Tracy's workshops! You definitely won't regret it.
