I really enjoyed this course. The subject material is actually so interesting, I found myself bringing up things we talked about in class in conversations with friends and family all the time. Its very easy to pay attention in class because the content is so engaging. The papers are short and pretty easy once you get the hang of the format. The quizzes are more difficult, but you can plead your case for your answers after the fact.
Really great class – learned a lot. It was really cool seeing the parallels between humans and mammals' behavior and this had some philosophical implications too.
Very chill class. Prof Benenson is enthusiastic about the work, and there are lots of niche facts you can learn. The final project was also an engaging exercise that allowed students to choose and catalogue a mammalian behavior "in the wild"
Take this class!! Joyce is the best professor with such a passion for the subject! The class is not hard, and Joyce really cares about her students and their wellbeing. Workload is low with a few 2 page papers that are not hard at all. Instructions and example papers are given to help you structure them, so it is very easy to get a 100 on each paper. I genuinely enjoyed going to this class, and taking it with a friend or two makes it all the better!!This was the first HEB or science class i have ever taken and it was not daunting or overwhelming in the slightest. take it!!!
I would tell future students to prepare heavily for the last quiz because it is weighted at about a quarter of the whole grade.

But the professor is really interesting, the lectures are pretty fun and chill, and she's pretty helpful and lenient. Take it pass/fail and you'll probably have a good time.
