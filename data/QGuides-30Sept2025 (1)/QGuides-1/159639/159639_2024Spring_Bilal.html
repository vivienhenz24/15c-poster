Good class, don't stress, lots of work, but you learn a lot
This was not a good class. Topics were haphazard, not motivated, and lacking any coherent narrative. I do not understand any "general principles" from macroeconomics, I feel like I just learned details about the proofs in the appendices of Adrien and Xavier's papers, but nothing fit together. Topics in the first half especially were often too difficult to learn anything useful from.
I highly recommend this class – it is very interesting and rewarding. It is fairly challenging, so be ready to spend time studying home and doing interesting homework assignments. The main strengths of the course is that it devotes enough attention to empirical facts, while teaching essential macroeconomic models.
