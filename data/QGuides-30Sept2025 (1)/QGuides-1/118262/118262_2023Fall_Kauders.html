What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
It is definitely easier than 20, but it may not be worth the extra semester of expos that it comes with.
This class is great if you are feeling unsure or not too confident about your writing skills as you enter college. However, the course is paced slower, so it is easy to not become too engaged with it until the paper deadlines come up. Make use of office hours with the perceptor as they are very helpful when writing papers. Hannah is great with feedback during one–on–one conferences and does a good job of guiding the class along when it gets too quiet.
I would tell future students to not procrastinate and give yourself time when writing assignments.
