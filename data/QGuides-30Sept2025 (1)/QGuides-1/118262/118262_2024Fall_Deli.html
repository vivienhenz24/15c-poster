What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I highly recommend taking Expos 10. If you are considering taking Expos 10 before Expos 20, I strongly encourage you to do so. Since taking this course, I have seen significant improvements in my writing. It has also boosted my confidence in my writing abilities, especially since I had very little experience with academic writing before coming to Harvard. Dr. Deli was a fantastic preceptor, and I am glad I was in her class this semester.
Take the course if you really feel like your writing skills were lacking before college. However, if you have taken any AP or IB writing courses, you should just go straight ahead to Expos 20. If not, you will feel bored and be given way too much annoying busy work throughout the week for a simple writing course. In the end, you might regret that you took an extra semester of writing when, in reality, you were capable of Expos 20. The reading material is easily digestible and is a good source for your papers. Finally, depending on your professor, you'll get different grades out of papers; some grade much more harshly than others, so be cautious of that.
Work with your instructor and read the class materials to understand.
