I would highly recommend this class!! Profesor Ofek is wonderful and encourages engaging and interesting conversations every week. On average, I spent one hour a week outside of class for this course. I came into the class with little to no knowledge about marketing. But, I now am even more interested in the topic and I really enjoyed each case study we looked at. This is a great Freshmen Seminar and I would differently recommend it to any student interested in marketing!
