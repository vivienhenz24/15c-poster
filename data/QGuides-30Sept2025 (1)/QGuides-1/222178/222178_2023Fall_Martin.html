Take this course if offered! You will learn so much, grow as a writer, and benefit greatly from the experience. The grading is more than fair, and the entire teaching is very responsive to questions.
10/10 class. It's a must–take if you're even remotely interested. This is everything a war history class should be. Weekly work load is maneagable, and assignments and final exam are very logical and rewarding. You will learn about battles and some leaders, but war is so much more than that; and this class is too. You also learn about the lived experience of soldiers and civilians, the war economies, the lesser–known stories and fronts, and the legacy of such a pivotal war. Prof Martin is an excellent, engaging, and expert lecturer. Couldn't recommend it more.

Really makes you rethink how you think about history and conflicts in the world today. If you're looking for a real history leture, take this.

TAKE THIS CLASS!!!!!! I absolutely loved this class. Professor Martin is amazing and so passionate about the subject. I came into the class knowing nothing about WWI and was unsure how I would like it, but it ended up being my favorite class so far. The lectures are so engaging and so interesting. Professor Martin makes even the more boring topics seem like the most fascinating things ever. Even if you are not super interesting in the war, you learn about so much more than just the battles. Professor Martin discusses a wide array of topics and includes information about all different groups of people. You will learn not just about 1914–1918, but the years before and after, and even more about the war's legacy today. I highly, highly recommend.
