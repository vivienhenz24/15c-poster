Worthwhile class in Stat. Not a crazy workload, but topics/exams sometimes difficult.
Overall, yes take it. The primary con is that it is just really boring (in my opinion). Lectures are just listening to the professor talk through slides with math on them. That being said, this course is significantly easier than stat 110 or 111; weekly problem sets are only a few questions and aren't too time consuming, and exams (primarily multiple choice) are very doable if you pay attention to the content. Won't be the most fun class you take at Harvard by any means, but a low–stress option to knock out a requirement for sure!
