I wouldn't recommend that undergrads take it unless explicitly given permission by both Sam and Adi; it's a course designed to give graduate students the practical knowledge to one day (hopefully) become finance professors.
This is a fun courseinstructors and TF are very flexible; content is helpful and interesting. The only thing is that the pace of lecture can be a bit fast, particularly at sustained length (3hrs).
This class is absolutely amazing. I took it as an undergrad because I'm planning on getting a PhD, and it was so incredibly useful it was crazy. Definitely difficult and challenging, but in a way that was still fun and lighthearted. Absolutely take this if you can!!
Take this course! Whether you're an econometrics–aficionado or just learned some of the methods in previous courses to use in applied work, you will have covered the entire core of econometrics after taking this class. Since the curriculum is ambitious, you will learn enough to become dangerous and have obtained a solid foundation to build upon in future studies. Sam and Adi make a great pair, and the classes are almost always fun, even during the dryest of subjects.
