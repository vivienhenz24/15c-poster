This is a really wonderful class if you have any interest in education policy or change. Professor Gottlieb is thoughtful and deliberate with how she presents material to the class, encouraging us to think deeply about our own experiences and contextualize them among both good and bad examples of how education is treated in the US. The discussions are also incredibly thought–provoking, as we all are bringing different experiences to the table and sharing how we think about education because of them.
