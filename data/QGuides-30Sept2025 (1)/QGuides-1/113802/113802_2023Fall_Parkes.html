TAKE IT! YOU WILL LOVE IT!
This is an awesome class! Completely manageable workload (pretty easy online homeworks 4x per week, a quiz or test every other week, and 3 fairly short writing assignments per semester) and awesome teaching staff. It feels a little fast from the start if you're like me and have absolutely no knowledge of German going in, but you catch on pretty quick.
If you're thinking about taking German, you should definitely do it! Though it is not the easiest language, there is so much support to help you get it in the end. Be patient with yourself and keep working until you get it and do not be afraid to ask for help.
