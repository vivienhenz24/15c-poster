This course was absolutely amazing and allowed for great discussion and a (VERY) small class environment. Nick is a great professor and an unexpectedly hard grader (with office hours and communication though it's fine) but it truly so knowledgeable about the topic and navigates the course well. Just be prepared for lots of reading and since it is a smaller class you should be prepared to participate a lot.
This was one of my favorite classes this semester. The readings were very interesting, and we covered a lot of diverse subjects within U.S. history. The workload was manageable with a midterm paper, final paper, reading responses, and leading a class discussion.
This is a great class! The class flows really well, the content is important and interesting, and Professor Bloom is a great teacher who guides discussion well and is very helpful and approachable.
