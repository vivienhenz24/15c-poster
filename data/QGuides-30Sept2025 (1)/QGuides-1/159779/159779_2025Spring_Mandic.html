Overall, I highly recommend this course to anyone interested in political sociology or looking for a class that makes you think critically about the world around you.

TAKE THIS COURSE!!! Political Sociology is the most accessible and most enjoyable course I've taken in the Sociology department! Danilo (prof) and Kyle (TF) were exceptional instructors who created an effective, open, and enriching learning environment. The readings cover such a broad range of topics that there is certainly something for everyone taking the course (and Danilo begins the course by asking how he can specify the course lectures to students' interests). Danilo delivers each lecture with such enthusiasm that I always looked forward to the (9am!) meeting times. Danilo also invited a range of guest speakers who came with new perspectives on the week's theme. In section, Kyle broke down the difficult theoretical concepts and added his own insights, making the denser readings much more understandable to those with no prior sociology experience. Kyle also put effort into making sure we knew how to read sociological/academic papers and covered data literacy in one section meeting. Really wonderful experience overall!

Professor Mandi is great and he makes sure to give you a lot of examples to understand information one of my favorite courses this past semester.
Take this course no matter your concentration, you will learn something.
