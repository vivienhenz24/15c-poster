Take this class!! I took BX and 123XB the past two semesters, and I cannot recommend continuing Chinese and taking 130XA enough. The workload is very spaced out and manageable, while you learn a lot of Chinese covering important political, social, and cultural topics. The teachers are amazing too and it really starts to feel like a community by the end of the year. The essay project that we do throughout the year was also really cool, I got a chance to interview my family members and learn more about my family's history in the process. Don't be intimidated if you thought 123XB was difficult, I think the pacing is much better in 130XA while also teaching you so so much. Highly recommend!!

My peers were phenomenal and kind people and our professors were always warm and encouraging. I learned SO MUCH about China and its current social issues. A lot of what I learned I would've never learned if not for this class.
