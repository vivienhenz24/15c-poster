This is a great class to take! If you're interested in wetlands or even water more broadly, Professor Matthes does a great job introducing you to wetlands and water policy more broadly. This was a fun class! Super interesting topic, manageable workload, and Professor Matthes is really nice and knows a ton about this field.
If you are even slightly considering taking this course, stop reading this and enroll in it right now! Jackie is an excellent professor with an impressive research background. She takes an interdisciplinary approach to teaching this course, and we look at the ecology of wetlands, food justice, and social justice concerns around Native communities. She teaches you how to use R and is willing to meet with you individually and spend as much time as you need to grasp things fully. Jackie has also thoughtfully selected course readings from a diverse set of scholars. I appreciate the guest lecturers that she brought in because our conversations were so fruitful. Overall, this class is incredibly accessible and has a low workload. Jackie is a phenomenal professor willing to help you with anything from academics to career paths to research opportunities. Take this class!!!!
