Take this course!! This was an amazing, low–stress music class. I had never played contemporary music before (and honestly wasn't even sure if I liked it) and decided to try Music 186 because it seemed interesting to me. I am so, so glad that I took it. It's one of my favorite classes I've ever taken at Harvard. The group classes, workshops, and Throwdowns are incredibly fun and you get to meet awesome music people through them. Throughout the semester, my group spent the time learning one piece and we got to explore it in a way I've never done before. We even had the opportunity to meet the composer of our piece (a perk of contemporary music and also just such an incredible experience)! Jessica and Claire cared deeply about this class and brought an unparalleled enthusiasm for making it the best experience we could have as students. They were receptive to feedback, constructive in their weekly coachings, and so excited to share their love of contemporary music with us. I could not recommend this course more!
