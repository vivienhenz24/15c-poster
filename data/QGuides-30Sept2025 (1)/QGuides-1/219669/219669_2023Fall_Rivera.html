What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Amazing class taught by an even greater professor! The course material validated some of the feelings that i have bee having regarding course work and the world at large, it basically gave me the foundation, both critically thinking–wise and language–wise, to be able to speak on these frustrations.

The class materials are diverse, and the professor provides outside material to read in order to ensure that there is a well –rounded line–up. I
I highly recommend this class. If you are asked to do presentations, I suggest you request the instructor to have those done at the beginning of class in order to have the appropriate time for the post–presentation discussion.
The course would be great for anyone interested in thinking more about / getting deeper into decolonial theory. The readings cover key ideas/themes in decolonial thought, and Dr. Rivera leads class discussions through these ideas in thoughtful and intentional ways. Sometimes I felt like I was barely following class discussions and that I wasn't understanding the cool thoughts from everyone, but I think nevertheless what I learned from the class helped me think in interesting and creative ways & the class was interesting and challenging!
