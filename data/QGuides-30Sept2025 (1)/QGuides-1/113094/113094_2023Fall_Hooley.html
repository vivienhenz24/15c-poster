If you can, take this class from Annie! She is an incredibly kind, caring and motivating instructor. Throughout this course, she provided excellent feedback and fostered really good class conversations. Overall, content–wise, this class can be quite dry since topics change quickly and it is more of a survey course for the field of psychology. However, since it is thought in very small sections, you usually get to discuss these concepts in depth, and get to know your classmates really well, which was probably one of the best aspects of PSY971! Definitely stay on top of the work because it can pile up really quickly. Thank you to Annie for making this a great experience!

I genuinely enjoyed this course!!
