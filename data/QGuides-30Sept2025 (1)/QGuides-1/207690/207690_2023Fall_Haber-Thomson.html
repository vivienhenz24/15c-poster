What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Work smarter, not harder. It's really easy to tunnel in on something for your thesis when it could be better to take a step back and see what you gotta do big picture. I didn't feel like the three assignments helped my thesis progress –– I found the desk crits (or just 1 on 1 time with Lisa or Rachel) much more helpful. Lisa and Rachel were amazing –– whoever the teaching staff is next year, meet with them outside of class! This class is not studio –– it's a lot of research (some historical, some through design/drawing) which took some time getting used to. In studio, you're given the framework for each assignment, whereas now you have to come up with the framework for your thesis. Don't worry if you don't do as much design work as you would've liked: developing and nailing down your narrative is super important and then you can design!
theses take many forms and it can be confusing to arrive at a mode of representation or a deliverable object but this class will help you arrive at that conclusion
If youre interested in doing a design thesis, please know that there is a lot of support from the leaders of this course.
