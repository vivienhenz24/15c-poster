Comments
Professor Pollan is such an amazing teacher and human being. Even if you are a Crimson writer (which I am), you will learn so much about writing and reporting. This class reshaped how I thought about coming up with story ideas and structuring narrative journalism articles. I don't have a science background, and this class was still amazing. Pollan doesn't have a science background either he was an English major! The course can be a lot of work, since reporting and writing a good long form piece takes a lot of time, but it was enjoyable work. Pollan also gives excellent feedback. He will actually critique your writing (I've found that not all Harvard writing teachers do this), which will strengthen your writing skills.
Loved this course! I think more could be done to balance voices in the classroom, as there was one student in particular who continuously interrupted others with her ideas instead of letting others talk. I am sure this is something that could be resolved in future years however.
Take this class! The opportunity to go through the entire process of ideating, reporting, and writing a long–form piece of science journalism while surrounded by caring and engaged peers is incredible. I learned so much about craft and the detailed feedback from Professor Pollan at all steps was so amazing.
This course was great because of its focus on the real–world applications of journalism, editing, pitching, etc. which is a focus I haven't had in previous creative writing courses. The suite of guest speakers really helped provide a different perspective on the work that goes behind journalism, which was refreshing. In general, Professor Pollan was also so encouraging, helping each of us develop our own strand of research as well as taking the time to help us in as many ways as he could.
SOOOOOOOOO this is the best course I have taken at Harvard. And You should take it.

Professor Pollan is one of the kindest and most incredible professors on campus his experience in the writing and editing industry is unmatched and he truly cares about every single person and piece. I came from a "nonfiction life writing" creative writing course before, so this class was such a nice change into journalism–style writing. You will spend the entire semester on one article, and Pollan is so integral in helping you find a topic and introduce you to experts in the field for you to interview. He also brought in incredible speakers from authors to famous editors to give you real advice in terms of pitching your piece for publication at the end of the semester. I've never taken a class like this and I don't think I ever will again take this!!
I strongly encourage students apply for this course! This workshop taught me the importance, basics, and skills necessary within science journalism. Professor Pollan's teaching was insightful and incredibly helpful in growing my reporting and writing skills; his expertise and advice further encouraged me toward a career in science writing. Each week, my peers brought contributions to the table and edited my work thoughtfully, creating a comfortable environment for discussions and editing sessions.
Take it! Pollan's the real deal, you'll learn a ton about how to write and journalism as a profession.
Michael is incredible and will give you back the time and effort you put into the class. A truly educational and growth experience with consistent support from all peers and Michael. Incredible conversations and constant laughs in class.
TAKE THIS COURSE! Professor Pollan is even better than you could imagine. Workshops each week left me feeling deeply inspired and moved.
