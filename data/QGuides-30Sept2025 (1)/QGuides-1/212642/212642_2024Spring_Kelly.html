This class is amazing!!! Professor Kelly is so enthusiastic about the material, really such a great professor and teacher and is so supportive of everyone's ideas and perspectives. The course is really chill but also super rewarded, strikes a nice balance and a lot of the work and assignments are really open ended and allow you to explore what you want! I honestly loved every second, everyone was so incredibly sweet and I learned so much just by listening to everyone else talk about the readings. Strong strong recommend!
Professor Kelly is a wonderful teacher. He is so patient and understanding and truly wanted all of us to learn more about the subject matter. He approached the class with an understanding that many of us did not come from arts backgrounds or may just have a small interest in Chinese supernatural tales or Chinese culture overall, and lectures were built to provide helpful context and to make the subject matter as entertaining and understandable as possible.
Assignments were fun and relevant, while being relatively easy to complete. If you had any questions, feedback was given promptly and OH were always available.
I highly recommend this class if you want a relatively chill but fun and engaging class while being able to learn more about Chinese stories and literary practices!
This is a great class to take! Super chill, all you have to do is read some cool strange tales for homework. Assignments are graded pretty leniently. Professor Kelly is a great teacher and is so knowledgable about the topic. You'll learn a lot, and read stories that I guarantee you wouldn't read otherwise. It's very eye opening to see the "strange" tales of a different culture, and it's fun too!
There's no pre–requisite for this class, I'd recommend this course to any student who has an interest in the strange and unusual. This is a great course to take and it's material is incredibly unique. You get to read fascinating and interesting stories, view the Yenching Library Collection, and visit the art museum to view amazing artwork.
***this class is amazing! both professor kelly and yi are super kind and knowledgeable. the readings are very interesting without being an overwhelming amount of work, and lectures and section are engaging. the course topic might seem a little niche, but a lot of the ideas and themes like female agency and how we deal with trauma are relevant far beyond the scope of chinese literature or strange tales. incredibly rewarding 10/10 would take again
Make sure you time manage. The assignments are not as spaced out as I would have liked which made it harder to time manage. Make sure to participate. It's easy to just sit back and listen to Kelly, but the readings are so interesting that you might as well contribute. The more you contribute the more you will enjoy the class. Make sure to take notes as this will help you apply lecture/ discussion to your assignments later in the semester.
