If you're interested in climate change, this course is worth your time.

If you need a fourth (or even fifth, like in my case) class to take that is not unreasonably difficult, TAKE THIS CLASS. By far, it was my favorite class of the semester. You learn so much about global warming and the different processes that affect our climate.
