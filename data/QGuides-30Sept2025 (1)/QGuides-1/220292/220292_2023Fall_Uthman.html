What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I'm delighted this course was offered, and I hope it continues being offered. Like the lands and people from which it sprang, the Albanian language is rich, fascinating, complicated, and a rewarding subject of study. With so few resources existing for English speakers to learn Albanian in a formal setting, this course turbo–charged my self–directed efforts. Through the class discussions and materials, and because I was inspired to delve still deeper in my own explorations, my Albanian is so much better than it was before the semester started, with the greatest improvements wrought in my ability to speak in complete sentences out loud. The dream is that continued speaking opportunities will result in a smoother flow with less buffering time and fewer mistakes.
This class was a ton of fun and I would whole–heartedly recommend it!
This is a great course to take if you want to learn about the Albanian language, culture, and community. The instructor is very patient, fun to work with, and uses a helpful class format that will allow you to practice parts of the textbook.
Faton is the best TF you will ever have! He is very helpful and helps you through all the lessons. Definitely a great resource if you are good at picking up languages or have some background in Albanian. Also, the community of students you are with definitely makes the class!
