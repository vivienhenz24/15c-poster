This was a really great course that covered a broad overview of pathology topics.
This class is great for "big idea" scientists, who want to know how disease impacts the body and what that looks like. It provides a great launching pad to new questions. I was able to get something out of every class, even those of topics I wouldn't originally think I'd be interested in. The course directors really care for the course and our learning, and it has been one of my favorite classes so far.
This is an excellent course! There are unique opportunities outside of the lectures (in lab sections or hospital activities) to get a deeper understanding of how life sciences research interacts with clinical practice.
Excellent course on pathology! Hands–on labs expose you to interpreting gross specimens and stained slides. A different guest lecturer speaks every course. Minimal workload with three problem sets and a short final presentation on a topic of your choice. The course largely follows case studies from the perspective of a pathologist trying to diagnose a patient and provide a treatment plan.
Great class to take! Very interesting and stress free
