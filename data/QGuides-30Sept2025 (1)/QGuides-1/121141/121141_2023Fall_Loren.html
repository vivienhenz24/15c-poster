This class is great. The sections are once a week and super engaging. No matter your interest in archaeology, it's incredibly exciting when you unearth an artifact from 100+ years ago. The professors are very kind and helpful and the workload is minimal. Highly recommend.
This was a fantastic course! Highly recommend.
This is an amazing course. This class affords the incredible opportunity to excavate on campus and develop archaeological skills needed for excavating, record–keeping, identifying artifacts, and understanding them through the lens of historical archaeology. You learn to engage more meaningfully with Harvard's history, and have the chance to actively contribute to our understanding of it via our findings. We also share our conclusions with the public thanks to public archaeology initiatives. Don't worry that this is a two–semester course; I am so excited to come back next semester to analyze and interpret what we have found. Trish (Dr. Capone) and Diana (Dr. Loren) and our TF Veronica are all incredible instructors, and are so passionate and understanding.
