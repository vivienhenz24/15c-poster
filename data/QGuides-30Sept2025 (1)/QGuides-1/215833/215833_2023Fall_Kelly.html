What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is really fun! This truly is one of those classes where the hours fly by, I don't think I can say the same for any other class. We worked with a really cool range of texts, objects, genres, and also class formats, so it always felt like we didn't have enough time to go through everything. I recommend it very much !!
I strongly recommend this class, as it deals with a range of fascinating texts (and objects) while giving a great overview of issues relating to materiality in Chinese literature, and literature more broadly. It also gives students a lot of room to explore areas of interest related to their own research, and encourages cross–disciplinary approaches, so it isn't limited to students of Chinese literature. Professor Kelly is always helpful, engaging, and approachable, which fosters a great class atmosphere.
