This class is mostly student run with student presentations every week leading discussions and summarizing readings. We do about 4 article readings every week.
Take this class. Very interesting material and Ryan is a great teacher.
This was an easy course and it is certainly one of those "you get out what you put in". If you want easy, then it'll be just that. If you're really interested in the material, you will get more out of the readings and discussion post. On the whole, very chill and covers interesting content. Weekly reading assignment is low–stress and you have a LOT of time to complete the final paper, which is also not a big headache.
I highly recommend this class! You learn so much about personality psychology from Ryan and your peers through engaging class discussions. The class only meets once a week and you just have 4 articles along with a discussion post to turn in every week. You can really be attentive in class because there is not so much work required of you. You also get to lead a seminar on one of the topics that interests you and there is just one final paper.
Take this class! It is a wonderful learning environment, very very interesting, and will help you develop skills like leading a seminar! i would recommend this class to anyone who loves psychology because of how applicable it is to your daily life.
PSY 1711 is a fun discussion–based course, and Ryan is a great professor. A different student leads classroom discussion every week, and it was a fun way to learn the material. Ryan is clearly very passionate about personality psychology, which added to the energy of the class!
