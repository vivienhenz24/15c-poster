Extremely good language class, doesn't focus too much on grammar, but allows you to have simple conversations in no time
Would recommend having some knowledge of another European language before taking this class.
Italian at Harvard is not one of the easier foreign language paths to take, but the class itself is quite fun and the focus on conversational Italian is nice when you walk away from so many foreign language classes feeling like you can not actually talk to a native speaker. Some of the class materials, particularly the take home written assignments and VHL online coursework, seem disorganized and at times it is unclear what they want you to do.

But the class itself is still quite fun, and if you want to learn Italian, it is a good way to fast–track your learning of the language.
Take Rebecca Peroni's section if she's teaching it. She's great
This class was simply phenomenal. It was fast and intense, but it was an extremely rewarding academic experience. I had an incredible TF.
I loved ITAL 10 and highly recommend! It is so cool to see yourself progress in a new language in only just a few months. I had Becky (Rebecca) and she was such a wonderful instructor who created a very supportive, engaging, and fun classroom environment. The nightly online homework assignments can be somewhat tedious at times, but they are very helpful. I would recommend staying on top of the vocab and grammar that you learn throughout the course because everything builds on the previous content that you learned.
