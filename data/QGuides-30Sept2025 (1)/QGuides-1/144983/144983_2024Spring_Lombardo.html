Brainstorm and work with your team from start to finish
Overall the class was extremely worthwhile, but it is highly instructor dependant Even thought the topic was not something I was interested in, it taught me how to apply design thinking to a complex problem and I believe this way of thinking is useful in many contexts.
If there is any scenario in which you do not have to take this class, do not take it. The teaching staff of our section was truly wonderful, and they did their best to make it a useful class, but it was still a complete waste of time. We spent all semester talking (literally just talking) about data centers in a three hour, twice a week class. There was no actual engineering component, but we still had to put in a lot of time.
This class has a bad reputation, but this really depends on the project and the instructors. While I think the format of the class itself could be improved (separate sections for separate engineering concentrations), I think that the instructors did a good job with what was given to them. The project was also very interesting.
Take it!
