This class is amazing, as is Houcine. The content is very interesting and satisfying since this course will likely never be offered again, I would definitely recommend reading through Chapter 7 of Stanley if you are interested, since symmetric functions are quite interesting. More broadly, Houcine is wonderful and very kind.
Houcine Ben Dali is one of the most considerate and thoughtful professors I have had at Harvard. As well as knowing the material inside–out, he lectures with genuine enthusiasm and will offer countless opportunities to take questions. Any questions asked are followed by a response tailored to the pace of those who the questions applied to. As a result, few queries were left unanswered as each lecture ended. Also, if you are a fan of good board work, he is pretty good at that too! Most importantly, Houcine is a great person. Not once did he say no to a request for additional office hours. There is a genuine drive from Prof. Ben Dali for all students to understand the material and have fun while doing so. The class itself was definitely more challenging than previous courses I have been involved in, but I never once felt unsupported or as if the class was impossible. Houcine played a great role in that. I cannot recommend Prof. Ben Dali more highly. I have taken away so much from this course and it is all thanks to him.
Houcine is a great instructor, and he is very accessible outside of class. The problem set questions are well–chosen and challenging. I also received very detailed feedback for my work.

Overall, I got a lot out of this class!
