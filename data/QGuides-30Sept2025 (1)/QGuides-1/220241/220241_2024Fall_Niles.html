Comments
Wonderful introduction to Native American studies at Harvard. Good mix of students from different academic backgrounds (anthro, history, etc.) that adds to lively, welcoming discussion. The course addresses a very important topic that all students entering Harvard should know and encourages open dialogue on Harvard's role in land disposession. Also, projects at the end of the semester are a highlight since it allows you as a student to address the issue in a personally meaningful way. Highly recommend to anyone interested!
Really great course! Teaches you so much about Harvard and New England Indigenous history, but also gives you the tools to address Harvard's legacy as students today.
