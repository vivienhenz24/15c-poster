What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is a wonderful class to learn about contemporary art from a curator's perspective, which makes it one of the most unique classes I've taken here! We got to visit many interesting places including artist studios and many museums around the Boston Area. Dan and Danni are very kind and approachable, and I learned so much just from hearing their experiences! If you have any interest in curating or contemporary art, consider taking it to learn more about the field!!
This class was wonderful!! I feel so incredibly lucky to have experienced it. Even though I do not necessarily come from an art history/visual arts background, I felt very supported and have taken so much away from this course. Every week we were seeing or doing something new, which kept it very exciting and something to always look forward to. I've definitely gained a major appreciation for contemporary art/artists as well as the entire process of curationthere are so many nuances I would have never considered. We also had the opportunity to engage with many artists and curators from around the area, which I think was very eye–opening and valuable to experience first–hand. It was also just a genuinely lovely group – discussions were always so rich and thought–provoking. Take this course!! You will not regret it!
