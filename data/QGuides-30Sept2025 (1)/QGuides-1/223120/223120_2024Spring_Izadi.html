The readings are fascinating and are some of the best parts of the class the course offers a rich, wide range of primary and secondary source readings. There's a combination of memoir and nonfiction writing that I really liked. Course discussions felt a bit circular and meandering at times, but I still really liked the class
This is such a great class! As someone with almost no NAIS education prior to this semester, I feel this class was an incredible introduction. Mandy is so sweet and, like few professors I've seen, makes students feel very cared for.
please take this course it is amazing course and one of y favourite courses!
This course is a great introduction to the field of Native American and Indigenous Studies. Professor Izadi is a very caring and understanding instructor. She does her best to foster a welcoming classroom environment. I would say that although this course it great for an introductory overview, I would recommend taking higher level Indigenous courses to learn more from Indigenous perspectives after this course.
Try to write notes to yourself about the readings or make your discussion post in detail because that will make the small writing assignments that come later in the course, much easier.
Professor Izadi is incredibly knowledgeable about Native Studies and is eager to meet with students to help them grow. If you're someone who likes grades rather than written feedback, then this might not be the course for you, because we have received no letter grades throughout the entire semester. HOWEVER, the feedback she provides is very detailed and aimed at making our writing stronger in case we want to have our work published.

It's hard to speak to future iterations of the course because she has expressed that this was her first time running it, and it will likely change for next year.
Dr. Izadi is a wonderful professor. The readings throughout class were deeply informative and taught us to think critically about the historical events we were studying. We were absolutely privileged to have guest lecturers and engage in real time conversations with them about their work and what we learned in class. The assignments were approachable and doable. Dr. Izadi is also an incredibly warm professor who is willing to accommodate students and work with them on their interests. You can get so much out of this course. I highly recommend taking it.
TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Izadi is such an amazing and loving professor, very easy to talk to, and someone who makes all of her classes very interesting!! Every reading will teach you something new. Furthermore, through her class, I improved not only my writing skills though her feedback, but also became more confident in sharing my thoughts. I couldn't recommend taking a class with Professor Izadi enough!!!!!!!!
This course provided an amazing overview of Native Studies. Dr. Izadi is a fantastic lecturer who leads a compelling and interesting class.
I would recommend taking this class as it teaches very useful knowledge. It is very interesting as well as engaging and will benefit you overall.
