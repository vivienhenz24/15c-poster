What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is a very challenging course but one that is worth it. It will thoroughly improve your knowledge of microbiology, but you will have to work for it.
I would say read the paper early and go over it again before class if possible. I would also say though that it is okay not to know every answer to every question and in those scenarios, be vocal about what you do and do not know so you can work to figure out the holes in your knowledge.
This class was difficult in terms of deeply engaging with the literature (perhaps in a way you never have before) but it was incredibly well–taught and I came out with a much stronger knowledge of microbiology and experimental design. Would highly recommend to anyone who is thinking about joining a microbiology–based lab/research.
