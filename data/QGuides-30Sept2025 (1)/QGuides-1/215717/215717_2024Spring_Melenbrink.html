unless you want to commit your life in the lab don't take this class, you don't get much feedback & course instruction is horrible
TAKE THIS CLASS. It's so fun and what other class can you take where you get to make multiple cool projects with your hands!!!!
TAKE THIS CLASS. It is a true Harvard gem, not in the sense that it is hard, but in the sense that it is an AMAZING class. If you have ever been at all interested about making things (like what one might imagine MIT people learn or what Iron Man does), this class is the one to take. You will learn a lot of cool things, make a lot of cool things, and be able to keep them. Your friends will be jealous. Totally worth it.
All that being said, not that this is not an easy class because of how much time it takes from you. It takes time for three reasons. 1) You need to come up with your own ideas, and so there won't be clear set by step instructions to follow. 2) Building things take a lot of time, even if your ideas are conceptually simple. And that will be in large part because of errors and problems that will come up along the way. 3) You will need to be in the lab most of the time. Having to travel to the lab and be there does take a substantial amount of time. It is very different from working on PSets in a library or your room.
So be sure you have enough time, but if you do it is totally worth it, and the experience of working in a lab is so memorable and enjoyable. Also, note that you may not be able to take the class P/F if that's your plan; so ask about it early on.
ABSOLUTELY TAKE THIS CLASS! Favourite class of all time. Staff is amazing, the class is amazing. It's a ton of work but you'll get so much out of it. I wish I could just take this class for the rest of my life lol. You'll learn 3D design, 3D printing, assembly, microcontrollers, webpages, web interface (controlling output devices from webpage), casting and molding, circuitry, wiring, and so much more. Your final project can be whatever you want as long as it makes the requirements. This class made me so confident to just get out there and build things.
If you get the chance then you have to take this course. It's amazing.
Very great class!! My only regret is not taking it before my senior year so I would have had lab access for longer. Put in the time to go to office hours and learn from the TFs, it will make your weekly assignments much easier.
THIS COURSE IS THE BEST!!!!!! Takes a ton of time but so worth it. Super fun and everyone there is super excited to be there so the whole class vibe is awesome. Nathan and the Tfs are all amazing and so helpful and so fun to be around. Learned a ton.
This course is worth it if you want time to explore a fun project and have access to a cool fabrication lab. You will have to do most of the actual learning on your own though.
This course was incredible and taught me the value of a hands on learning experience. Over the course of the semester you gain confidence and comfort with building projects YOU want to build and getting creative. You learn the basics for many tools needed to build, prototype, and iterate many things you'd need for different kinds of projects, and you gain the confidence to know you can learn more to get better in the future. This class actually changed my life and I cannot recommend this enough.
