This is a great course, but be advised going into it that there is a LOT of work. The average week might be something like a vocab quiz on Monday, textbook homework due Tuesday, a writing assignment due Wednesday, a conversation recording assignment and more textbook homework due Thursday, and then another vocab quiz Friday. If you're willing to take on the workload, you'll benefit a lot in terms of improving your language skills.
