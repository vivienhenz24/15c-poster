This class is an excellent tutorial. I would just make sure that you have a real interest in urban studies and/or anthropological methods (ethnographies, etc), as that would allow you to get the most out of the course, but it is still meaningful nevertheless. Dr. Guan is a kind and great instructor. The readings can be long but the class is dedicated to discussing the readings, so even if you can't finish/understand them on your own time, class discussion will help you get the most out of them.
Take this class!!! It was so incredibly interesting and Dr. Guan is an amazing professor to take a class with. You won't regret it!
This course is fantastic! I think Xinyu is a great instructor. He was incredibly available, thoughtful, and responsive to feedback, and tried to make this class as engaging and productive as possible. Highly recommend taking this course if you are interested in urban studies or ethnography.
Take this course!!! The readings may be daunting (and honestly they are quite long) but they really are so enjoyable and about topics you probably wouldn't encounter if not for this course.
