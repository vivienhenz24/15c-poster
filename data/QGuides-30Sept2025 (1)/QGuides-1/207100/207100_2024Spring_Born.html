Reasonable follow up to NB215A. Requirement for first year Program in Neuroscience graduate students. A fair amount of effort clearly put into making this a cohesive course, but its mainly just lectures and some somewhat connected journal clubs. Its a nice way to get to know your PhD cohort.
I had a much better experience in NB215B than in NB215A. You will learn so much in the second semester and be exposed to so many cool lines of thought in neuroscience!
This class is as engaging as you and your friends make it! If you really care about or are interested in a particular topic or paper, dig deeper! Contribute to discussion! Ask questions! Not everyone will know all topics, but everyone's diversity of expertise can come together. 215B shined in moments where people were bouncing ideas off one another, learning, debating, etc.
Engaging with the material by participating in group discussions was my favorite way to understand the material.
