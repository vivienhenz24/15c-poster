Take this course if you must (i.e. if you've already taken Ma) but, if you have any experience with calculus at all, make the jump from M to 1a. This course spends a lot of time on building foundations, which is great if you don't have much math experience; however, if you do, you will be bored for most of the course.
Math MB is a course that can take a lot of time out of your schedule. Be prepared to work on PSETs three times a week, mixed in with studying for Skills Checks and Exams. Thankfully, there is a great support system in things like Office Hours and the MQC (Math Questions Center) that will help tremendously along the way! I would highly recommend taking advantage of those opportunities to help your understanding of course curriculum.
Its a good course to take. They sometimes tell you different courses lead into eachother but Harvard teaches math in an interesting way so creating this foundation is great
Math M series is very frustrating. There is so much group work for no reason, and the section is very minimal in terms of what it actually teaches you. You basically have to teach yourself on your pset.
Make sure to start P–Sets early as they can be difficult sometimes and it helps to go to office hours early.
