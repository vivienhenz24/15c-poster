Well based on what I have been told, there will be no future students in this class, but if for some reason there are, you should definitely take this class. I think it is one of the best expos classes offered at Harvard. The content is engaging and timely, and Professor Cole is passionate about it.
I recommend taking this course if you need a relatively easy expos class and are interested in Orwell or any other dystopian literature or ideas. I personally had a lot of fun and did not think it was hard.
manage your time well in this class. There's not a ton of homework, but if you fall behind on your homework you will be grinding late nights
Expos is a requirement after all. Although many people said that it actually improved their writing skills I do not feel like my writing has changed a lot throughout the course. However, this section about Orwell's 1984 was amazing (mainly the first half of the course that is more about fictional writing and analysis). Prof Cole is an amazing teacher and really enthusiastic and makes the very best of this mandatory class! Take it!
This class offers an opportunity to take your writing to the next level. The feedback is excellent and the assignments all have an actual purpose. I would recommend this class to anyone interested in government systems, dystopias, or just 1984 in general.
This class was truly amazing, I learned a great deal and Professor Cole is an excellent mentor.
You would be hard pressed to find someone who knows more about Orwell or 1984, and is more enthusiastic about sharing that knowledge with students than Prof. Cole. You will learn more about 1984 than you could in any other setting. And, concerning Expos courses in general, I think you will appreciate the small class setting.
