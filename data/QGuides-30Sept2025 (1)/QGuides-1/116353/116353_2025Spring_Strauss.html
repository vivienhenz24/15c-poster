Comments
I would definitely recommend this course to fulfill the EXPOS 20 requirement. The course material is super fun and interestingit's mostly just watching and talking about really good movies. Sometimes class time felt a little unstructured and empty, but overall, I think this is a great choice for expos, especially if you have any interest in film and psychology topics.
I would recommend this class over and over again as it was so rewarding to sharpen my writing skills using a new medium mainly focused on films. You also learn so much about persona and what it means to you and the context of the world.
TAKE THIS EXPOS!! Professor Strauss is one of the sweetest people I've ever met but she also helped me improve massively as a writer. The final capstone project rather than another essay is so much more interesting, and watching films makes the analytical aspect of this course much more engaging.
This is such a great Expos. Not only are you watching and writing about movies, but Prof. Strauss is so chill, nice, and understanding. She is also very clear and communicative about her feedback and what she wants out of you. You significantly improve as a writer and thinker, and are given many opportunities to flesh out your thoughts both on paper but in class–wide discussions. You should also take this course if you simply find movies and the concept of persona super interesting (which I bet you do). Having or witness a persona is inevitable in all of our lives – we all come across it, especially here at Harvard. This class couldn't be more pertinent for that reason.
Prof. Strauss is an incredibly kind and gracious teacher! She will make the class feel warm and wants you to be your genuine self. The theme of persona for me was an interesting one throughout the semester to follow and I thought each unit was compelling. Using films as a lens for analysis was fun, however, there is one week where you will have to watch three films, but that is the heaviest this class gets in terms of homework. It is super manageable and Prof. Strauss is also very understanding. I thought the two essays + capstone was a perfect distribution, and each essay did not feel rushed for time since it was rolled out early on before the deadline. I had a great time in this class and would recommend, especially those who like psychological films.
