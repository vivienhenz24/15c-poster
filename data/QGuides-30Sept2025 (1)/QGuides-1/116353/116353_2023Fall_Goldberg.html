If you are interested in social theory and gender theory or you simply want to develop your writing by exploring interesting subject areas, this is a great expos class. Dr. Goldberg is wonderful when it comes to his passion for the course material and his support for each student in the class. It may be somewhat difficult compared to some other expos out there, but by the end of the course, you will definitely come out satisfied.
I recommend this course so much, not only because Dr. Ethan's curriculum structure of giving 2 weeks between the 1st draft and final draft (which most Expos don't give) was actually helpful in revising essays into ones that were honest to your ideas, but also because the readings themselves were so interesting and pretty mind blowing at some points. Most expos is mainly focused on the mechanics of writing, but this class taught me so much about theories and introduced me to many short stories that I'd now call one of my favorites. HIGHLY RECOMMEND THIS CLASS!!!
Ethan's feedback is fantastic. The topic of the class is versatile enough that you can pretty much write anything you want in terms of commenting on contemporary culture.
Dr. Goldberg is a sweetheart and you can tell he genuinely cares about his work and about writing! The readings typically don't take too long but be ready to spend at least 2–3 hours analyzing movies during the second unit –– even more if you watch your movie more than once. Grading is, in my opinion, pretty lenient. You are also given the opportunity to write about whatever interests you. If you are dedicated to analyzing our status quo and rethinking norms, this is definitely the class for you! *

A good intro to college level writing that teaches you what you need to know. Professor Goldberg is great and provides really useful feedback. Follow the prompts in essays and do the readings and you will do well. Getting an A may require more effort though so if this is your goal be sure to go to office hour or the writing center. Overall in this class you get what you put in but the value you get is definitely worth the effort.
