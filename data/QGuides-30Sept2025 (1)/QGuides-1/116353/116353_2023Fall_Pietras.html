What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
When reading through the Q Guide before registering for this course, it seemed like Expos as a whole was just a requirement that you have to get out of the way in your first year. The Q reports for this class specifically seemed to be mostly negative, however, I had the complete opposite experience. Dr. Pietras was so helpful all semester, and I found that he gave very intentional and helpful feedback that improved my writing significantly. This course does at first seem like a queer history class, but keeping in mind that it is an Expos course, I feel that it does a fantastic job at building those foundational skills that are necessary for college writing. The course is especially interesting as a queer person, but this has been one of my favorite courses of the semester. If you're looking to become a stronger writer while learning about some very interesting patterns in queer media, this is the Expos for you!!
