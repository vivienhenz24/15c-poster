This is a great class, out of all the Expos 20, 100% the best. Keating (the professor) is so engaging, fun, and laid back. He talk about sports a lot of class but then is very knowledgeable and ready to help you write your essays. The only downside was that this class, while a gem and interesting, is not very intellectually stimulating, all but 3 people were athletes in the class, and a lot of them either did not talk or did not care. And that was annoying. It was me and two other kids who were practically the only ones that contributed.
I thoroughly enjoyed my time in the class. I actually did almost all the reading and watching of film, which I think very few people did, and it made class super easy and essays very manageable. The readings took very little time anyways. Keating does not ask very much of your time. A lot of the stuff you can get away with not doing, but the reading is very applicable. I got an A on all three of my essays and because that's the whole grade I don't have much to say about the grading.
If you are at all interested in sports or politics, this class is an excellent choice for EXPOS. The work is not very excessive, the essay feedback is helpful, and the grading is fair. Highly recommend!
Take this class, the instructor is super welcoming and just wants to help you develop. Don't feel pressure to be the best writer at the beginning, the instructor just wants to see progress and that you are doing your best to take what he teaches and implement it in your work.
THIS IS A GEM! I highly recommend this class. The work is so manageable and if you are interested in sports at all it is super interesting. Overall this class is a must take and I would rank it #1
