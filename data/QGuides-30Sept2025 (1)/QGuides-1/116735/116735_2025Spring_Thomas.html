I recommend this class very highly, and Professor Thomas especially. This class becomes more rewarding the more you invest in the poems yourself, outside of class, so I highly recommend doing adequate preparation, which is not overly difficult considering we typically have less than 200 lines a week. Highly recommend.
This is an incredible course! Professor Thomas is so knowledgeable and shares incredible perspectives on the poems, and class discussions are overall such a joy. The class really changed the way I think about Latin literature and what areas of Classics I want to pursue going forward. I could not recommend the course more!
Professor Thomas has a lot of really interesting insights and has no shortage of things to say about each poem. I liked the general structure of the class, the student presentations, the discussion of poem themes and deeper meanings, and overall would recommend the course to all Classics students.
Stay on top of the readings and read the commentary.
If you have an opportunity to take this class, take it! I came into this course having never taken an upper level translation course, and I think that this may be one of the best first upper–level classes one can take. Professor Thomas is not only one of the most brilliant professors I know at Harvard, but he is a genuinely kind individual who truly cares for the growth of his students. Mandatory office ours periodically throughout the semester really help to break the initial barrier, and, by the end of the semester, he seems more of a friend and mentor than an imposing top scholar in the field. At the same time, every single class was filled with fascinating information about the odes we covered, and I am leaving this class with just enough knowledge to know how much I don't know about the odes and how much more I want to learn –– something which I receive more rarely than you might think. One piece of advice is to stick to the readings each week as much as you can. If you slack, it is very difficult to catch up since there are over 80 odes to review by the time of the final (speaking from experience, it is not fun to review so many within a couple days!). That is not a problem of the class as so much as a problem of time–management, however. All in all, this was an excellent course that I highly recommend to anyone with a solid understanding of Latin.
Make sure that you know your Latin grammar and stay on top of the weekly readings/translations.
Anyone who wishes to engage with the wonderful poet should take the class.
Definitely take this course! You will learn so much! Cannot recommend enough!
Take this class! Richard is an amazing professor, and is very forgiving when it comes to assignments. That being said, you still have to put in the work if you want the class to go smoothly, but the class is set up to provide a lot of support should you need it.
