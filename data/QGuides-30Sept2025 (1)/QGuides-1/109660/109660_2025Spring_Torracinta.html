Comments
You learn a lot in this class about writing in this field. Start early and do good work in the chunks. You will thank yourself! Really think hard about what you want your topic to be once you have done a good analysis of the primary sources available to you. I was wishy washy with a topic so the final push for the draft and final drafts were a lot.
This course gets progressively harder as the year goes on and the deadline for the final paper approaches. It is helpful to have a topic in mind beforehand, or at least a general idea of what fields or time frames you are interested in researching , but it is definitely not necessary. The course is designed to leave more than enough time for a person to explore different ideas and hone in on one. Additionally, the course is designed to where the assignments consistently build off of each other. During the last few weeks of the course, the time I spent working on the course significantly skyrocketed as I was consistently reading, writing, or revising. However, the course is an overall positive experience as you create a final paper that relates to a topic of interest. Also, the course has a lot of built in support and feedback!
Great class, especially if you are new to writing! This class breaks the really long final paper down by having you write chunks of your paper throughout the semester. Additionally, there are really great examples of Histsci papers and you learn how to analyze a variety of different sources that could potentially contribute to your paper.
Take this class! Simon is so fun to work with and truly incredibly understandingcan be a harsh grader/reader sometimes but it became so helpful towards the final stretch
This is a great course! if you put in the effort throughout the semester, it will truly help you refine your writing skills
