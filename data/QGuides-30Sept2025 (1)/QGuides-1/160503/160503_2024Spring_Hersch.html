What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course benefits from a brilliant and charismatic Professor Hersch, who simultaneously leaves much room for everyone in the class to participate. I have not only learned immense amounts about the history of technology but also about Academe as a profession, through Professor Hersch's many professionalization workshops. This was by far one of the most instructive and light–hearted courses I have ever had the chance to take.
TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Hersch is a fantastic professor, I honestly recommend any class by him. The seminar format was great and there was lots of lively discussion and fun activities (submarine field trip!). He also gives great feedback on your writing.
Prof. Hersch is highly enthusiastic about teaching and deeply invested in supporting students toward their educational goals. He is open to allowing students to tailor their final projects to meet their own needs. The course was a ton of fun with great discussions. Most importantly, you get to go on a submarine, an experience which is objectively very cool.
If you are at all curious about the history of technology, take this class!! There's substantial flexibility to investigate your own personal areas of interest, but also a thorough survey of the important texts and ideas in the field. Hersch is engaging and adds fun elements to his classes (i.e. straw bridge competition, learn how to make fire with a flint). Also, he shares a lot of the "unspoken" rules of grad school and academia. Overall, was super fun and useful!!
Take this class if you have even a bit of interest in the field – you won't regret it.
