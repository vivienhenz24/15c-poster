Comments
If you are interested in medicine or immunology, this course might be a good choice. It covers many areas of immunology (auto–immunity, cancer, neuroimmunology, etc). The lecturers are all great people of their field. You will benefit from the lectures and discussions with everyone.
Some of the lecturers were really great and some were not so great, but you can definitely learn some interesting things
You are going to enjoy this class so much!
I highly HIGHLY recommend this class! It is very fun and provides you a way to learn about applications of immunology in various diseases; you will not regret it!!
Amazing class that allows you to learn from a diverse range of professors working at the cutting edge of immunology
I think reading the review papers as opposed to the discussion papers was sometimes more helpful when going into the class, specifically if the lecture is about a disease which has a lot of terms you are not used to. Most questions on the PSETs were designed well based on what was discussed in class before but a few them were definitely not something we had heard of through the lecture and required reading a completely different paper or talking to the TAs for help.
This course is more clinical than translational but very insightful if your are looking to expand your medical knowledge as a non med student
