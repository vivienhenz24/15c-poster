Overall the class is good. You learn a lot about inequality in the US and it sets up a really good historical approach to Sociology thats insightful. However, the readings do become a lot, and lecture HEAVILY depends on it. Which can make lecture a little boring since it honestly just feels like a book club. The professor is really well educated in what she's teaching which is really nice. And she is really fair with her grading and how she runs the class, as long as you talk to her about it. Overall I would recommend it, but just know it was definitely my hardest class of the semester.
