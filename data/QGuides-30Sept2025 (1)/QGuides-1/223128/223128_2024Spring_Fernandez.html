Comments
This class is wonderful and Dr. Fernandez is so attentive and knowledgable! I recommend to anyone interested in either comics or Asian American studies.
You should definitely take this course! It's such a unique and amazing course, and combines critical Asian American Studies theory with fun and interesting graphic narratives. I wish that there were more classes like this at Harvard. I learned so much, and want to take more Asian American Studies courses in the future.
This course is a hidden gem. The workload isn't bad at all, or maybe that's because I found the readings so much fun. Discussions are pretty casually structured, which makes it very easy to share thoughts and opinions with peers and with Dr. Fernandez. Moreover, the project where we got to create our own comic was a lot of fun, even if you're new to comics. I thought that this class was both a great way to look at Asian American graphic novels and to get more familiar with the visual language of comics. My concentration, English, lacks any classes about this literary medium so this one absolutely fulfilled that niche, and I was introduced to different kinds of theory this time as a result of the class being a part of the EMR department. Super cool.
This course was wonderful and an invaluable addition to the EMR department. Its success and salience in these tumultuous times of heightened racial division cannot be understated. Dr. Fernandez is a wonderful teacher and their class kept me in touch with my identity, and my humanity when my other classes had worn me down. For those who are interested in critically examining the Asian American experience and understanding the deep, emotional, and historical histories of Asian Americans that have so long been ignored in main stream discourse, this course is an absolute must.
Cant recommend this class enough; if I could take it twice I would!! Grad student, freshman, fellow, etc, Dr. Fernandez is an amazing teacher and you will learn and be challenged. All readings are provided via Canvas or online (which is just a bonus). You will get what you put into this course, so come to lecture with questions and openness!
Take this class!! Low commitment and high reward
