What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
TDM has serious issues, and it's reflected in this class. If you're not a concentrator: do not take this course, unless you're willing to reshape your life around this course with absolutely zero benefits. You will dedicate so much of your life to this class with absolutely no payoff in the rest of your life.
This iteration of the course was unfortunately problematic and led to the entire run of shows being canceled due to having caused immense harm to Black students. Production studios tend to be hit or miss (recent hits include Spring 2023 "The Singularity Play"), so do your due diligence on the specific work and creative team being staged that semester. Hopefully the TDM department will take a much–needed reevaluation at the production studio framework and prevent this from happening again.
This is such a fun process. I look forward to the class everytime I take it. You get to create a play and learn from real world professionals who somehow have the time to care, support, and teach you. It is a lot of time at the end of the semester, but I love the time I spend with my collaborators. It's a requirement for TDM, but if you have the time I cannot recommend taking this class as your "let me try this out" class!!! It does a great job reaching to all levels and finding your place in it. I have so much love for production studio.
