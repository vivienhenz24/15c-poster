This is a great class!! I loved discussing ethical issues from a Christian perspective, especially with such a wide variety of students and it was different from the conversations I usually have with my (Christian and non–Christian) friends. I appreciated how tolerant and thoughtful everyone in this class is. Many different perspectives were brough to the table and carefully considered, whether we agreed or disagreed. Everyone was also so friendly, an added benefit!

I would 100% recommend this class to anybody, it's definitely my favorite class that I've taken here
Take this course. I have never felt more comfortable expressing controversial views and discussing them in good faith in another course here. You will learn and incredible amount about your faith and its requirements.
This was hands down my favorite class at Harvard. Chip was an amazing instructor, and this course encouraged me to rethink and prove all of my beliefs, which in some cases made them stronger, and other times made me change my mind. Regardless, I made so many friends in this class and will take the ideas I learned in this class with me for the rest of my life.
I could not recommend this class more. The reading material is incredibly thoughtful and engaging, incorporating a wide range of perspectives to allow for good class engagement. In addition to loving the readings, it was truly one of the best class environments I have been in. Chip and all students in the class worked hard to make everyone feel comfortable expressing their genuine opinions, even if unpopular. Each class centered around relevant topics, and there was incredibly fruitful dialogue (the best I have seen during my experience at Harvard), where students were practically jumping out of their seats to participate.
This class really is fantastic and Professor Lockwood is an amazing teacher. Please take this class if you like discussions about ethical issues. All the students were super friendly and everyone was there to promote open–mindedness and mutual learning.
