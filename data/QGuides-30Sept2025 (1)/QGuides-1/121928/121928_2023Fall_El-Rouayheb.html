Take this class!!! Dr. El– Rouayheb is fantastic. Whether you're interested in Islamic Philosophy or not, this class will truly stimulate your mind and introduce you to brilliant thinkers and their ideas. He is very knowledgeable of the subject and keeps the class fun.
Showed me how rich Islamic Philosophy is and how seriously it can be taken. Richly interesting for students with general philosophical interests. Takes the history component seriously, so be prepared to be exposed to dates and historical context. At the same time, the focus was on the arguments and close analysis of texts. This class deepened my inchoate desire to study Islamic Philosophy.
This is a fantastic course, that I could not recommend more highly. If you are interested in philosophy or history, you should take this course. I will note that this is a course on Islamic philosophy, not Islamic law or Islamic history, and so if you are interested in political developments or jurisprudence, then this will not really focus on those topics. Prof. El–Rouayheb is an absolute gem of a person, hilarious and witty and of course extremely intelligent.
Pay attention in lecture! And give the readings a very honest effort.
