Lectures were fantastic and really well done, and assignments were great as well! Honestly a really interesting class and liked Prof Wilson––would recommend taking. One thing that frustrated me was grading of assignments (and the sections themselves) felt pretty openly political at times (I say this as someone with generally the same political leanings, but it was quite extreme), so worth thinking about. But overall, I thought that class was designed well, feedback was given on assignments promptly, the professor was super accessible outside of class time, and it's a good choice as an elective.
If you are an English concentrator who has taken the core suite of courses (10, 20, 97, and ESPECIALLY if you've taken 98r), this course will feel very easy. Just have fun and stay engaged!
