What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is difficult because of the volume of reading and complexity of ideas, but the content and the structure of the class ended up being one of the most rewarding courses that I've taken. Since it was taught in Italian this semester, I was very pleased with how much my language capacity improved throughout the semester and was challenged to create my own perspective based on the readings. It was daunting at first, but then I started to connect more with the reading and gained confidence. Prof. Erspamer focused a good portion of the class on our own ideas, which lead to a sort of lecture and then discussion format in the class.
Take this course if you are interested in Italian literature!
