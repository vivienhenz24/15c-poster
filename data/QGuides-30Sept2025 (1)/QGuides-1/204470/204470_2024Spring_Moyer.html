Comments
A really valuable course for those interested in med and imaging tech careers. Not only do you learn the physics of each imaging modality, you get to practice them and learn how to apply them in different situations. Take time to invest in the course and you'll get a lot out of it. Dr. Moyer is a super great professor and person!
Linsey was great as always. Labs were super interesting, fun and easy. Workload was incredibly manageable, especially for an engineering course. Definitely take this class.
TAKE THIS CLASS **
Take this class! It was one of my least stressful classes and the topics we covered are very cool and interesting and applicable to life. I found it very cool to learn the science behind how different biomedical imaging modalities we see in the hospitals or in research labs work. I also gained a greater appreciation for the quality of images we can gain from these devices and how quick these images can be obtained. Overall, you see and learn a lot of cool stuff in this course.
This class is PHENOMENAL. If you are thinking of taking it, definitely do it. The workload is beyond reasonable, Linsey is super knowledgeable, and all of the assignments are super fun.
This is an awesome class if you're interested in the science behind different imaging modalities! I feel that I have a general understanding of the difference in information gathered and applications of each imaging modality even though we only learned about each for two weeks. The workload is very manageable and actually worth the time.
