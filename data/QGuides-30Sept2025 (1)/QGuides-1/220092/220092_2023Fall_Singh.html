1000% TAKE THIS CLASS! WE don't take about technology and mental health enough and this class completely changed my perspective on social media. I feel like I actually contributed to class discussions and even deleted some social media apps in the process (which led to much better attention span over the semester); Dr. Singh is one of the kindest professors I've had here. She cares about her students, makes class really engaging with frequent discussions and group work. She is extremely knowledgeable in clinical psych, speaks about her own experiences, and is pretty interesting to listen to. The class is super chill, and it never felt like a chore. 2–3 light readings a week and you only have to respond to two in the whole semester, an assignment ~every other week (the longest was a 4–5 page group paper & presentation but we had like 3 weeks for it), and 2 hour lecture once a week flew by (plus she always gave a break halfway through and brought us pizza on presentation day). The weekly appchats (you only do one a smester) were really cool because we got to discuss our own experiences with social media apps, some that I had never even heard of. Getting to design our own app at the end and hear from a principal at a venture capital firm for feedback made me feel like I was at HBS or on Shark Tank. Overall, enjoyed this course so much I'm taking another one with her in the spring so highly highly recommend.

What a fantastic course. Truly, there was never a time when I was bored in this class, which is high praise given the fact that it was a two–hour lecture in the late afternoon. Dr. Singh is a fascinating lecturer who brings a breadth of experience to the table, ranging from her clinical practice to her business experience in venture capital. This made the class feel multidimensional; one week, we researched clinical technology interventions on a disorder of interest, and the next, we were pitching a technological intervention of our own to a venture capitalist. And, considering we spend hours per day on social media, it is only wise to take the time to deeply understand how were are being influenced by these platforms. Doing so has radically changed my social media use for the better. Absolutely take this course if you get the chance!

Grading is also easy. A good amount of group work.
