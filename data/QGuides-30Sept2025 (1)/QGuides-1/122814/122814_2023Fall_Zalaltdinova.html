What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Prof. Zalaltidnova is the most engaged and enthusiastic language teacher I have ever had! Her class is fun and challenging and really holds you accountable in your language learning progress.
You learn a lot of new information about the richness of Russia during this class. You can definitely expect to improve your reading and writing skills, but be prepared to put in a lot of time and effort. Although the assignments are well distributed so that you’re not really doing a lot more work in one week than in other weeks, every week overall has many assignments. Despite this, I would recommend this class to others interested in Russian culture and improving their Russian– language skills. Liya really cares about the students learning and understanding the material. She is always ready to help students catch up on any difficult topics, she’s responsive to questions, and she’s overall an understanding person.
