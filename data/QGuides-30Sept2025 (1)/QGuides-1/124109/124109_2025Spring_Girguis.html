Fair warning: your assignments won't be graded until the very end of the semester leaving a big ?? of where you stand grade wise. If you complete everything you should be fine, but you definitely can fall into a pit if you aren't careful.
Take this course but know that you will only get out as much as you put in. Really engage with the readings, ask questions of the guest presenters, and speak frequently with the course staff. The assignments and workload is not bad and if you put in the effort and time you have the potential to learn a ton from this course.
This class was really fun and a good way to spend my Friday mornings. We read some really interesting papers and the rotating weekly speaker setup gave us the opportunity to hear from so many amazing microbiologists/adjacent–scientists.
