That being said, the course is very clearly geared towards sophomores who plan on declaring IB, as a student who took this not as a sophomore, and not as an IB concentrator, I sometimes found this a bit frustrating. It is pretty easy to get a good grade, but I did wish the course was a bit more rigorous. Because it has to cover so much, we often had to skim over topics, and since we weren't ever formally tested on them I finished the class feeling a bit unsatisfied with my knowledge. That said, if you have to take this as an OEB concentrator, don't worry; it is not very stressful and has some fun and interesting lectures and great professors.
