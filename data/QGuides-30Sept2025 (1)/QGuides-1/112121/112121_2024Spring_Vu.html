If Kelly's teaching this again, this is a must–take
This is by far my favorite statistics course I have taken! The content is interesting, and Kelly is a great lecturer plus really gets to know everyone in the class. The problem sets can get long and tedious some weeks (though not nearly as bad as 110 or 111), but there is more than enough office hours support to get through it.
Take it!
I would definitely encourage students to take this to change up their typical statistics course–load. Kelly is an absolutely wonderful teacher and will ensure you are supported throughout the course. I hope to see more courses like this in the department going forward!
Take this class!! It made me actually feel confident about my abilities as a statistician and my choice of concentration. The material is presented in such a well–thought–out way and problem sets actually reinforce what you learn in class. The material is really practical and the experience you get working with famous, real–world datasets is great.
Stat 160 is a great class. Kelly is a great lecturer and really cares about her students. The amount of R required on the assignments may take some time to get used to, but are very doable if you put enough time into it. The notations can get very messy from all the subscripts, but Kelly makes sure to keep the notation consistent (and it is incredible that she does), so this is not really a problem. This class probably won't be offered again too soon, but if it is, I would strongly recommend it!
This course is AMAZING, esp as a stat elective. It is incredibly useful if you'll be using survey data in your future work (which I am as a data scientist), and the teaching is impeccable. Kelly ran the course like it was her 10th time, despite it being her first. The notes are clear, the lecture notes by the TFs were super helpful, the snacks were always appreciated, and every assignment felt purposeful and advanced my understanding of the field. Could not recommend enough if you've taken 110 and 111 and want to learn about surveys. Enjoy!!!!
