Even if Katie doesn't teach this class, the teachers in this department do their best to make this course fun and enjoyable for everyone, especially since they appreciate students showing continued interest in this department and language.
Be prepared to put in a lot of time and work into the daily assignments.
This is a great course that pivots towards textual study & therefore feels like a more culturally engaged way of learning the language.
Take this class! Katie is an incredible teacher and the classroom environment is so supportive. Being able to apply previous knowledge of the Italian language to an interesting topic, Italian literature, really helps with comprehension of the language and culture of Italy. I adored this class and looked forward to every section meeting. There is a lot of homework, but it always pertains to class topics, not busy–work, so it's helpful in comprehension.
You should absolutely take this class! You will learn so much about the Italian language and culture and you will improve more than you think. The materials are super fun to engage with and the structure of the class is really stimulating. It's definitely a lot of work, but it's so so worth it!
Take this course!!!! Ital20 requires a fair amount of work outside of class each week, but the good thing about this is that no single assignment is weighted super heavily. You will be exposed to many different Italian books and films, which I found very enjoyable. The small class size facilitates rich discussion and connection. It is definitely possible to do extremely well in the course as long as you keep up with the work.
Great course with a great class community! I really feel that my Italian skills reached a new height.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!! You will learn so much about the Italian language in culture in this class, and I would say it's pretty reasonable for the most part! The course is built on learning from Italian literature/films, so much of our assignments are reading short stories, watching films, or listening to music. This makes the assignments super enjoyable, and at times don't even feel like homework! The exams and the grading on exams are super reasonable, so definitely don't get dissuaded by those. The weekly workload was also reasonable so long as you pace out the work and try to get it done early! Classes were also always soooo much fun, we would mostly do small group / full group discussions about the pieces (suuuper laid back, it's always just a fun coversation), and at times games in class or fun skits. This class also doesn't have a 3 hour final!! There's a small in–class final exam (super reasonable) and a final project (which is also extremely reasonable and honestly kind of fun... you have to create a genre and write a short story for your genre). Seriously take this class!!! Definitely don't worry about your performance––the teaching team is so kind and understanding!! Also if you have the chance, take this class with Katie!!! She's so helpful and her teaching style is the best for a language class!!
Take this course! Do NOT end your Italian studies with Italian 11. You read and watch some awesome stuff, the instructors are incredible, plus it’s reviewing everything you’ve already learned in the previous courses.
