This is a great course to take if you already speak Spanish, and Jaque is a super kind, friendly and fun instructor!
Would definitely recommend taking this course if you are interested in learning Brazilian Portuguese. People came with very varying levels of Spanish, from native speaker to only a few years, and everyone did fine. It was interesting linguistically to compare the three languages (Spanish, Portuguese, and English) but I am not leaving the class feeling like I would be confident having a full conversation in Portuguese.
You get what you put in. If you study more you can likely advance more. It's a good fifth class. Some days are more stressful than others but for the most part it's pretty light. Some projects are pretty high time commitment. If you can I think I'd recommend 15S instead to learn more. But otherwise a pretty good course. I'm a senior and wish I'd taken this class earlier so I could continue learning.
This course is a great one to take! I would recommend spending time reviewing outside of class so you remember everything and to advance your learning, but overall a great course and Jaqui is great!
I liked this course, it's very fun and refreshing compared to the rest of my courses. I got to learn a lot about Brazilian culture and it was nice. The professor (Jaque) is really nice and supportive, I like her a lot. I liked it but as any language course you will progress as much as you put effort on it.
This was a really good, chill language class. I enjoyed the time I spent with the instructor who is really nice and kind. I do wish that the class was a bit more structured and organized in terms of lesson plans and assignments because it did feel like we jumped all over the place very randomly. But all in all, I am happy I got to take the class and learn the basics in Portuguese.
Take this course the instructor is super nice and she is really good at helping students learn the language
TAKE THIS COURSE! I took this class as my fifth and it was extremely helpful in learning the language while also being very manageable and not time–consuming! Jaque is amazing at giving feedback and her activities are always so entertaining! The small class setting also means I made a lot of friends who I could practice Portuguese with!
You should take this class. It was my favorite class at Harvard.
