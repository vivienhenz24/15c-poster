Take this class, it is hands down the BEST class I have taken at Harvard so far. The course isn't particularly intensive, it's a three–hour lab once a week, so if you are efficient in class, you can complete the majority of your work within class each week. (If you don't do the assignments in class, expect to spend 2–3 hours a week outside of class working). In terms of the assignments, they were all labs with associated lab write–ups. I have never had more fun learning about topics than I have in this class. If you're interested in anthropology or archeology, and you'd like to get some hands–on experience doing the work that field professionals do, I would highly recommend this course. Sarah is an excellent teacher who has a fantastic way of teaching without lecturing, and she was always available and ready to help with any difficulties. 10/10.
