Comments
Although intensive, this course is a great way to learn Italian and to learn a lot of it quickly. The teaching staff, Italian department, and the students are all so lovely and supportive, and you really feel included and involved when in class or beyond class. I felt that my knowledge and understanding of Italian increased dramatically after taking this course, and I would recommend it to anyone looking to sharpen their previous Italian knowledge, but also learn a new, beautiful language.
Take the section at 9 am. First, it is not that early. Second, you join an incredible community of similarly committed Italiani. Prof. Tragni is a fantastic teacher and will transform your language learning experience. Harvard language classes are so much better than high school. You will be fully communicative in Italian by the end of Italian 11.
This course will be the best 9am you will ever take. Even if you struggle, professsoressa will 100% help and make sure you succeed.
If you have any interest or curiosity at all about Italian language and culture, Ital 11 is designed for you. Not only will you develop the necessary grammar and vocabulary to communicate in Italian, you will also learn about the culture, gain a linguistic community, and engage with the language in authentic contexts.
Italian 11 is a great course, but be prepared – it is about twice as much work as Italian 10 and very time consuming. If you are already taking a busy schedule don't take this as an easy "add–on."
Professor Tragni is AMAZING. She is so funny and excited about teaching Italian. She genuinely cares about her students and how well they are grasping course materials. Professor Tragni is reachable almost always and encourages participation and engagement. She truly made the class enjoyable.
If you like Italian, this class is a must – you will get exposure to a lot of Italian culture and be able to have conversations in Italian as you progress.
