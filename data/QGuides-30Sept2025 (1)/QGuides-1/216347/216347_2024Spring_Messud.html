Claire is amazing! She will provide a very structured environment to write fiction, and is always encouraging, kind and enthusiastic. It's a great class to learn about how other people approach writing, and how to revise and expand upon ideas you've had previously. I would highly recommend this class to anyone who wants to work on fiction while at Harvard, and who is simply looking for something to root it in and a group to discuss it with.

Claire is both an incredible reader and an incredible instructor. She creates an encouraging and collegial classroom environment where the contributions of students at every experience level with creative writing were valued. As a novice creative writer, I was nervous to take this course, but I soon felt comfortable taking risks to develop a new skill. I highly recommend this course to anyone interested in developing the craft of storytelling.
If you want to be a creative writer, take it!
