To future students taking FAS–GOV 1737, be ready for a hands–on, practical learning experience. The course offers a deep dive into the design and implementation of randomized controlled trials (RCTs), which are essential tools for evaluating public policies. Youll learn not just the theory, but also how to apply it to real–world situations. Be prepared to engage with case studies and design your own RCT proposals, which will help solidify your understanding. Its helpful to have a basic understanding of statistics, but the course will guide you through the key concepts and methods you need. Collaborate with your peers, as working together on assignments will give you different perspectives and enhance your learning. Overall, this course is a great opportunity to develop valuable skills for anyone interested in policy analysis or research.
