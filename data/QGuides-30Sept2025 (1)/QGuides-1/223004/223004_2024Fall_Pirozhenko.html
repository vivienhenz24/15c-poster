What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!!!!! It was wonderful. The texts you read are very, very fun. It is a LOT of work but it will really improve your German, especially reading comprehension.
This course is very difficult in practice, but Dr. Pirozhenko just wants you to learn. She is very enthusiastic. Don't be afraid to meet with her and contact other students to talk about what's going on in the class to make sure you understand things. My advice is to focus more on plots and themes within the texts than trudging through the German language
This is a really fun course that exposes you to fairy tales beyond the Brothers Grimm, and also provides a great opportunity to improve your German writing skills.
A challenging but rewarding class. Dr. Pirozhenko will push you to read challenging texts and then analyze and discuss them, all in German. This class was almost like a literature class taught in German as we read primary and secondary texts and then wrote analytical essays on them. Definitely a serious time and work commitment, but overall a fun time.
At the beginning of the semester, the reading was very difficult to get through because of a break from German, but eventually there came a time when everything fell into place! It was helpful to find a routine.
The course is very demanding in workload, but they are definitely manageable. You will be fully immersed in the language and it is a very rare class you find in the US to be very academic and language focused at the same time.
