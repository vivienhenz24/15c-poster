This was a great course! I learned about the vast expanse of disease in the eye and their neurobiological roots. The course format was very fair, and doable (yet difficult) for someone who is not a neuro concentrator. The presentations and reflections encouraged me to think deeply about the subjects. Prof. Sanes is very knowledgeable and always available to discuss ideas or class concepts.
If you are interested in vision, particularly the neurological side of how the brain processes vision, I could not recommend this course more! Dr. Sanes is extremely nice and willing to discuss different topics. He cares a lot about students and is truly passionate about the topic. However, if you are not interested in the topic this will be a pretty boring class, and I do not recommend taking it. You really have to be interested in this class for it to be worthwhile. I had a phenomenal time and am sad this was probably the last time the course was offered.
