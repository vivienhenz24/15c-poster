Hank is great! Be careful about how theory heavy and difficult the class is though. You will do a lot of MATLAB too.
This is a very tough class, but it's very rewarding once the concepts click. There are only 4 psets, but don't be fooled –– they will take a while (depending on your prior comfort with the subject matter). There is also a course project which lets you apply the concepts from class in depth, but will also take a lot of time.
Dr. Yang is a great lecturer, clearly expert in the material, and provides very helpful detailed lecture notes, and he is very helpful outside of class too! I am sure this class will be even better in future years.
Professor Yang gives clear and thorough lectures. His provided course lecture notes are also very well–written, both for understanding the theory as well as applications through code examples. I'd especially recommend the course to anyone interested in studying the sum–of–squares hierarchy from both of these perspectives.
