Difficult class that will slowly stress you every day. There are so many components to it – 4 exams, 4 big pieces of writing, slack channel you need to check every day and write stuff on to get participation points, offers to go to events outside of class to get more participation points and "tasks" like homework for almost every single class. The actual class and class work is fine, it's just the combination of these components that make this class unnecessarily stressful for students trying to complete the language requirement. I learnt a lot of Spanish, but this class stressed me out much more than it should have. There's just always stuff to do. So overwhelming. If you need to do it, do it and you'll be fine like all of us. If you don't need to do it, I recommend you don't.
