This course offers a fantastic introduction to the climate, not just of Africa, but of nearly the entire Earth, through nearly its entire history. Daniel Green is brilliant and enormously knowledgeable. Expect historical readings as well as scientific readings (about 3–5 a week, so very manageable).
Great Class!
If you have some background in life–sciences/HEB/EPS and want to focus more on how the topics apply to Africa, I recommend this course! It is a low–stress way to explore various topics about African climate. Dr. Green provides timely and thorough feedback on assignments and leads good class discussions, making two hour classes fly by. He is also very accommodating and willing to provide extra help.
Take this course! It's new (I think) but it is so varied and very different from anything else I've taken before. The assignments are very reasonable and Dr Green is very good with feedback. Discussion was interesting and I liked the structure of the assignments in particular, which meant that you were never under too much pressure at any point during the semester. So glad I took it.
