Comments
If you genuinely want to study film and if movies have a significant influence on your creative field, then take this class. Otherwise, you might miss out. Don't expect to watch "La La Land" or Marvel movies here.
TAKE THIS CLASS! I was so skeptical coming in, as I had taken no film class before. However, this changed my perspective on not only films, but also the industry that surrounds us. Each and every film we watched was so unique and so refreshing. Some films are for sure extremely weird, but that is the best part about the course. Every filmmaker we learned about is so passionately connected to the art of filmmaking that you start to feel their obsession. Take this course if you truly want to see if you love filmmaking! It will, absolutely 100%, push you out of your comfort zone, but it will also, absolutely 100%, teach you more than you could ever imagine.
Professor Guest facilitates seminars expertly. All students get involved in discussions and are invested and engaged in the topics. It goes without saying, but getting to watch dozens of 16mm experimental films in a cinema for 13 weeks straight is a ridiculous privilege, so taking a course like this is a once–in–a–lifetime opportunity. If you love love films or barely ever watch movies, this class is so great for you because either way it is going to challenge all the assumptions you have about film as a medium. It is impossible to come through this course without having a dramatically altered perspective on the potential of the film medium in art. Professor Guest has an encyclopedic knowledge of these films and film history in general so he is an excellent person to lead rich discussions.
Take this class! If you want to expand and challenge your perceptions of cinema, there isn't a better class to do that than this one. Hayden is an amazing professor and you will not regret it.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!!!!
The most wonderful thing Harvard has to offer students of film are the incredible collections at the HFA!! This class is such a wonderful way to explore them.
TAKE THIS CLASS! Professor Guest's passion about the topic makes for extremely interesting and insightful discussions, and each week is so different from the previous one so it's always exciting. I enjoyed this class so much.
