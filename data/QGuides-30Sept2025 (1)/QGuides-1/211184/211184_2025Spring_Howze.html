Comments
This is a great class if you're looking to understand more about the role theater and the arts play in exploring identities and different intersections. Get ready to try a whole variety of exercises and to be pushed out of your comfort zone at times. The work can feel draining but it is rewarding.
Professor Howze is deeply caring and an expert in his field and the arts. I loved the broad approach he took to playwriting and the various multimedia art forms he incorporated in to the syllabus. It is clear he cares about the individual growth of his students greatly and it was a privilege to learn from him!
