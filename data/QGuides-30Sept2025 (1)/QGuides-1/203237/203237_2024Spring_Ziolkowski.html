Awesome class! If you've been taking Latin, it's really unique to look at its use during this completely different time period. The often colloquial nature of medieval latin really helped me practice just reading the language when before I had spent more time with a dictionary and grammar notes than anything else. So super great to boost your reading skills and read in a different context!
This class is really fun if you're into medieval Latin or medieval studies! You learn a lot about the linguistic evolution of Latin as medieval Europe changed, which I found super interesting. It's definitely a class where you get out what you put into it––it's not hard to do reasonably well if you don't have the time to dedicate to it, but you can learn so much by putting in the extra effort. It also definitely depends highly on your TF; Phil was not my favorite teacher ever, but the material was great.
this course is amazing, though almost exclusively because of the professing genius of Phil Wilson.
