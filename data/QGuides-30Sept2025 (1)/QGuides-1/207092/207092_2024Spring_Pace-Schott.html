I personally think the lectures could have been more engaging as well, but I'm not sure how. Overall a reasonable and chill class though but it's very much you get out what you put in
The materials in this course were all very interesting and applicable. I really enjoyed the sleep and dream diary we had to keep.
I really loved this class!! The professor is wonderful and gives amazing lectures. The workload was extremely manageable and actually useful for the course. I found the concepts really interesting and easy to grasp, even as someone with 0 background in neuroscience.
This is a very interesting class. You can choose any topic surrounding sleep and mental health for the final paper. Very manageable workload and enjoyable to learn the content.
Take this class! Professor Pace–Schott is so kind and genuinely cares about his students' learning. He is extremely passionate on the subject and will help you out whenever you need. The assignments are graded very fairly and you always feel supported.
I would tell future students that this is a good MBB elective to take if you want to take a class that is interesting and low stress! You don't learn as much about sleep and mental health together as I would have liked but the class is more than manageable. You have one paper, one mini paper, and one quiz and if you give yourself enough time to do them all, you're golden.
Dr. Pace–Schott is an amazing professor and has so much enthusiasm for this course. He is very understanding of every student's particular situation and is always willing to listen and talk about subject matter or other related topics. I would highly recommend taking this course if you can get the lottery for it. The discussions are really interesting and it has been one of my favorite classes this semester.
