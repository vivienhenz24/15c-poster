I wish I had started taking Japanese a year sooner because the class and the whole teaching staff is amazing and felt like a community. I enjoyed being able to come back after one semester to familiar faces, and to know that there's ample support for me here. This course is also the perfect blend of challenging and interesting and keeps language learning fun. You will have to put in the consistent daily work to get the hang of the language, and I feel like there are plenty of opportunities for you to do that –– through homework, drills, and the frequent Office Hours with any of the instructors.
Japanese Ba and Bb are the best classes Ive taken at Harvard. Learning a new language may seem hard, but all the senseis make it a very approachable and smooth process! After talking to my friends at other universities and even in other language programs at Harvard, I think the Japanese Language Program is really the best! We learn a lot of contents compared to others but with much lower stress. All the senseis really want us to learn Japanese in a fun way, and Ive learned so much over the past year without ever feeling too stressed about the class and my grade because I feel internally motivated to study every day and learn more. PLEASE TAKE THIS CLASS!!
You will never regret to take Japanese class at Harvard!
