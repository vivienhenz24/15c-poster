A great way to get involved in behavioural research at HBS! You'll have flexibility with workload, assignments, and get to work on an interesting project!
This course can be a great change of pace from a normal class structure. It is interesting to see the behind the scenes of real psychological research. I'd recommend this course to anyone interested in the role of psychology in business, regardless of whether you are specifically looking to go into research. It gives you a new perspective on psychology and provides an opportunity to develop meaningful relationships with researchers at HBS who are doing very interesting work.
Take this opportunity to learn something new and do not be afraid of research that may not have caught your eye in the first place.
A really chill course that provides an awesome introduction to the reality of research in organizational psychology.
