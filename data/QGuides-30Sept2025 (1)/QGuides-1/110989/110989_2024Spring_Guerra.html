You will learn a ton in this course, even if it feels like a lot of work and the content moves quickly (a lot of the content builds on itself in this course, so if you don't feel like you get something at first you will certainly have more opportunities to practice it). The CAs are fantastic, and MQC is an incredibly helpful resource. I had a very positive experience in this course, and would certainly recommend it to anyone looking to take a linear algebra/differential equations course.
