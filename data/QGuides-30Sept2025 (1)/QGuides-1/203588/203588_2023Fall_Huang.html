TAKE THIS CLASS!! Karen is an amazing instructor and this class is one of my favorite classes I've taken at Harvard. Our discussions were incredibly meaningful, and I absolutely loved all of the course materials she assigned (ranging from comedy movies to poems to tweets). If you are Asian American, I think this class is a must–take. This course shed light on themes I've never had the chance to examine in an academic setting before.
THIS CLASS IS AWESOME! I learned so much about Asian American history and culture and really enjoyed learning from Professor Huang. The materials were really interesting and I often looked forward to reading them every week. Professor Huang is excellent and led really thoughtful discussions. Overall 10/10 class! I had a wonderful time taking this course. The syllabus contains so much diversity in ethnic groups, historical periods, and media as you learn more about Asian American culture. It's an inviting, easygoing environment that still encourages you to take intellectual risks.

Take it if you're interested in being exposed to Asian American media's connection with the history of Asians in the United States. This was my first time taking a course in the department and I'm someone who picks up a nonfiction book over a novel if given the choice, but I found the course material and discussions that we had incredibly engaging and educational.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! So many amazing materials to consider (so glad to read Bulosan's essays and to watch Past Lives for the first time), and it felt like we tackled so many aspects of Asian American history from Bhagat Singh Thind to Cambodian America to the 1992 LA riots. Prof. Huang created such a welcoming class and I felt like we were able to share a lot about our personal experiences and thoughts in a safe space; she was also very accessible if we had any questions or wanted any feedback for assignments. Only minor gripe is that this isn't really a class about Asian American culture, per se, but more so an intro to Asian American history and studies. But otherwise, incredible experience.
