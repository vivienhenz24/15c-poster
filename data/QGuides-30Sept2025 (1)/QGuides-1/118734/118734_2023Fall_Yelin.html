I didn't find the lectures to be always very clear, so that is a consideration
The material taught makes this class worth taking, and the environment is quite relaxed. I think lectures could be confusing, but problem sets were designed quite well, so by the end of a week, I understood what we had done. Susanne and Abigail (our TF for the semester) are great!
Material is largely quantum treatment of abstract and atomic electron quantum systems, flexibility in thinking about such systems is a must.
Take it if you have the right background. I enjoyed it and learned a lot, but I also struggled a lot. Perhaps i would have gotten infinitely more had i take some other classes that would have thoroughly presented the material covered in 285b. Prof. Yelin and Abigail were fabulous, and i think lecture were overall engaging and always very interesting. Perhaps some were a little disorganized and hard to follow, but Prof. Yelin is extremely knowledgeable and super friendly/accessible, so you can always reach out to her and ask questions
