HEB 1378 is an amazing class!! Definitely take it take it take it!! I believe that if you are in the science field, you should take this class. From popular science writing to policy writing or writing scientific papers/grant proposals, this class teaches you many aspects and areas of scientific writing. This class is one of the classes that you can come back to and use the things you learned as a reference because it covers a wide range of things, and you can decide to pursue some of them if you realize that you are really interested in the field. Dr. Alex is amazing and working with her and learning from her is the one of the best parts of the course. She is very approachable, supportive, and helpful, and works with you on your project. It is a class that make you think about different forms of science communication through lecture discussions and weekly assignments. You choose a project that you will work on throughout the semester and work on that project, and meanwhile learn all these different types of scientific writing. Great course, and I absolutely loved it.

You have a lot of flexibility to focus on the kind of writing that is most interesting to you (journalism, research, etc.). There are a lot of opportunities for peer feedback, and the instructor is very supportive!
