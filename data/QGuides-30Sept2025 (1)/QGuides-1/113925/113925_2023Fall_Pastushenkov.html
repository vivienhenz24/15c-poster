What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is amazing. Dimitri is a great teacher that, despite you having to wake up at 9am everyday for class, will put a smile on your face and make the class engaging. As an 8–credit course, it is a bit tougher than your average course, but in my experience the workload did not feel equal to one of two courses. The teaching staff is really reasonable and will work together with you on any problems, scheduling questions etc. you will have. There is really nothing to complain about, and most importantly, you will be shocked by how much of a language you can learn in 3 months.
TAKE IT!! This class brought me so much joy. You learn an amazing amount very quickly. And despite the number of assignments and tests, it never got overwhelming. Russian just became a part of my life – it stopped feeling like a class. You also get close to the other students because you see them every single day for multiple hours. Our class had such good banter and inside jokes that I was genuinely happy to get out of bed at 9am every day. Lastly, I cannot overstate how supportive the teaching staff is. Dima and Nadia are so kind and put it so much work. They were always willing to adapt the course to our needs and pace, while keeping us on track. It was nice to learn Russian for the first time from people who grew up in Russia. Their cultural insights made the language come alive outside of the formal grammar we were learning. They are amazing teachers. The fact that our entire class is continuing with intensive intermediate is a testament to how good the class is.
Take this class, you are making the right choice.
