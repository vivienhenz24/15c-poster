What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class definitely had less parameters and rubrics than my other classes, which I do not think is bad necessarily but if you are the type of student who really needs organization and rules, this might not be the class for you.
This course was incredible!!! Everyone should take this course! Professor Moises Lino e Silva is the sweetest person. He is magnificently knowledgeable about queer anthropology (his book is amazing) and he will be so supportive to everyone during discussion. I learned so much about different aspects of queer identity in the global south, I also loved that this course largely rejected the use of theory, instead relying on individual case studies and centering human lives over lofty theory. As my first WGS class, this was challenging but approachable, and I cannot recommend it highly enough.
The class gives you an opportunity not just to delve into queerness in other cultural contexts, but to also ground things in your own experiences and those of your peers, which feels amazing, and those community building moments are awesome.
