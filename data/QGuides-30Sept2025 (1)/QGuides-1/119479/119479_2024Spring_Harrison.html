This course will teach you a lot about survey research methodology, much like you'd expect! The lectures aren't especially engaging, but the assignments are very manageable and reasonable.
This is a very boring but manageable course. If you're genuinely interested in surveys, it seems like a good course for you, but if not you definitely won't be having much fun. For a QRD class, it's an absolute gem, so I recommend it if you're not very mathematically inclined.
This course is the most relaxed way to complete the QRD requirement. I was scared of the requirement as I'd largely taken humanities courses, but this course eased me into the QRD component and was never crazily challenging. The teaching staff was incredible as well.
Take this class! It may seem irrelevant to the type of work you want to do, depending on what career you want to go to, but the information you learn from this course will help you through whatever path you may want to go through. It's necessary information everyone should take the time to learn!
Chase is super sweet. Definitely on the gemmier side of classes, but you'll have to stay on top of individual and group assignments. The final project is very rewarding and can be very interesting if you make it so. EASY way to fill QRD!
Take this class! Accessible QRD and you'll learn a lot. Surveys are something we encounter daily–– this course has changed the way I think about survey data for the better.
