What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this course to get a wide breadth of knowledge of Southeast Asian literature it was really interesting to read all these works (many of which were welcome surprises) and to engage in comparisons. I do wish that there was more depth in the historical background and analysis we did but since it is a survey course that's a very understandable limitation. Professor Lienau is great and really passionate about the topics!
This is a great course for those truly passionate about SEA history and literature. It is a true gem in the comparative literature department, as is Professor Lienau! The professor is a brilliant and knowledgeable literary scholar, an incredible resource, and an eloquent educator. I was constantly learning new words in her class. It was inspiring to realize the continuities across regions and to learn about our oft–forgotten history. For example, themes of grief, loss of nation, class distinction, gender roles, etc.. were truly inspiring to me as a literary scholar. It's a true survey class, so the expectations are not too high while the support is generous from the professor. You will learn so so much, and I really enjoyed the work I produced in the class. It was great that we had a diversity of classmates as well, although I must say the most profound observations were made from the professor herself. It's not a must–take class by any means, but a very strong one to add to your schedule, especially if you have a passion for literature and SEA as I do.
I LOVE COMPLIT 100!!! I really, really appreciated getting to study Southeast Asian fiction in a formal setting, and with such an intelligent and kind professor too. This class was the highlight of my sophomore spring, and has inspired me to continue engaging in Southeast Asian literature beyond the classroom. Our class discussions were incredibly generative for me, and Professor Lienau's precise disentangling of themes in every reading has been formative to my own understanding of literary analysis.
Professor Lienau is an absolutely amazing professor. My world view has opened so much since taking this course and I am so grateful for this opportunity. From the films selected, literature read, and the quality of our discussions, this was one of the best classes I have ever taken at Harvard. Take this class or any other class offered by Prof. Lienau! LOVED this class!!
Take this class!!! Prof. Lienau is so kind and eloquent. You'll learn so much about a beautiful region!
