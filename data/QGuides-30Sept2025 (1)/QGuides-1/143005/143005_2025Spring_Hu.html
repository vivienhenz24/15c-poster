Professor Hu is truly one–of–a–kind professor, this class was very informative but also chill in a way that I think really helped make the most of the material. Probably the class that was the best use of my time this semester, I felt like I learned a lot with a reasonable workload. Evelyn does a great job really getting to know her students, enjoy getting to know one of the coolest people at Harvard!
Take this class!! Evelyn is such a great professor and she cares so deeply about her students. This class was great to learn more about the application of EM concepts and to learn how to best utilize different concepts to develop new technologies. Also, the class is structured well. You will either have a lab report or a homework due in a week, not both in one week. Also, a large portion of the class is also dedicated to getting to know your classmates, which I feel like is very rare in a STEM class. Definitely take this if you are looking to learn a lot and have hands on practice!
This course builds strong intuition around fundamental electromagnetic concepts covered in introductory physics, and Professor Evelyn is incredibly supportive and engaging. Overall, it's a fantastic course.

Take this class!
