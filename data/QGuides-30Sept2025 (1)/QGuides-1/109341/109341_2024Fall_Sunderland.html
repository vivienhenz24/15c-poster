What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
You definitely learn a lot about research skills in environmental engineering, but this class was much more literature review, policy, and health related than I thought it would be. I found the data collection, trips, and analysis much more interesting, but they were hard to follow as they were mostly done for us and we didn't do any of our own coding. This class is great if you're interested in environmental effects on health or science writing and want to look closely at one issue in preparation for research. I would recommend some prior research work though and ideally be a junior/senior.
The class can definitely be challenging since there is so much writing, and you will have to dedicate time for the writing outside of class. However, I have truly learned so much by spending so much time understanding the results of our research, and I feel like I have been exposed to a field of research that I never thought I would actually be interested in.
I really enjoyed this course, and felt like I learned a lot. If possible try to engage with research before taking the course as it makes things much easier to understand.
Take this class, especially if you are interested in becoming a scientist! The entire class is a deep dive into one research study where we actually complete our own research in the field, work on understanding the data and doing a data analysis, and then presenting the data and writing up an academic paper about it. The field trips were so much fun, and Elsie is a wonderful professor who provides so much valuable feedback into your work while not being overbearing! I highly recommend the class.
Great class, would recommend to anyone. Elsie is wonderful, and the class is a great balance between interesting, applicable, and manageable.
Good opportunity to see what research is like! Take accountability for your learning early to avoid sturggling with the final report
This is one of the most unique and rewarding classes I have taken at Harvard. The course material is very interesting and you get a great hands on experience during the lab exercises. Professor Sunderland is amazing and provides very in–depth feedback for all of your written work. If you take the class, I recommend taking extra time to really understand the lab procedures and concepts because it can be hard to keep everything fresh in your mind throughout the semester once you are writing your final report. I highly recommend the course!
Fabulous course! Working with Elsie is amazing, and the class is manageable while still being engaging. Lots of group work during the course, so be prepared for that. Very useful skills learned in this course and the field trips are really fun.
