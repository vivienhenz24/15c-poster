Highly recommend this class if you enjoy chamber music and want a chill music class!
Please, PLEASE take 189, you'd be SO remiss not to take it. You get out of this course what you put in, and there is SO much you can get out of this course if you rehearse seriously with your group. It's a really special opportunity to have access to the Parker Quartet, and they are incredibly smart and sweet people. Special shoutout to Daniel Chong and Kathy Chi. They are both really sharp and have extremely tangible and meaningful advice. Also, workload is not overwhelming at all.
189 is a wonderful opportunity to play chamber music with talented peers and receive coaching from members of a prominent string quartet and their guests. The commitment is what you make of it depending on whether you're after a professional final product or are just looking to have fun.
A great course!
Very fun experience with great coaches. Low stress environment, but a very valuable experience. The musicians and friends that you meet are amazing. If you are a pianist, prepare to practice A LOT.
If you're a musician period, JOIN THE CLASS. It's a great opportunity to meet new people and collaborate. Close friendships are often formed through music, and that was definitely the case for me. You get to pick which piece you play for the class and the performance classes are spaced out nicely. Everyone only performs once. IT'S A GREAT CLASS. Highly recommend.
Take this class!!!!! Your chamber group determines everything about the class. The Parker quartet is incredible, so the coaching is always good.
Take this course if you enjoy playing chamber music! You'll learn lots, and grow closer to your chamber mates!
This class is a great option for those wanting to do chamber music in college (ideally with a chamber group already in mind). While it is to some extent what you make of it, the Parker Quartet is lovely; I've always felt as if expectations in coachings are high enough that we are consistently motivated to rehearse well and at least once weekly, and the feedback you receive is always very helpful even in just improving your mindset towards the music.
This class is typically taken as a 5th class, as the time commitment is sooo little because it consists of 1 hour coaching and a 1 hour rehearsal. I'd recommend signing up for the class with a set group of people that you want to play with!
Fantastic course and fantastic coaches. No better way to stay involved in chamber music as a college student, in my opinion.
This class is such a good experience; would definitely recommend for a breather from your other stressful classes!
Taking this class is an amazing way to explore chamber music for the first time or develop as a musician. It was also my most fun class this semester, so if you simply enjoy playing, get a group and take the class together.
