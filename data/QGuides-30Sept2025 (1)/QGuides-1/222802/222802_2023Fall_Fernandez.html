What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The course was incredibly engaging and thought–provoking. The way it delved into the subject of orientalism, especially in the context of technology and futurism, was both unique and enlightening. I really appreciated the educational environment of the course. It felt like a space where everyone's opinions and insights were valued, which made the learning experience even more meaningful.

Professor Fernandez is a standout. His depth of knowledge in the subject is remarkable, and his ability to convey complex ideas in an understandable way is commendable.
What stood out most was his supportiveness and active listening skills. He genuinely values student participation and makes sure everyone feels heard and respected in discussions. His dedication to fostering an engaging and inclusive classroom atmosphere really enhanced my learning experience.

I'd highly recommend this course to any student interested in exploring the nuances of orientalism, especially through the lens of Asian literature and technology.
It's not just about the content; it's about how the course makes you think critically and consider perspectives that you might not have encountered before. This course is a real eye–opener and a great addition to anyone's academic journey.
The content and topic of this course are very exciting and make us critically consider issues around today's cultural and literary representation.
I learned so so much in this course. I didn't even know what Techno–Orientalism was before I took it and now I want to write my thesis on it (!) I really cannot express how much I liked this class well enough in a Q guide response but if you are even considering taking the course, definitely go for it. You'll be exposed to texts and theories you likely won't read or learn about in any other class this is really the vanguard of humanities research and you'll get to read and watch so many cool/fun things!
