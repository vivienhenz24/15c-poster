Make physical flash–cards featuring the experimental techniques' names, how they work, their pros, and their cons. In other words, make sure to really learn these techniques! It is extremely useful for bioinformaticians like me to know what is possible to validate and not.
I had a lot of fun :D Definitely recommended!
Do not worry too much about understanding all of the material in this course. Focus on the big ideas, and delve into the details only when necessary.
I think future students should take this class if a) they want to learn about a broad range of molecular biological techniques, b) they want to practice giving a chalk talk (necessary skill in academia) and c) they want to become closer to their cohort.
There is a lot of busy work and not all of the content will be applicable but the process of designing and defending chalk talks is very valuable.
