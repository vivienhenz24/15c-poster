Comments
This workshop is the best! Laura does an amazing job guiding the discussion and giving great feedback and useful writing tips. I also learned a lot from the other students in the class. The workload is manageable and fun! And since world–bending is a pretty broad category, you can make the prompts your own or use them as an opportunity to branch out of your comfort zone. I improved so much as a writer, grew in confidence writing different genres, and learned a lot about generating ideas and revising past work. Definitely take this course if you love writing!
Take this workshop! Laura is lovely and builds a great environment with which to grow in as a writer
Laura is a wonderful instructor and writer, and anyone who has the opportunity to take her class is lucky to do so. This was a great workshop with really interesting readings that explored all sides of speculative fiction. I'd also recommend this class if you're looking for a workshop with more of a craft focus – Laura spent a good amount of time teaching us the specifics of how to construct a story, which was more useful to me than other workshops which take a more vibey approach to craft.
solid course, good professor, cool readings
Laura is absolutely wonderful. It's a generative class, so be ready to write a lot in the first few weeks, but it'll slow down and you'll get to work on your own short story for the rest of the class while workshopping others. The readings were great, and loved the enthusiasm of other class members. If you want to write any form of speculative fiction, this class is for you!
Amazing class. 10/10! Prof Van den Berg is so kind and so interested in her students' work.
