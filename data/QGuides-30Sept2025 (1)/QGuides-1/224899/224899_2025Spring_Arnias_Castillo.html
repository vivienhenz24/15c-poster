Very rewarding experience
PROFESSOR ARNIAS IS THE BEST HE IS SO KIND AND COOL AND HELPFUL AND PERSONABLE!! def take the class if you are interested in film and wanna give it a try!!
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! Without a doubt, this is the best introductory video/filmmaking course I could have asked for. Luis (prof) and Cecilia (TF) are both wonderful instructors and made every class session enjoyable yet educational. I had zero camera experience coming into the course (and to be honest, was uncertain of my interest in film/video), and I now feel so much more prepared, informed, and excited to continue making film/videos!! The class is involved but not stressful (which is a rarity for a studio/practice based course). You learn how to use a camera and how to edit, you receive extensive and useful feedback, you get to show your work and view your peers' work, and you meet/talk to professional filmmakers. Most of all, you come out of the class with a final project you can be proud of. Absolutely take this course with Luis and Cecilia if you get the chance!!
This class takes a lot of work but for good reason.
This is a studio course, so be prepared for long hours. Know whether or not you're able to handle sitting in front of a computer for hours at a time editing.
