This class was an amazing introduction to Asian American literature! The readings were very interesting and Prof. Mao was great at leading discussions.
I've never taken an English class before, and I've never engaged with Asian American literature before, so this was actually such an eye–opening class, especially for me as an Asian American myself. Readings are all so well–chosen and felt like I was just reading a book I myself picked out and simply wanted to read at leisure – didn't feel like assignment at all! The class itself was also so calming; Sophia is an incredibly empathetic and understanding prof, and whenever she spoke in class, I just immediately felt calm and at peace. I think this class certainly changed my perspective of Asian American lives and backgrounds; for someone who's only experienced and really witnessed one narrative her whole life, this expanded my viewpoint so much and also served as a very healing experience. Assignments were minimal, and Sophia was super understanding and sympathetic about mental health so I never felt like this class was a burden. Would highly recommend to all!!!
