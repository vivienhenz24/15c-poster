What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Dr.Habbal is a very nice man, he cares about people learning. I feel like the lectures didn't feel directly connected to what we were doing in CNS sometimes and I also think we could have started our project earlier. However, I do think this class is useful especially if you don't have much CNS experience. Even if you do, it's nice exposure to other skills you might not have already developed.
You should come into this class having your own initiative. If you only do the minimum assignments, you will learn only a little bit. It would be ideal if you come into this course having an idea you would like to bring into reality using MEMS technology, as (1) you will know what to do for your final project, and (2) you will be much more engaged and learn more from the labs.
