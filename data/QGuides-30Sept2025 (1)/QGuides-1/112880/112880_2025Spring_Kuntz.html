This class is a really fun time –– but also a place to explore emotions, physicality, and connections with your peers through acting. I took a lot away from the class in approaches to acting and acting choices, as well as how to build off of each other while acting or especially improvising with others. I will say a big benefit to the class was having so many talented/open classmates. It starts off with a lot of theatre games/exercises, which are fun, but just be aware that we don't get to scripted scene work until April/May in the class, so there are only two opportunities to work through scenes in the class (monologues and partner scenes), wish I wish there had been a little more of. Johnny and Wes and super insightful and brilliant with directing/workshopping the scenes.
Great experience and would recommend to any. You have to put in ours of preparation outside of class to memorize lines, which can be very very challenging for new comers.
Johnny and Wes are simply the best. So much you can learn from them. Take a chance and take this class!!!
This is one of the most fabulous courses at Harvard! Johnny and Wes are an AMAZING teaching duo, and I think I can speak for my entire class when I say that this class was the highlight of the week. So fun to act in such a safe environment. Definitely ideal if you have some performance experience (in any capacity), but they make it open to all. 11/10. AMAZING. BEAUTIFUL. PERFECT CLASSS!!!!! Johnny and Wes were a lovely duo and I thoroughly enjoyed the ensemble they assembled and cultivated throughout the semester. If you are expecting a rapid pace or many assignments/projects this isn't your class, but if you are open to the wax–on, wax–off approach then this class is for you!
One of, if not my favorite, class that I have ever taken at Harvard. I looked forward to it every Wednesday. I just had so much fun, and the class was at times genuinely cathartic. You really just have to experience it yourself.
