This class is amazing! It is suitable for all students, no matter what concentration you are. Professor Bu is a wonderful professor and cares a lot about his students. His classes are a mixture of watching videos, lectures, and circle discussions, which I found extremely effective. Would highly recommend taking it!
LOVE the class and Dr. Bu!! If you're interested in Korea and art, it's definitely a class worth taking! Korean language classes were a bit too much for my schedule and there weren't many Korean history classes offered but this class was a great balance of history, culture, and art. Dr. Bu is a great professor to talk through your projects/assignments with and so supportive, so go to office hours! ***
Professor Bu is incredibly caring and really tries to give time to his students to get to know them and guide them. I would recommend to anyone who is interested in media studies in Korea. Although you don't need any background in Korean history to do well in this course – I didn't know anything about Korean history going in – I think that it would be helpful to have some knowledge prior/ do extra reading on Korea during the course.
TAKE THIS CLASS!! All the audiovisual materials, films, etc. are materials that I would have never watched/had access to if it were not for this class. Professor Bu is super knowledgable and enthusiastic about the course content which just allowed me to learn a lot more. It is also clear that he is also very supportive of his students and our learning. I think my biggest takeaway was the intersection between art/media and history, a lens in which I never explored Korea before.
Talk the professor as much as possible.
Take this course if you are at all interested in Korean art, or just art in general. Professor Bu is so enthusiastic, and the guest lectures were both memorable. Gemmy overall as no section component.
