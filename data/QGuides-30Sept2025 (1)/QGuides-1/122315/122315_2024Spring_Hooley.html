This is a great tutorial! Dr. Grace is awesome, very knowledgeable, kind, and created a very comfortable environment for all. The readings are very doable (and it’s okay to be confused with some). The assignments are also manageable, especially because you go over and practice the necessary skills for them before they are due.
Don't be intimidated by this being a neuroscience tutorial. The topics vary, and its great that each week, there is a new unit. The readings are not too bad, and you get a real breakdown of the writing process for psychological writing. Work on your paper early, and schedule meetings with your TF ideally with questions prepared. Also, DWF, Maggie, was a great resource, so book with her as well for extra support.
I think that this class can bring success, probably depends a bit on who your TF is but it can be a pretty low key way to engage with the field of psych and learn more. There’s not as much discussion as I would like and a bit of busy work but it’s not too bad.
