What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

This is a super, super intense course, and you will do a ton of work, but you will also learn a ton. Throughout this semester it was my most demanding course, with several quizzes weekly in addition to constant homeworks and frequent exams. As a result, however, I look back and am surprised that I have been able to learn so much in a difficult language in such a short amount of time. Also, the teachers for this course are phenomenal. Both my lecture and section teachers were super kind and good instructors, who wanted me to succeed. Additionally, some of the assignments are pretty fun, like the final project which was to make a film, or the Korean cultural event participation requirement. I would recommend this course for people who want to learn Korean. One side note: I am not Korean and have no prior Korean experience, but there will be a decent amount of people in the class who have grown up in Korean–speaking homes and will naturally come in knowing more than you. At times this would frustrate me, as they would finish exams first etc., but I also learned so much that it didnâ€™t end up bothering me.
