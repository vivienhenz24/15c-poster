Comments
Take this class!!! :)
Korean does take a decent chunk of time each week. I'd recommend that each weekend you mark out time to learn the vocabulary for the week and to do the Workbook content for Conversation 1 and 2 of that week's chapter. This helps to reinforce the vocabulary for the vocab test on Monday and frees up the assignments that would be due on Tues/Thurs at the same time. Once I realized this I started doing much better in the course and having less instances where I felt like I was under time pressure because of the daily homework.
Be prepared to do like at least an hour of korean work every day lol
It is amazing how much Korean you can learn in one semester. If you take this course seriously (which you should) and buy into the teaching methods, you will be incredibly happy with your language proficiency progress!
