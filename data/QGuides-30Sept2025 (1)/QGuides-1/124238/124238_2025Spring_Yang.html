TAKE THIS COURSE IF YOU WANT TO HAVE LOTS OF FUN AND LAUGHS WHILE ALSO RAISING YOUR CHINESE PROFICIENCY AND FLUENCY!!! Definitely one of my favorite courses I've taken at Harvard. Not only you'll become more confident and skillful in using formal chinese to discuss more complex and interesting topics, but you'll also be able to engage with your classmates in various group activities. While this is primarily a language course, you'll also talk about chinese culture, history, internet trends, social issues, and much more. The smaller class environment allows for a bit more personalized learning experience and you really get to know your classmates. Overall, it's an amazing course that I would highly recommend if you're looking to fulfill your foreign language requirement, working towards your chinese language citation, or just want to take chinese in general!
