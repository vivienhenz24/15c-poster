TAKE THIS CLASS!!! It is filled with extremely fun guest lectures from professional artists, field trips to the Harvard art museum and mfa, movie screenings, and hands–on art activities! the workload is pretty low, being just a weekly response paper on you thoughts on the material/reading that feels more like a conversation than an assignment. The material is also incredibly engaging, discussing technological developments in art history like camera obscures and camera lucidas and then attempting to use them in class. Professor Aravi put so much effort into this course and it shows, if you are searching for a freshman seminar, this is the one!
It is quite fun and lots of good memories!!!
I think you should take this class if you are at all interested in art! The class is as much work as you put into it – if you want a chill few hours once a week this course is perfect, and if you want more of a deep dive into art history the assignments and notes provide a good background. The excursions are very fun; we even were able to get into the art storage at the MFA!
Take this class!!!!!! Professor Samuel is amazing and is so passionate and enthusiastic about the material. Truly so fascinating and fun
I loved this class. Prof. Samuel is awesome, and you can tell hes really passionate about the topics we learn about. Its chill and the workload is light, and you still learn so much about art history and get to do fun, relevant hands–on projects too. I had a great experience in this class and would definitely recommend it, especially for people interested in history.
