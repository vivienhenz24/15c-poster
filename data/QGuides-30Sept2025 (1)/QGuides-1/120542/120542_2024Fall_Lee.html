This class is amazing. Professor Lee has so much knowledge to offer and her choices for the course content are perfect. I have learned so, so much from my classmates and from Professor Lee. This class has changed my life and has better informed my experience as an Asian American person, how to engage with history and Asian American history, and my own role I would like to play in learning and impacting the field of Asian American Studies. I loved this class and it was such an honor and privilege to learn from Professor Lee.
not an easy class but you learn a lot and a professor who deeply cares for her students
This is my favorite course I've taken at Harvard! Professor Lee is amazing and so knowledgable about the subject, and all the students in the class were super engaged and enthusiastic about the subject matter. We went over Asian American Studies content through a historiographical lens, which I've never done before, so I learned a lot. We asked a lot of important questions, especially regarding our own positionality. I really wish there were more classes like this at Harvard. We really need them.
This history course was incredibly fulfilling. With Prof. Lee's tenure and this new course, so much has already changed and improved at Harvard. We finally have an institutionalized place for Asian American history at Harvard! For the first time ever! Professor Lee is the among the most brilliant, empathetic, and bold professors I have met at Harvard. Her involvement with community and public–facing initiatives provides a blueprint for where higher education needs to be. Taking this course was so very refreshing as well as challenging. I loved every minute of it, and I would totally do it over again. We had such an incredible class with so much diversity. I can't wait for all of Professor Lee's future courses! Mixing undergrads with PhDs was such a cool experience and there was so much student–led discussion and engagement. Hugeee HIST1965 fan here!
Erika is an incredibly accessible instructor who truly cares about her. Do not hesitate to go to office hours and ask questions. I highly recommend taking this course, especially if you are looking to understand what the current state of play looks like with Asian American history.
