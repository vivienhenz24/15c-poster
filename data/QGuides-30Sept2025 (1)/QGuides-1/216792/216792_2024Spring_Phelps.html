TAKE THIS CLASS! This class is fantastic. Dr. Phelps is such a wonderful lecturer who is so knowledgeable. She really knows what she is talking about but is so humble. We had 4 essay assignments throughout the course with two essay questions per assignment. Max 1 page single spaced per answer. She graded us out of 10 and we had an opportunity to revise our score. She really took the stress out of neuroscience.

This is a fantastic course for anyone with interests in both neuroscience and psychology to learn neuroscience from a psychology–oriented perspective. The class is taught so well and is genuinely focused on your growth! Take it!

The papers we read were difficult to understand and much more neuro–based than psychology. She did a great job of inviting the authors of the papers we read and let us ask them questions directly, which was incredibly cool and also helpful!
