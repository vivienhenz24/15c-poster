Tyler is the most kind, understanding, and uplifting professor you will ever have. He gives such a powerful personal perspective on human rights law while still offering a very clear and objective understanding of the subject. I could not have chosen a better FYSem.
Amazing class! Professor Giannini is incredibly knowledgeable, impactful, and accessible about humanitarian legal aid. The class is a pass/fail, so you get out of it what you put in, but I felt super inspired and pushed by our class discussions and assignments. Wonderful course.
This course is awesome!!! Tyler is an excellent professor who has hands–on experience in human rights law (EarthRights International co–founder), and is always eager to share his experience with his students. This class was so manageable in workload and very well–structured in that the readings (some of which are Tyler's papers!) and activities are so rich in insight. Whether or not you have interest in law, this class is so worth it because you'll walk away enlightened in ongoing events.
Take this course! Wonderful professor, wonderful class dynamic! Had an incredible semester–––you learn much without the pressure of grades. Would reccomend to anyone interested in international relations, law, and power dynamics between corporations and marginalized groups.
Solid class. You will spend more time on the practical application of skills (HOW to interact with communities as a human rights lawyer) rather than gaining technical knowledge of different laws, precedents, and when they are applied. Tyler is very kind to his students, I thought that because he taught at the law school, he would be intense but that was not my experience at all! He is clearly passionate about the subject and has a comprehensive understanding of the field. Readings are slightly heavier than other FYSMR classes but are certainly interesting (and yes, you do have to complete them since we spend a significant amount of class time discussing them). I would recommend this if you are curious about the work of human rights lawyers.
This is a good class if you are interested in lawyer stuff. Interesting even if you aren’t but the focus was heavily on this. Tyler is a great professor and makes class, assignments, simulation really fun.
