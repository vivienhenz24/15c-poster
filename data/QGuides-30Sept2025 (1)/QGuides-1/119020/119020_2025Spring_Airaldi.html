TAKE THIS CLASS! I absolutely loved getting to work with Remo, and I really felt like I learned a lot about Acting Shakespeare. This class definitely helps you feel more comfortable as an actor overall. Remo is so incredibly knowledgeable. He is really great about giving everyone 1:1 feedback and really taking the time to workshop a scene with you. You also learn so much from watching your classmates perform. I felt like this class really built a great community! I LOVED THIS CLASS!!!!!!
This class is amazing. Remo is such a great teacher and he truly cares about the subject. He gives amazing feedback and puts so much time into workshopping every scene individually. He truly wants your work to be the best you have the potential to make it.
PLEASE TAKE A CLASS WITH REMO (whether it be this class or a different one, he is great and has truly had such a large impact on my college journey!!!)
I would recommend this course to everyone. If you have a chance to take it, or to take any other class offered by Remo, please do. One of the best experiences I've ever had in a class. You learn so much about both acting and Shakespeare throughout. I truly feel I'm a better performer because of this course.
This is an excellent and accessible course. Remo knows his stuff.
LOVE this class. You can tell this is where Remo really shines as both an actor and instructor. Definitely take this class if you're interested in acting Shakespeare. I had no experience with Shakespeare going into this, but I learned so so much, and Remo creates such a great class environment.
This class is definitely a fair amount of work/time outside of class. Do not underestimate how much time it takes to memorize the scenes and having to rehearse with Remo outside of class definitely also adds on. I took this class for fun and it was enjoyable, but I would maybe recommend more to people who are really passionate about acting. In addition, I felt like I improved a lot with the individual monologues or scenes I was working on but not as an actor overall. Remo is very lovely though, and I am sure if you wanted more out of the class you could work something out with him.
I never particularly liked Shakespeare growing up. However, as an actor, I had many other people tell me that you don't learn to fully appreciate Shakespeare's works until you perform them. After taking this class, I can say this is true. Remo is amazing, and has so much experience with Shakespeare that you can learn from. I learned to see the beauty and the genius in the works, and this style of performance pushed me to grow as an actor. Take this course if it is offered again!
Take this class. Whatever you do, take this class. Even if you're not an actor and you haven't acted since that required middle school play take this class. You will be transformed! If you're worried about the audition, don't be. As long as you try your hardest and express your interest in the essay that you write to apply to the class, things will work in your favor. Secondly, buy in. I found that I had a great time despite being nervous very often because I had bought in. I wanted to be an effective actor. I wanted to leave the floor knowing that I was believable. Remo will give you so many suggestions and so much advice and you should treat them like gold. Don't hesitate to push back if you have doubts or questions though, he will gladly talk you through his reasoning and incorporate your reasoning as well. Oftentimes you might have an instinct that he didn't think of. I became a more confident person because of this class. I want to engage with Shakespeare more because of this class. I gained new friends because of this class. I probably got through my thesis because of this class. (It was really great to take a creative and expressive class in the midst of a silent laboratory semester) I wish I could put into words how I feel about this. I took this class in my senior spring – it was my first TDM class – and that's to say it's never too late to jump in! Although I did wish I had this experience earlier (so if you are an underclassman thinking about taking this class, do so. If you've applied before and didn't get in, apply again.) Loved this class, probably the best one I took at Harvard.
