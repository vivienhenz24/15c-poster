Comments
This course is excellent! Ivy is an amazing, patient professor who presents the material in a clear manner and prepares practice readings that are at exactly the right level, interesting, and helpful. The TF was also amazing, super helpful and patient. My favorite part of this course was my fellow students and the course atmosphere. Everyone was interested in learning the language, but also interested in having fun and interacting with each other. Unlike other courses, people actually take the time to get to know each other, participate in class by asking interesting questions, and have fun.
TAKE THIS CLASS! I took it after having already fulfilled my language requirement, but I wish I had just done Latin instead. The teaching staff, Ivy and Kevin, are incredible, and its so clear how passionate they are about the subject and helping students learn. We take mental breaks during class, do fun and interesting translations, and they even provide vocabulary Quizlets for you. The workload feels manageablejust enough to help you genuinely learn the language without feeling overwhelming, even with classes meeting 3x a week.
If you are someone who needs to fill their foreign language requirement but hates speaking said language in front of others, take Latin I! It is very heavy on reading and translation, and there will be minimal speaking. Also, Professor Livingston is a gem! She always brightened my day and is so knowledgable in Latin that she can easily explain concepts and questions with clarity.
This class is a great way to fill the foregin language requirement. It was not that difficult, and you really need no prior Latin background.
This class is super fun, and if you want to learn Latin, I would highly recommend this course. Make sure you stay on top of the vocabulary and the homework, and you'll be fine in the course. The conjugations and verb endings can be a little bit overwhelming, but if you learn it over time, it's very manageable.
