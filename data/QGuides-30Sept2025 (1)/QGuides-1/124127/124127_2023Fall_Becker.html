GENED 1093 was the first GENED Ive taken during my time at Harvard, and it was definitely one of the best decisions I couldve made. During Fall, 2023, GENED 1093 took away the midterm and mini–paper requirements, making your grade solely dependent off of 4 quizzes given in section (worth 10% each) and a final paper (+ attendance/participation). The quizzes are EXTREMELY easy and the staff also release a topic bank a week beforehand, which provide a list of 5 sample questions of what may be asked. Then, on the day of, the quizzes often provide a list of 5 questions that very closely mimic the topic bank, often only adding a few more specific details on what you need to address within your response. As a result, this made the quizzes a very easy 20/20, especially if you have a nice TF.

In terms of the course itself, the material is definitely very interesting. However, since the majority of the teaching staff are white, the class partially gives white savior complex where it seems like they are focusing on how white people are going to save the world and contribute to the bettering of medicine across the globe. Undoubtedly, the work the team has done, especially some of the Professors, is indeed impressive, but definitely know that it can come off that way on certain topics.

Next, during Fall, 2023, the course began to mandate lecture attendance, with them incorporating PollEv into lectures. However, this was done extremely inconsistently, to where there were only around 5–10 polls throughout the entire semester. If there just so happens to be a poll on the day you missed, this can very easily tank your attendance grade, so be careful!

Additionally, on the first day, our class was told that the reading assignments were very little for the course, which looking back, was a COMPLETE AND UTTER LIE! The readings are often long, and I did not end up touching a single one throughout the entire semester. PLEASE do not waste your time looking it over, as you can just rely on the Topic Banks and lectures to do well on the quizzes in class.

Overall, GENED 1093 is a very enjoyable and chill class. Outside of the few minor criticisms Ive outlined above, it is definitely an outstanding and well designed course that many of us were able to enjoy. Best of luck to all of those enrolling in the future! ****
