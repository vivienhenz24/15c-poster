What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you have an interest in machine learning in Python, this course is likely a great resource. Otherwise, this course is unlikely to be relevant to you. The assignments are long and the material is challenging unless you have a strong computer science background.
This course takes a LOT of time outside of class. If comparing to work load for an EPI course, this one took me 2x the time outside of class than EPI 289. The work and output is very gratifying.
The course is very challenging if you don't have a background in math and coding. You might not get enough out of it otherwise.
The first half of the course was very educational––I learned a lot and got a lot out of it. The second half, the half that focused primarily on current topics in machine learning went over my head and was not the main focus of the class as it was described in the course catalogue. I would tell students to take it to become very comfortable with good coding practices and coding project management but that the amount of time spent on assignments may not ultimately be worth it for them, though it was for me.
