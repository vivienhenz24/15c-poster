Comments
This was one of my favorite philosophy classes at Harvard. By using Sanskrit philosophy as the general orienting lens, you explore several questions in metaphysics, epistemology, philosophy of memory, etc. This is certainly a hard class and requires a lot of patient reading (writing the essays also took a significant amount of time, and Smriti holds you to very high standards), but I found myself often thinking about the material outside of class. It was one of my favorite and most rewarding classes in the phil department.
Take this class! Smriti is a great instructor, she makes the material interesting and easy to understand. The class is incredibly well–structured and the readings are definitely foundational to philosophical thought! I
