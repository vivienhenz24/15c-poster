I LOVED THIS CLASS. My favorite so far at Harvard. It was my first acting class. Tania was a great Professor she taught me a lot and always encouraged me after each performance with great feedback. The class was so much fun I met amazing friends and got to connect with everybody. It was truly one of the best experiences I've had at Harvard. Its not like any other class you really get to express yourself and find freedom as you perform. Its an environment where you get to be yourself and improve as an actress.
I would tell future students that this course is as open–ended as you want it to be. Tania is open to accepting and discussing whatever you put in front of her, which is a huge opportunity the course offers.
