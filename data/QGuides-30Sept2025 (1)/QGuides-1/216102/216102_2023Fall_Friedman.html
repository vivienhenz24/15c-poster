PLEASE TAKE THIS CLASS! It will unequivocally transform your life in such beautiful, profound ways! If you love and appreciate nature or the world around you, you will not believe the ways in which this class will make you contemplate and embrace the beauty of life. You do not need to have any experience whatsoever, just make sure you do all you can to take this class — I genuinely believe everyone would love and grow spiritually and emotionally from this class. Professor Friedman’s and Daniel are the cherry on top with their childlike–love and fascination with plants and their relationship to humanity. If you are prepared to rethink your approach and perception of life and what it means to be living, take this class!!!!!!
Take this class! Ned and Daniel are a dynamic duo who have so much knowledge about trees and the natural world. Being able to escape the Harvard bubble every Monday is a refreshing and valuable opportunity that you should take advantage of. The relationship you build with your tree will be something you carry on throughout your college experience and will make adjusting to Freshman year much easier. The assignments are thought–provoking and varied, and the intersections that trees have with different parts of the world are so special. You will emerge from this class grounded and more humble, with a stronger connection to nature.

This is a very fun class. You generate very strong relationships with the other classmates and the professor. The only downside is the commute (40 minute uber ride, covered by Harvard). On days that you go to observe your tree, it is a bit of a time–sink.

Ned and Daniel are both so knowledgable and do an awesome job in tailoring the course to what you find interesting and what you want to explore.
TAKE THIS CLASS! It was super fun to learn about trees and escape the Harvard bubble multiple times a week to go to the arboretum! Our walks around the forest and discussions about every aspect of trees were always memorable and well worthwhile, and the workload was very manageable. Ned and Daniel are both so passionate about trees and nature in general, and they are also just some of the nicest people you will ever know. Just note that it is a time commitment to go to the arboretum once a week (on top of class), but also a great opportunity to bring and get closer to new friends!!
