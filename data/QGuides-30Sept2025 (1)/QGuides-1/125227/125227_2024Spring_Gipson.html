Expos 40 was an amazing class! You learn so much about public speaking and through a lot of practice you inevitably become gradually better at it! The USP of the class was definitely Terry, who not only did a great job at sharing his enormous expertise with us but also genuinely cared about that everyone does well and creates a really great and close community among everyone in the class that makes it a truly enjoyable experience!
LOVED THIS COURSE! Hands down one of my favorite courses here at Harvard. I grew so much as a speaker and feel that I will actually utilize the skills I obtained in this course going forward. The work in this class is easy in itself but preparing for speeches requires a lot of practice. You also get super close with your class :) Would definitely lottery for it until you get it!
TAKE EXPOS40! It is nothing like Expos 20: it is more fun, no struggle, and a guaranteed A. Expos 40 was one of the best classes I've taken at Harvard, and it really does its job: I have definitely improved my public speaking skills and I am a more confident speaker. The section was a lot of fun and was very engaging. The assignments are pretty easy and are interesting, while exercises really get you to know your classmates. I can't recommend this course enough.
Everyone at Harvard should take this course, even if you think you're already pretty good at public speaking. The course is designed to fit your individual needs and meet you where you're at skill–wise, so you will learn no matter what.
This is a phenomenal class: relatively little work (at least with Terry) and not too difficult, but you learn so much by just thinking about speaking and the aspects of speech delivery. Over time, with dedicated practice, you WILL become better at modifying your speaking style to better captivate your audience and communicate your message––even if you go into the class with relatively solid speaking skills. Terry is very supportive and offers helpful feedback. In this section, there is a lot of time spent listening to others speak or providing feedback to each other, which may get boring BUT is generally quite fun because the class community is so great. All in all, great class community and great low–pressure learning experience.
TAKE THIS COURSE!! As someone that was extremely hesitant to take this course because I knew that I struggled with public speaking and was nervous to write speeches. I could not be happier I took this course and have probably learned more about myself in this course than any other class taken in my four years at Harvard. Most people in the class don't have public speaking experience, so you all are in the same boat from the start. I had no idea the importance of different aspects of speech delivery and how it really impacts things you say. You can apply what you learn in this course to literally any aspect of your life – you also will become really close with your class
Everyone needs to take this class! This course completely strengthened my confidence in public speaking – before taking this course, I would struggle a lot with my confidence in public speaking. I could hold conversations with individuals, but as soon as I had to speak in a formal way (i.e., class presentation, work presentation, answering a question in a big class), I would completely freeze. Now, I feel sooo comfortable in all of these situations. Terry does a lot of impromptu speaking exercises in class, and they are super fun but also the most helpful exercise in building confidence. Terry makes this class extremely fun and you become super class with all of your classmates. I made great friends in this class and I looked forward to going every time!
