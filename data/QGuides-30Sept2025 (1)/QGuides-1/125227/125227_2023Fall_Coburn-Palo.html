Take this class if you can. I would take it every semester if I could.
*** Expos40 gets a lot of hype and maybe a little too much. That being said, it’s a great 4th class and elective. You get out of the class what you put into it. Nicholas was a very chill professor who really supported and cared for his students. He encourages us to make mistakes and learn. However, he doesn’t come from a drama background, so you won’t play theatre games or get any advice on dramatic flare. But, you learn great speech fundamentals with a reasonable workload. The speeches are fun and you can make them as time consuming as you want. You get close to your class and it’s fun!
If you are already comfortable public speaking, this class is probably not for you. It can hone some skills and point you in the right direction, but most of the benefit is just from practice if you already have experience giving speeches and presentations. The assignment due dates can be a bit confusing. But Nick was a great instructor and did a great job faciliating improvement.
