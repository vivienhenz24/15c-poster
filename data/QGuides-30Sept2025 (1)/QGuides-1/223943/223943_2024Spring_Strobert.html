TAKE THIS CLASS!!! The workload is very reasonable and you'll come out of it with a greater appreciation for abstract artwork!
this is quite a serious afvs course, which may or may not match your experience with/hopes for the department. kianja delivers feedback in a very direct manner, which again, depends on what you're looking for. i personally really enjoyed how this course stressed grounding one's practice in past artists' work & learning from others
Go in with an open mind! Abstract art is very challenging if you aren't used to this type of art. You will have so much fun and its a great class.
This class is a fun intro to abstract art, it is very hands–on and it gives you a lot of freedom of what you would like to work on. Kianja and Kathryn were great –– they were supportive while also offering helpful feedback. The workload is also quite manageable. The critiques can get very tedious, as they can last for the entire 3 hour class period with not much said, but there are only a couple of them, so the course overall is quite worth taking.
I would tell them to take it if they have any passion for art. You need to be fueled by your own ambitions in this class.
Great class to take, though I can see someone being frustrated if abstraction is not their thing. Even then, Kianja and Kathryn are great and will work with you to develop your style in a more abstract manner. ***
