This is an awesome class. We watch scandinavian films together and then have revealing discussions about them. I really enjoyed seeing films I probably wouldn't have watched otherwise, and getting large takeaways from them (for example, Phantom Carriage, The Seventh Seal, Persona, Insomnia, Joan of Arc). The discussions were very comfortable, and it was clear that everyone found the films interesting, as we rarely ran out of things to say. I like how the course culminated with the opportunity to create a film of our own. The small size of the course creates a community where people are very comfortable talking to each other and sharing ideas.
