What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Would recommend this course with Prof Yau
As taught by Prof. Yau, this course contains some techniques and ideas that I think are essential for many mathematicians working in and around analysis (including in geometry, statistics, etc.). Though the problem sets are sometimes difficult, I think they are manageable if you are willing to spend some time and are very educational. I emerged from this class with a much greater appreciation for the intuition behind real analysis, which Prof. Yau managed to convey very well (and much less fear).
All students interested in analysis, PDEs, geometry, or probability should take this course. Prof. Yau's treatment of the material will provide a broad and practical introduction to the subject of analysis while providing interesting examples, topics, and fun anecdotes along the way.
