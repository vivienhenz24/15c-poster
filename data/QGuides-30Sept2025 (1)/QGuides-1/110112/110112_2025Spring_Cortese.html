What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this class! It does an incredible job of cultivating a motivated community in music at Harvard. The rehearsals are rigorous, and you will need to spend time outside of class to practice the high level of music that the orchestra plays. However, it is incredibly rewarding, both musically through Fed's leadership and musical expertise, and also through the friends and community that you build through weeks and weeks of practice, rehearsal, and concert!
Take this course and, of course, keep playing in HRO!
