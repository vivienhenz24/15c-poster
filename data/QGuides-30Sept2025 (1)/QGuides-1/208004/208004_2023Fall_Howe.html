What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take it!
This year DTV was optional, but in the end the vast majority of us took it because it let us maintain have one part of the EC fall designed uniquely for the MS/MBAs (both academically and socially). That alone makes the course worthwhile. Tom and Rob are very open to feedback and have a clear interest in making the course valuable to the cohort, so it is worth making time to connect with them outside of class and share your learning goals. The MS part of the class needs to be reworked, and I suspect they'll act on the feedback. Otherwise it is very easy, in line with a typical HBS class with case readings and some exercises. The final project is a cool opportunity to partner with a startup, but could be integrated into the class a bit more consistently.
Overall, all MS/MBAs should take it since it's a great opportunity to get time with and learn from your MS/MBA peers like you did in DTP, EDIM, and TVI.
