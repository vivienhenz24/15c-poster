Comments
This course will teach you most of what you need to know in order to engage in fieldwork. Note that it is time consuming and requires background in phonology, syntax and semantics, so taking it on your first year is not recommended.
(1) This course does take a lot of time – think of it as an opportunity to develop excellent skills in fieldwork, which takes time
(2) Use the personal elicitation sessions to your advantage! Out of these you can get new project & paper ideas (I definitely got quite a few from mine)
