What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Definitely realize that this is an abstract painting class, where you will be expected to make abstract art! It's very open–ended though, and there is as much guidance as you would like (it's a pick your own adventure). I came in thinking I could never understand or create abstract art, but came away with new realizations of my potential style and respect for the style. If you want free range of exploration, take this class.
It doesn't explicitly say in the course catalogue, but this is primarily an abstraction course, so don't be surprised! Maryam and Julia give great feedback, and this course is a nice chance to experiment with different mediums and a lot of liberty on assignments. It is not a main focus of the course, but there is also a nice discussion component.
