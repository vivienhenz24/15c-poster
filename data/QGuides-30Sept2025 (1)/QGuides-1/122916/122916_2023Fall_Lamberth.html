What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This course essentially teaches you how to be a PhD student successfully, and how to prepare for the exams, prospectus, dissertation and job market phases of one's career. Once you've gone through a year of doctoral work already, many (though by no means all) aspects of the course will seem very easy, even redundant, e.g. preparing a conference paper (most of us will have presented more than one of these by this point). If this course were taught in the first semester of the program, it would be more challenging, but also substantially more rewarding. That said, give the assignments your best – they helped me develop ideas that I think will prove really beneficial for exams and prospectus–writing.
