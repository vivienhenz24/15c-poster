This is a good class to take if you want to write casually with others or seriously alone. I wouldn't really recommend it if you're someone who works well under pressure or who needs deadlines and structure to keep you going. There were a couple of people who didn't seem invested in or serious about the class, so if you're sensitive to this then also avoid this class. Paul is very kind and encouraging of everyone though, so it is a laid back environment.
It's a great course and I loved the pieces we read and the discussions we had. Professor Yoon is super friendly and passionate about what he does, but first and foremost he is so enthusiastic about his students and writing in general, which really just made me excited for class each week. I would recommend this course to anyone who wants to receive feedback on their pieces, and also find a really fantastic and safe environment for their ideas and questions.
Paul is one of the most wonderful teachers I have ever at Harvard. He facilitated such a good classroom environment, was INCREDIBLY understanding, and, all in all, has such a clear passion and love for writing.
Highly recommend!
