What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I absolutely loved this class. If you have any remote interest in the subject, I would take it. Professor Bu is so encouraging and an excellent teacher. If you are an undergrad, don't let this being a grad class stop you. Assignments aren't too challenging in of themselves, but you get so much out of the discussions. Also, if things don't make sense, Professor Bu is accessible to help you understand. Highly, highly recommend.
This course is an absolute MUST–TAKE for anyone who's vaguely interested in film/media/documentary studies. The readings are diversified and intellectually engagingthe class keep referring to each week's reading throughout the semester, which demonstrates not only how foundational they are (and how motivated the class are to read them carefully!), but also how deeply they resonate with our ongoing concerns with the social issues. The course is thoughtfully designed, rigorous enough for graduate students while offering a rewarding introduction for undergraduates coming from a totally different field. Professor Bu is a real gem and an inspiring scholar to study with. Because the topics introduced in the course were so interesting and intellectually challenging, all of us approached weekly posts with essay–level depth and the discussion section becomes such a valuable space for reflecting on not just our own but each other's thinking.
