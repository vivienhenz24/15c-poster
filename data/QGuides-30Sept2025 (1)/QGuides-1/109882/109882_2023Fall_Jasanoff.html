This is a great class! Professor Jasanoff is a brilliant mind and lectures well, giving concrete examples of somewhat abstract topics. Sections are helpful for flushing out ideas and making them more understandable, but the readings are usually straightforward and present really interesting arguments about the influence of culture and society on technological development. Take this class if you're interested in some really cool ideas of technological development!

Overall the course was pretty boring. I am surprised that taking a course with Prof. Jasanoff is an ESPP requirement. I do not feel like this course will help me as an ESPP concentrator.

Topics covered in this course are interesting. However, I was really lost in terms of what I was supposed to take away from lectures as well as assignments. There was little to no guidance on how we can utilize the learning in class to real–world problem solving. This course has a strong emphasis on STS networks but I felt explanations to such concepts were not sufficient. I would not recommend this class unless you have working knowledge in the STS field and are ready to get lost.
