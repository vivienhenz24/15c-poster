What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I think this was an excellent class with two really knowledgeable and kind professors. The topic was so interesting, and while it's about the deep past, I think it's incredibly relevant for people to understand. There wasn't a lot of work outside the two exams and the final paper, which was nice. You do have to use Perusal, but it honestly didn't bother me much. I think it makes you engage more with the content of the readings and you get to hear what your classmates think. Most people in the class were very engaged with the topic and we had some great discussions. There were a few issues with the exams, but I don't think this will be the case for future versions of this class. I highly recommend it!
Future students should be prepared to embrace aspects of the class that they may be less familiar with and push themselves to question both their own behavioral models as well as those hypothesized in the past.
