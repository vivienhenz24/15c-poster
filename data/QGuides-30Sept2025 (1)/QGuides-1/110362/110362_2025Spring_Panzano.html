What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Great course! Really brought together more what we learned in HAA96A and fine tuned our architectural drawing skills (esp drawing with Rhino and Photoshop which we didn't do much in the previous course). I feel like i learned so so much through the structure of this course that was more conceptually continuous from beginning to end than 96A. We were given a topic in the very beginning that pretty much stayed through all the various assignments and built up more and more later into the semester which is really enjoyed! Very happy with my last semester of studio, and prepares great for various skills needed for senior thesis!
Go in with an open mind but be prepared for some scrutiny and lots of hours of small edits and redesigns. The course was fun and rewarding but definitely my most stressful of the semester and can be very very overwhelming
Best suited for those pursuing architecture after graduation; the time commitment is equivalent to two advanced–level courses.
