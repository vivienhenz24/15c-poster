Great for everyone regardless of opera background. Loved being able to sing our piece at the end of the semester
If Matt teaches this course (or any other course) in the future, and if you have the inkling of interest/curiosity needed to open this specific Q report, you should take it without hesitation. He is an absolutely incredible mentor and professor, from the kindness and understanding he extends towards students, to the vast expanse of knowledge he carries on contemporary opera and composing music for the voice. I learned so much from being exposed to different operas every week, working in 1–on–1 office hours with Matt, and receiving feedback on my writing assignments more helpful than any English/essay based class I've taken here. That's not even mentioning the hands–on experience of composing the final project, which may seem extremely daunting but ended up being alright even for the majority of us who had no composition experience beforehand. (You can also take this class as a librettist and get paired up with a composer!)

You will not get many opportunities at this school to take a course as accessible and pragmatic, yet still so deeply intellectual, as Music 187. This course was absolutely the highlight of my semester and possibly of my time at Harvard.
