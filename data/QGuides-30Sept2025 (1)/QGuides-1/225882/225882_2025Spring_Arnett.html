This was an amazing class, truly one of my favorites at Harvard!! Dr. Pranji cultivated a super collaborative, thoughtful class environment that was the epitome of a topical seminar. Really really interesting content. Naturally brings together a group of like–minded people with overlapping interests but fascinatingly diverse backgrounds.
Take this course!! Especially if you have an interest in the intersection between neuro and music this course does the best job of directly addressing this intersection in a low–pressure, welcoming, and friendly environment. When I took the class if was 6–8 pm but I was genuinely interested in the topics and Dr. Pranjic is so great so it was definitely worth it! Dr. Pranjic is also super down to earth, approachable, and passionate about this subject matter so it really made the class enjoyable!
This is an amazing class for those who are interested in the musics effects on the brain! Not so much of a gem considering the amount of readings, student presentation and a research paper, but I enjoyed it very much! Dr. Pranjic is amazing! She is very responsive and gives very detailed feedback on my writing all the time!
This is a very interesting course with much to offer if you are interested in these topics or have a personal connection to them.
TAKE THIS COURSE!! First and foremost, Dr. Maria Pranjic is just the most wonderful teacher: she is so incredibly versed in neuroscience of course, but as a musician herself she brings a unique and holistic perspective to this course (which has made it such an incredible experience). She is kind and cultivates community, as well as making sure class runs efficiently and seriously – I have gone from essentially a neuroscience beginner to generating multiple projects and a final paper within one semester because of her investment in my learning. Secondly, this topic is so very important, and Harvard having this discipline (music/arts/creativity and the brain) as a course in the MBB department is absolutely essential. Come prepared to work hard and you will reap the benefits of this amazing course! I sincerely hope the MBB department will continue adding and supporting more courses like this one (for everyone's sake, but selfishly so I can take them too!) :)
MBB 980EE is such an amazing course, and Prof. Panji generates so much enthusiasm about the course and facilitates an amazing conversation amongst the students. While the course was amazing, the students make or break the course with some feeling the need to constantly reiteerate that they have an X amount of music experience or that they have "perfect pitch". Sometimes I felt the course could have delved a bit more into neurobiological topics given that it is a junior seminar, but was a bit simplified for some of the underclassmen that were given the opportunity to join the course. Regardless, the papers were interesting to read and gain knowledge on the far reaching implications that music has in emotions, therapy, and other aspects of our lives.
Take this class!! Whether you come from a science or music background, youll walk away with a new appreciation for both. The material is fascinating, the discussions are thought–provoking, and Marija is not only brilliant but also incredibly kind and supportive. Youll learn about everything from brain oscillations to music therapy to different neurodegenerative diseases and even get to see real EEG recordings in action. I cant recommend this course enough.
