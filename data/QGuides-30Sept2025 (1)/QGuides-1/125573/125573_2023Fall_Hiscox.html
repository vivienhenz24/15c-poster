Professor Hiscox is by far one of the best Professors at Harvard. I was able to learn so much in the duration of this course and I would recommend this seminar so enthusiastically. If you are looking for an interesting course taught by an amazing professor who is truly interested in your growth as a student look no further! This is one of the best courses (if not the best course) I have never taken at Harvard thus far. The material is so interesting, and the contributions you can make in the class are endless. The concepts covered from child labor, regulatory agencies and consumerism were os interesting. Also, I was a student who had limited knowledge in this field of government and economics. But, this course was very digestible and I would advise you apply even if you don't have such an extensive background in the materials covered. As long as you are willing to learn and make contributions from things that you are familiar with, you will do amazingly in the course.
This seminar is one of the best classes I have taken in my time at Harvard. The class is structured very clearly and logically which allowed us to cover many topics within a short time span. Professor Hiscox is also a great teacher who gives really good feedback on our papers and is very encouraging and supportive of his students. I highly recommend this class to anyone who is interested in learning about private governance.
