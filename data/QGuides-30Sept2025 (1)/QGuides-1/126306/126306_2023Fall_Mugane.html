What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Don’t take this course with Professor Chery. The class had very little structure. The lectures were barely productive after the first 45 minutes and most students had to rely on family members to really learn the language. The homework wasn’t really productive to the learning process and frequent enough to really be beneficial the professor also stated many times how he spoke better than he spoke Haitian Creole, which doesn’t really help when we’re all trying to learn Creole. The class is also set at a really inconvenient time for everyone but the Professor. Unless this is your culture or you’re dying to learn the language, don’t take this class with Professor Chery.
This is certainly a Beginner course. Sometimes the pace can be quite slow. On the other hand, the workload is very manageable and you do slowly but surely make progress. If you are keen on learning one of the major Caribbean languages, then you should definitely make the most of the provision that the ALP offers.
