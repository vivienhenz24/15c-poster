This is among the best classes I've taken at Harvard!!! Professor Wang is brilliant, thoughtful, and incisive; he makes classroom discussions lively and enriching in a way few professors are capable of doing so. You'll learn a ton from the readings and the class sessions, which are structured around discussing them. There is no busywork. Everything you do in this class is directly conducive to learning about comparative political development. Could not recommend this class enough!
Really great gov class. Professor Wang really facilitates discussion and the readings are so diverse. Learned a lot and had fun.
TAKE THIS CLASS. You will literally learn so much but at the same time have way less stress on your back. Wang is such an excellent, compassionate, cool professor, arguably my favorite this semester. He facilitates amazing discussion, the workload isn't too much, and people around me were really enthusiastic about the subject matter. That being said, if you don't like to talk, don't take this class, because a significant portion of the grade is participation. Honestly though, I'd say I learned the most content with the least amount of stress in this class. It's a no brainer to take it if you enjoy discussion –– you learn a lot, you only meet once a week, you rarely if ever stress about the class, you have one presentation, and you have a really epic professor. TAKE IT!
I would highly recommend this class. I had no background in government (or the social sciences) at all, but saw the incredible q–reports from last year's class and decided to give it a go. I am so glad that I did. This class was the perfect introduction into political theory. Professor Wang hand–picked some of the most interesting and popular readings from political science and organized them chronologically from the very earliest forms of government to modern–day politics. The readings consistently played off of each other and led to great class discussions. I learned so much and now feel confident in my ability to think and talk about international political development, and read more papers in political science. Professor Wang is ridiculously knowledgeable and such a joy to take a class from. Plus, the work load is very manageable and makes the class feel accessible to people of all backgrounds.
This is a great class!!! Manageable work and you learn SO SO SO much from the incredibly teacher and the assignments and class flow. Basically the class teaches you all of the crazy ways of analyzing and determining political outcomes with different variables across space and time. It will help you better understand politics and history.
