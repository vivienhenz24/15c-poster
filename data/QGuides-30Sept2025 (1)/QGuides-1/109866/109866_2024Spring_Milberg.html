Take this class if you're looking for an easy and insightful class in your schedule. Prof Milberg is great and everyone in the class is just here to learn and ask questions
Very insightful and chill class! The professor has tons of knowledge and excitment for the field – truly a sweetheart. One negative thing is that there was little student discussion, commentary, or active involvement. It was mostly being lectured at, unless student presentations were happening. Nonetheless, good class. We got to play with the brain, give presentations on some very cool cases, and talk to world–renowened experts in the field.
The brain dissection is an awesome hands–on way to learn about the brain / different measures of cognition. The second half of the semester is largely taught by the students, each week a new pair presents a case study to the class. Besides the case study, there is not much work outside of class, the material is manageable, and Dr. Milberg is very passionate!
I think this course needs to lessen its content and offer more room for discussion. Professor Milberg is always rushing through the course and it would be nice to slow down and have more time for student questions. The content is very interesting but needs to be reduced given the weekly nature of the course.
I LOVED this course and Professor Milberg is truly so inspirational; he speaks about the subject with such passion, and I've had an incredible semester :)
Fabulous class! Prof Milberg is amazing, the material is super interesting, the other students were super cool. Just overall fun and different from the other classes I've taken here.
Dr. Milberg is fantastic. If youre looking for a chill MBB seminar that gives you room to explore cognitive neuroscience in a relaxed environment, take this course. You will learn to think critically and not worry about your grades
TAKE THIS COURSE. You wont regret it! Prof. Milberg is incredible, and its evident that he cares a lot for students and their wellbeing. I learned a lot from the case presentations and brain dissection. Low workload, but high payoff!
