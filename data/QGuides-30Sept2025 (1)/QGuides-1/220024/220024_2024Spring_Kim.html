This is an amazing amazing class! I strongly recommend it to anyone who enjoys novels, reading, literature, creative writing, or just wants to dive into some dark and weird books! Professor Kim is an awesome lecturer and I really learned a ton from her insights into the novels we read for the class. The course is also self–graded, so I didn't feel a ton of pressure at all despite the large amount of reading. It definitely takes a lot of time to read the novels assigned for this course (about one a week) but it's well worth it and made for an unforgettable course for me. If you're at all interested, definitely give it a try.
I would strongly recommend this course. It was by far my favorite class Ive taken at Harvard and the best discussions Ive had in a class. I grew as a writer and reader and am far more confident in my abilities. It pushed me out of my comfort zone as a stem concentrator but in a perfectly supportive environment.
Be ready for a quirky, unconventional, and fascinating set of readings. I thought lectures were meticulously structured and thought–provoking. The Professor is highly attentive to her students and cares a lot about your individual goals and progress. I was so glad to finally find an intimate GenEd (both in size and quality of attention given to students), which made the class feel more like a "real" class than a large, impersonal survey. Sections were super funny given the bizarre nature of the readings. Every facet of this course was awesome and I would take it again. I LOVED THIS COURSE!!!!
Take this class!!
It is so worth it to do all of the reading for this class –– you won't regret it!
