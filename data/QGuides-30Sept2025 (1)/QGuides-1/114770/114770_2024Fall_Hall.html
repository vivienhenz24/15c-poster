This class was extremely interesting, especially if you are interested in Europe and/or politics and/or history. The assignments are not necessarily easy though (even though there are no exams). You have to think and write on a higher–level, so this class is definitely not a gem. But it is still very interesting and the lectures/sections are stimulating.
If you're interested in both history and government, I would highly recommend this course! You will learn a lot about European history and how their governments were shaped. Prof. Hall is an incredible lecturer, and he has a lot of enthusiasm for the content. Stay on top of the readings and you'll do great!
Take it, yes. Readings can be dense sometimes, you actually have to skim through them and not chat got when you're writing an essay because this course requires detailed references. Overall, Prof Hall is an amazing professor and a person, and I have no regrets of taking this class
TAKE THIS CLASS. Professor Hall is amazing and his lectures are actually engaging. I was surprised someone could make a topic like feudalism sound so interesting, but he does. You'll learn a lot and become a better writer.
This is NOT a gem. It is difficult to get an A in this class unless you are willing to commit many additional hours into the readings and essays. The lectures are very interesting and Peter Hall is a great lecturer, but the class is somewhat difficult. It is essentially a history class, so Gov concentrators should be willing to undertake that when they enroll.
Take this course! Professor Hall is amazing and is so committed to getting to know and talk to students. Sung In was a kind and attentive TF, who is also intensely knowledgeable. I learned so much from the course and it is such a balanced workload. All around, fantastic and a great survey of European Political History. No particular notes for improvement!
Class lectures are great. Professor Hall is a good lecturer and has genuine passion for the subject. However, the grading is very arbitrary and you never know how you will end up doing. Meetings with the TF are good, but even after you make edits, you will still get docked points which is a bit frustrating.
