Personally I really enjoyed this class because both the topics covered this semester were ones that I already had very strong interests in. Learned a lot about sign language and language reclamation, and definitely was one of my favorite classes in the semester because of that. If you're not interested in the topics though, might be a bit of a struggle, but I think these are also topics that everyone should have some form of baseline knowledge in. You could examine your own personal interest in linguistics within the scope of what the tutorials are for your semester and still learn a lot! Overall a course I really enjoyed.
