I absolutely loved this course! Learning about a new language is one thing, but this course immerses students in deaf culture through having Deaf faculty members and requiring students to go to various community events (my class had to have 10 community hours for the semester but this was not a burden at all). It is such a privilege to have a Deaf person teach the class, and introduce us to the nuances and insights of Deaf culture and bring us into Deaf spaces. I feel like I've learned so much on so many fronts and I am excited to continue on! Dr. C, and the rest of the teaching staff have been so influential and created such a great community that I am considering getting an ASL language citation!! If you're unsure about what language to take for your requirement TAKE ASL!!!
