Comments
Take it take it take it! There's a lot of reading, but it's worth the time and effort. I leave lecture almost every week and tell my friends not in the class something fascinating we talked about, and people of every academic and religious persuasion have expressed envy. My only warning is that if you aren't really careful about it, you'll end up seriously contemplating a concentration or secondary in Religion.
Take this course if you're interested in the New Testament. Period. Full stop.
If you have the space for this course and even a passing interest in the New Testament, I highly recommend taking this course. Unlike some other "introduction" courses I've taken at Harvard, this is an actual introduction to the New Testament. The readings are fascinating and Professor Dunning is an excellent lecturer. Trust me, you'll have a great time.
