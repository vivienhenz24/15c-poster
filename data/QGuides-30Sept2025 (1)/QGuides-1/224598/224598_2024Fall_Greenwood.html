This seminar is perfect for anyone hoping to read some more contemporary fiction, which a lot of syllabi in the humanities departments here lack. Also, I'd highly recommend any seminar with Professor Greenwood she's stunning. This class was amazing.
I would recommend Professor Greenwood's class, whatever it may be, instead of just any one course. I had a wonderful experience in this class on adaptations but it is really the professor and not the subject matter that makes the difference between a "that was alright" class and "why did this have to end" class! Professor Greenwood brings together a really really extensive repository of knowledge and sensitive engagement with the study of the Classics in its myriad of forms. She also provides great feedback that encourages you without flattering you, allowing you to feel inspired to explore your individual interest while providing astute, actionable feedback.
This class was very fun! A great selection of material and wonderful lectures to frame the discussions. I really enjoyed the way the class continuously returned to the same few questions regarding what adaptation and its role are. Professor Greenwood is an incredible teacher and she cares a lot.
This course is incredible if you have an interest in both the classics and contemporary literature. The reading list is very broad and filled with gems. In–class discussions were fantasticProfessor Greenwood is great at facilitating.
