This is by far the best course i've taken at Harvard. I usually never write evaluation's for courses but this course was so incredible that I had to write this response, so here we go. The lectures and readings are intellectually stimulating and Professor Frank Johnson is an incredible orator and teacher. For how difficult some of the subjects are that this course addresses( Holocaust, and antisemitism), Professor Frank Johnson does an amazing job unpacking these topics in detail while also illustrating larger higher level social patterns within Germany. The classes assignments are rewarding and build upon information from section and lecture, but are not assignments just to give the students busy work. Rather they are assignments that are shorter but have the students thinking critically about themes in this class. Any course with Professor Frank Johnson I would recommend, however this course is so amazing that its worth taking.
