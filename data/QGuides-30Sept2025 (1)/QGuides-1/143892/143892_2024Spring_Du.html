The teaching team for CHNSE123XB is excellent, just like for CHNSE BX. They will do their best to accommodate you, and deliver engaging lessons. This course is a notch up from BX, both in terms of content and pace. Vocabulary and grammar become more complex (as should be expected). I came out of this course with significantly improved Chinese ability, and able to express my thoughts in spoken Chinese much better than before.
This is a class about consistency. If you are consistent, keep up with the homework assignments, read the text, and study the vocab, you will get an A. If you are not a person who is okay with consistency and don't want a class everyday, don't take it.

Your Chinese will improve. A lot!
Great class – super well–run and definitely challenging without being unmanageable! As someone with a pretty weak heritage background, I felt like the Bx/123xb series has been the perfect pace to push me to improve my Chinese and understanding of Chinese culture while still being an engaging class and a very reasonable amount of work (~5–7 hours/week outside of class, I probably spent 3 hours on the weekly homework, 1.5 hours for studying new vocab/the textbook, and time on some other projects scattered in between). Towards the end of the class it was harder to keep up with all the new vocab, but for the most part I really enjoyed the course. The teachers are all super nice and very dedicated to making sure everyone has a positive learning experience. There are a lot of opportunities to improve your grade so there's never really that much stress about doing well on one particular assignment. This class makes the daily trek to Vanserg worth it!
Take this class if you genuinely have interest for Chinese. It was more work than Chinese bx for me and most of my peers, I spent about 3 hours on each homework assignment, not including the hour or so of review beforehand. I would also spend about 30–60mins the night before each quiz learning the new vocab. If you're willing to put in the work, the content is really interesting and you'll learn how to engage in chinese conversation above just basic small talk.
