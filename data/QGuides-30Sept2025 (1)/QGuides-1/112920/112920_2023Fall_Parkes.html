It is a language class! You will learn the language as long as you do the work. Don't let yourself get caught in a trap of procrastinating the homework assignments even though they are small; having done them before class time is essential to have familiarity and learn faster.
German 20A is a solid class and very similar structure to german 10A/B. There are a couple films and writings you analyze which really engaged the class.
Since the class is entirely section, its quality depends almost completely on your TF. In my case, my TF was amazing and fostered a great atmosphere where everyone felt comfortable speaking German and making mistakes. I feel more competent in German after taking this course!
Take German 20A if you want to improve your speaking and grammar skills! I loved the class so much. Even though I already fulfilled my foreign language requirement with another language, I continued taking German because it was so much fun. The teachers are super educated and qualified. And, the department invests a lot of time and effort into creating a community for German language speakers and learners. One thing I will say is that how fun your specific section is, depends entirely on your preceptor's personality.
Frau Chen is very nice and so the class is not too stressful. German 20 is definitely a step up from German 10 and so expect to put in more time. It is worth it though, as you'll see that you can understand German at roughly the B1 level.
