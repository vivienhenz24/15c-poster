What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I highly recommend this course! As difficult as it might prove, it is very rewarding. The work pays off.
If you are interested in Arabic, and you took AA or have equivalent experience, I definitely recommend continuing to AB. It is definitely a lot of consistent work but I think very worthwhile, and Ustaaz Ozzy in particular is fantastic. He is very kind and considerate and so knowledgeable, and I recommend, at the very least, taking a different class with him if you are able.
If you want to actually start to understand Arabic both on a technical level and on a practical level then I highly recommend this course. That said, you get out of tis course what you put into it. Do the homework, pay attention in class, and actually participate and you will be absolutely fine.
Don't get discouraged if you didn't do well in Arabic AA – this class will fill in your gaps of knowledge and help you grow.
