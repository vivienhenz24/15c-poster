What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The class was amazing. Professor Katz teaches with enthusiasm and has developed a very interesting course, especially for those interested in labor markets. The course interestingly covers good breadth and depth. I thoroughly enjoyed being part of this.
This course provides an excellent overview of theoretical and empirical developments in the field of labor economics. The coursework is largely driven by independent reading and research rather than problem sets. I would encourage students to start thinking of research ideas early on in the semester.
– take the research paper seriously
– be on top of noting key takeaways from lectures
If you're interested in labor in any sort of way, this will probably be the most helpful class you can take.
