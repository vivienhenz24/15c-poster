I'll admit that this tutorial was the class I was most worried about at the beginning of the semester, but most of my worries didn't come to fruition, and I'm so glad that I decided to still take it. Sol is a super understanding instructor, and she makes every effort to make the course manageable, including allowing us to self–designate the weeks we're writing essays or leading discussion. Managing time to write your essays on time can be difficult, but the weekly readings are very easy to understand, and discussion tends to be rather informal. The weekly speakers are also all amazing and bring exciting points of discussion to each session!
Sol is the best, and learning about some interesting topics and theory without the stress of a large tutorial and grades is really nice
This class is great. Professor Cusicanqui Marsano really allows for a supportive learning environment where varying opinions are taken into account. The essays are quite short and not too difficult.
This course provides a very good introduction to theory; writing about and discussing it in a small group setting helped me to appreciate it more. Sol is a wonderful instructor and is very supportive, helping us to think critically about the material and encouraging our interests. The guest speakers were also very helpful in illuminating possible paths within the field.
