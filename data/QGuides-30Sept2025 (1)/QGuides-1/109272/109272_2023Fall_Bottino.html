I really enjoyed this class and Paul!! He's great. I loved how flexible it was. It gave me a lot of time to work on my own startup.
If you have a startup you want to take seriously this semester, take this course. It's very very light on the courseload side –– basically only one small assignment the whole year + a weekly Slack post –– and you will get accountability and feedback as you build your startup.
RAD is a great place to pursue your startup. I highly recommending having a startup with some traction already and not going in without one, as you'll get way more out of the course (and you'll likely feel out of place if you aren't working super hard on your startup).
This class is really awesome and you will learn so much as you work on your startup. I feel like this was one of the most impactful classes I've taken at Harvard.
A great class to work on your startup and discuss with prof and peers.
I met some of my best friends at Harvard through this course, and I don't think that was a coincidence. If you are an entrepreneur, the best thing you can do by being at Harvard is to meet other people on campus building cool ventures, and this class is one of the best ways to do that. I really appreciated the time it gave me to work on my startup and cherish the connections I have made with others. Being in the Slack is something that continues beyond the class and is valuable on its own, too.
