This course was my favorite of my semester. As a philosophy concentrator, this course felt like just the right amount of challenge and difficulty for me to grow both in my reading and writing. Sometimes the demand in readings was difficult to meet but attending section and reading my peer's discussion posts helped me fill in some of the gaps. I appreciated the course materials and how diverse they were despite this being a class on Proust. The secondary readings did not take over the focus of the course but offered significant ways to make sense of some of the more difficult themes in the volumes. Section was also a wonderful way to dig deeper into neglected passages and it allowed students to guide the discussion. It was an honor to take this course with Professor Moran and the TF, Luke.
