TAKE THE CLASS! The instructors cultivate a wonderful environment and the papers, despite being old, are actually pretty intresting
Class material focuses on reading seminal papers in the field of gene–expression. This both provides great educational material to ground commonly known biological processes (their discovery and methods used) but also teaches you how to read paper efficiently – a skill that seems be going away in the current age. Even though the course focused on analyzing impactful papers, the key takeaway for me were discussions in the class. The professors were great at moderating, explaining, framing and questioning topics. The clarity in communication provided by the instructors teaches you how to express your own ideas precisely.
Great class with great professors, highly recommend
Take this class! It's a lot of work, but well worth it if you're going to do any biological science even tangentially related to genetics (so all of it).
From the very beginning this course sets out to foster a critical evaluation of scientific literature, we're not just learning about genetics but also thinking about ways the field has progressed and could further do so. I highly recommend this course to all students doing biological research seeking to broaden their understanding of various topics in genetics.
I would tell them to be ready to read many papers, and if they have a strong interest in genetics they will enjoy them.
I would highly recommend and say that what you get out of it is the time and focus that you're willing to put into it.
